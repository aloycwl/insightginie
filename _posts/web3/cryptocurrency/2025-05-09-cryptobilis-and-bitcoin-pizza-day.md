CryptoBilis, a leading crypto commerce and community brand in Southeast Asia, marks its role in fostering mass adoption through hardware wallet sales, swag, and vibrant events. As part of this mission, CryptoBilis has embraced Bitcoin Pizza Day—celebrated globally every May 22 to honor the first real‐world Bitcoin transaction in 2010—to host Southeast Asia's largest Pizza Day gatherings, featuring giveaways, hardware wallet demos, and networking opportunities. This article explores CryptoBilis's origins and offerings, the history and significance of Bitcoin Pizza Day, CryptoBilis's event activations, and the broader impact on regional crypto adoption.
