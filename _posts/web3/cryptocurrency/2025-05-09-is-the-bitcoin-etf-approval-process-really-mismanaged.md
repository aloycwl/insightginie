The journey to U.S. spot Bitcoin ETF approval has been marred by protracted delays, abrupt reversals, and opaque decision-making that even SEC insiders have labeled “terribly mismanaged.” Critics—from Commissioner Hester Peirce to industry stakeholders—point to shifting deadlines, unclear guidance, and occasional “fake news” mishaps as evidence of a process that has alienated innovators and shaken investor confidence. This article explores the historical timeline, key criticisms, market impacts, and what lies ahead in the evolving landscape of cryptocurrency ETFs.
