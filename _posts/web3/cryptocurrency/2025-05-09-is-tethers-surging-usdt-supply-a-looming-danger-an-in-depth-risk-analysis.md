Tether's USDT circulating supply has just reached **149.73 billion tokens**, translating to a market capitalization of **$149.73 billion**. As the world's largest stablecoin by volume, USDT underpins a vast share of cryptocurrency trading and DeFi liquidity. However, rapid supply expansion—up from $100 billion in circulation just over a year ago—has reignited concerns about **reserve transparency**, **systemic contagion**, and **regulatory oversight**. This article examines the origins of these risks, their potential market impacts, and the evolving policy landscape aimed at safeguarding financial stability.
