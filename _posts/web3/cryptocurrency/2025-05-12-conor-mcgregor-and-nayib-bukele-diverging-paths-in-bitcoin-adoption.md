### Nayib Bukele's Bitcoin Policy Reversal in El Salvador

Contrastingly, President Nayib Bukele of El Salvador, once a pioneer in adopting Bitcoin as legal tender in 2021, has recently scaled back the country's cryptocurrency initiatives. Facing economic challenges and under pressure from the International Monetary Fund, El Salvador removed Bitcoin's status as legal tender in early 2025 .[The Times](https://www.thetimes.co.uk/article/i-went-on-holiday-to-el-salvador-and-spent-only-bitcoin-35t898fk5?utm_source=chatgpt.com)[El País](https://elpais.com/america/2025-01-30/bukele-da-marcha-atras-y-retira-al-bitcoin-la-condicion-de-moneda-legal-en-el-salvador.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
