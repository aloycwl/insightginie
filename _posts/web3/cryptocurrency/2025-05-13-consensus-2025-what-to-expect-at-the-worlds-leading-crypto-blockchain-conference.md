What Is Consensus?
------------------

### H2: The Premier Event for the Crypto Economy

Since its launch in 2015, **Consensus** has grown into the **most influential blockchain and crypto gathering in the world**, attracting tens of thousands of attendees annually. The event brings together stakeholders from across the ecosystem:

* Blockchain developers and startup founders
* Institutional investors and hedge funds
* Public company executives
* Crypto-native protocols and DAOs
* Regulators, policymakers, and journalists

### H3: Hosted by CoinDesk

As a leading crypto media outlet, **CoinDesk** curates the event to strike a balance between **thought leadership, regulation, development, and market trends**. The agenda reflects the most pressing issues in the Web3 world—and often sets the tone for the year ahead.

What's New in Consensus 2025?
-----------------------------

### H2: Key Themes and Trends to Watch

Each year, Consensus evolves to reflect the current state of the industry. For 2025, several high-impact themes are expected to dominate the stage.

#### H3: Institutional Integration & Real-World Assets (RWA)

With giants like BlackRock, Fidelity, and Goldman Sachs now active in the space, Consensus 2025 will highlight how **real-world asset tokenization** is transforming traditional finance. Expect panels and demos showcasing:

* On-chain real estate
* Tokenized bonds and treasuries
* Digital identity systems for compliance

#### H3: Crypto Regulation in a Post-ETF World

Following the approval of Bitcoin and Ethereum spot ETFs in major economies, 2025 sees **regulators and builders in closer dialogue**. Consensus 2025 will feature top voices from the SEC, CFTC, European regulators, and Asian policy think tanks.

#### H3: Layer 2 Scaling and Cross-Chain Interoperability

The scalability wars are not over. Ethereum rollups, Solana's performance upgrades, and cross-chain protocols like Cosmos and Polkadot will feature prominently, along with live demos and dev workshops.

#### H3: The AI x Blockchain Convergence

For the first time, expect **dedicated sessions exploring the intersection of AI and blockchain**. Topics include:

* Autonomous agents in DeFi
* AI-governed DAOs
* On-chain model training and verification
* Privacy-preserving computation
