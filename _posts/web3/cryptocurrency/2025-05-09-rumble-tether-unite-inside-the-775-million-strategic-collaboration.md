In December 2024, Tether, the issuer of the world's largest USD-backed stablecoin (USDT), agreed to a **$775 million** strategic investment in Rumble, the video-sharing and cloud-services platform. Under the deal, Tether purchased **103.3 million Class A shares** at $7.50 each—injecting $250 million into growth initiatives and funding a self-tender offer for up to 70 million shares—all while preserving Rumble CEO Chris Pavlovski's controlling stake. Beyond capital, the partnership envisions deeper **crypto-payments integrations**, **cloud solutions**, and the co-development of **Rumble Wallet**, a new on- and off-ramp for creators and users. This alliance underscores both firms' commitment to **decentralization**, **free expression**, and **financial inclusion**, promising to reshape creator economies and media infrastructure.
