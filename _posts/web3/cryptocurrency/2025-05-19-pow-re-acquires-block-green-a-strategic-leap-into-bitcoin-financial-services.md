<p>In a significant move poised to reshape the Bitcoin mining and financial services landscape, Canadian sustainable Bitcoin miner POW.RE has announced its intent to acquire Swiss-based Bitcoin liquidity protocol, Block Green AG. This strategic acquisition aims to merge POW.RE&#8217;s robust mining operations with Block Green&#8217;s innovative financial products, creating a comprehensive platform that caters to the evolving needs of miners and investors alike.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Acquisition Details</h2>

<p>POW.RE plans to acquire 100% of Block Green&#8217;s shares through an equity swap, supplemented by additional payments in Bitcoin (BTC) and U.S. dollars. The transaction is expected to be finalized within 90 days, pending customary regulatory approvals. This merger combines POW.RE&#8217;s sustainable mining infrastructure with Block Green&#8217;s expertise in Bitcoin-native financial services, including revenue-streaming products, lending, and hedging solutions for miners and data center operators. </p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Strategic Synergies and Market Impact</h2>

<p>The integration of POW.RE and Block Green is anticipated to generate multiple synergies:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Enhanced Market Access:</strong> Leveraging Block Green&#8217;s established relationships and regulatory licenses, including a Swiss financial intermediary license and pending EU MiCA license applications, to expand POW.RE&#8217;s reach in the European market.</li>

<li><strong>Diversified Revenue Streams:</strong> Combining mining operations with financial services to create new revenue opportunities and offer miners advanced financial tools powered by hashrate technology.</li>

<li><strong>Strengthened Regulatory Position:</strong> The merger positions the combined entity favorably within regulatory frameworks, enhancing credibility and compliance in the rapidly evolving cryptocurrency landscape.</li>
</ul>

<p>As part of the agreement, POW.RE will commit a significant baseload of hashpower to Block Green&#8217;s marketplace for a minimum of 24 months, ensuring sustained growth and stability for the platform.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Leadership and Governance</h2>

<p>Post-acquisition, Block Green&#8217;s CEO, Sebastien Hess, will join POW.RE&#8217;s board of directors, bringing his entrepreneurial and financial technology expertise to the company&#8217;s governance. This leadership integration is expected to drive innovation and strategic continuity, aligning with the combined entity&#8217;s vision for the future of Bitcoin mining and financial services. </p>
