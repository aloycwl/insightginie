As Bitcoin (BTC) climbs to new local highs and dominates crypto headlines, seasoned investors know what might follow: **Altseason**—a period when alternative cryptocurrencies (altcoins) outperform Bitcoin in both price and percentage gains.

While Bitcoin's strength often sets the foundation for market-wide confidence, it's the altcoins that tend to **deliver exponential returns** during these phases. If past cycles are any indication, the signs flashing on-chain, in charts, and across sentiment tools suggest we're on the cusp of another **explosive Altseason**.
