Beginning May 16, 2025, Steak 'N Shake will accept Bitcoin (BTC) payments at all U.S. locations—making it one of the first major fast-food chains to roll out nationwide crypto payments beyond pilot programs. This initiative follows months of social-media teases and industry chatter, including an X poll that garnered input from Bitcoin luminaries like Jack Dorsey and Luke Dashjr. By integrating crypto payments, Steak 'N Shake aims to evaluate Bitcoin's transaction speed, cost, and customer adoption in a high-volume, low-margin environment, potentially setting a precedent for broader restaurant-sector innovation.
