When BlackRock, the world's largest asset manager, steps into crypto, the world listens. But beyond headlines, it's the **mechanics** of their approach that signal a seismic shift. Their recent implementation of **in-kind redemptions for its Bitcoin ETF (IBIT)** may seem like a technical detail — yet it's one of the most important developments in institutional crypto adoption to date.

This feature has profound implications for market structure, tax efficiency, custody models, and the evolving relationship between TradFi and DeFi. Here's why it matters — and what it could mean for the future of finance.
