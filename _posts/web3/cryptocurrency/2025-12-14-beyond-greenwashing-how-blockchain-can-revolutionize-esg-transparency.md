Beyond Greenwashing: How Blockchain Can Revolutionize ESG Transparency
======================================================================

In an era where corporate responsibility is under an ever-increasing microscope, Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) factors have moved from niche concerns to mainstream imperatives. Investors, consumers, and regulators alike are demanding more than just profits; they want proof of ethical practices, environmental stewardship, and robust governance. Yet, despite this surging demand, a significant challenge persists: the pervasive issue of *greenwashing* and a general lack of verifiable, transparent data in ESG reporting. Is there a technological solution that can cut through the ambiguity and establish a new benchmark for corporate accountability? Many believe blockchain technology holds the key.
