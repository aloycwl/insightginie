<p>In the ever-evolving landscape of blockchain technology, Nervos stands out as a pioneering network designed to address the inherent challenges of scalability, interoperability, and long-term sustainability. Unlike traditional monolithic blockchains that attempt to handle all functionalities within a single layer, Nervos introduces a modular architecture that separates the base layer from the application layers, allowing for greater flexibility and efficiency.</p>

<p>At the heart of the Nervos network lies the Common Knowledge Base (CKB), the Layer 1 blockchain that serves as the foundation for the entire ecosystem. CKB is built upon a novel generalized UTXO model and employs a RISC-V instruction set-based virtual machine for executing transactions and smart contracts. This design ensures that CKB supports all current and prospective cryptographic primitives, making it possible to build diverse Layer 2 networks on top of it .</p>

<p>One of the standout features of Nervos is its commitment to security and decentralization. By leveraging the battle-tested Proof-of-Work consensus algorithm, CKB ensures exceptional security under all conditions. This approach makes the network resistant to capture by adversarial actors, maintaining the integrity and trustworthiness of the system .</p>

<p>The modular nature of Nervos allows for unparalleled scalability. While the base layer focuses on security and decentralization, the upper layers, such as Layer 2 solutions, offer virtually unlimited scalability and programmability. This separation enables developers to build decentralized applications (dApps) that can scale efficiently without compromising on security or decentralization .</p>

<p>Interoperability is another cornerstone of the Nervos network. CKB is designed to seamlessly interoperate with all heterogeneous blockchains and can anchor various types of sidechains, state channels, and Layer 2 networks. This capability ensures that Nervos can integrate with existing blockchain ecosystems, fostering a more connected and collaborative blockchain landscape .</p>

<p>The native cryptocurrency of the Nervos network is CKByte (CKB), which serves multiple purposes within the ecosystem. Holding CKB allows users to store data on the base layer, with one CKB permitting the storage of one byte of data. Additionally, CKB is used for paying transaction fees, ensuring that the network remains secure and functional .</p>

<p>Nervos also introduces a unique cryptoeconomic model that aligns the interests of all participants and stakeholders. This model ensures that miners are compensated for providing security to the network in perpetuity, creating a sustainable and incentive-driven ecosystem .</p>

<p>For developers, Nervos offers a robust platform to build and deploy dApps. With protocol-level account abstraction, developers can create applications that work seamlessly across blockchains, enhancing the user experience and broadening the potential user base .</p>

<p>In conclusion, Nervos represents a forward-thinking approach to blockchain design, emphasizing modularity, scalability, interoperability, and security. By addressing the limitations of traditional blockchain architectures, Nervos provides a platform that is not only capable of supporting current decentralized applications but is also adaptable to future advancements in blockchain technology. As the blockchain ecosystem continues to grow and evolve, Nervos stands poised to play a pivotal role in shaping the future of decentralized systems.</p>
