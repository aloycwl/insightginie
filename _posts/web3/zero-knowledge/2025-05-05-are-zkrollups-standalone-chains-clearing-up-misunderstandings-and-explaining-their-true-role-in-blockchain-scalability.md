zkRollups are an exciting development in the world of blockchain technology, offering the promise of increased scalability, efficiency, and reduced costs. However, there is a common misconception that zkRollups operate as standalone chains, separate from the Ethereum mainnet or other Layer 1 blockchains. In reality, zkRollups function as Layer 2 solutions that rely on the security and decentralization of the main chain while offering scalability benefits through transaction aggregation and zero-knowledge proofs.

In this guide, we'll explore why zkRollups are not standalone chains, clarify the role they play in the blockchain ecosystem, and address common misunderstandings about their structure and functionality.
