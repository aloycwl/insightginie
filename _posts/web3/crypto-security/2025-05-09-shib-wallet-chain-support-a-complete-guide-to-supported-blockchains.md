Shib Wallet is the Shiba Inu ecosystem's official multichain smart wallet, designed to aggregate and manage your SHIB, BONE, LEASH, and $TREAT tokens across a wide range of blockchains. Beyond Ethereum and Polygon, Shib Wallet extends native support to Shibarium (the Shiba Inu Layer-2), as well as major EVM-compatible networks such as BNB Smart Chain, Avalanche, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Fantom, and more. It even reaches non-EVM chains like Solana and Blast, consolidating 14 chains in one intuitive interface. This breadth of support makes Shib Wallet a true one-stop hub for portfolio tracking, DeFi interaction, and dApp access across the Shiba Inu network and beyond.

---

Official Supported Chains
-------------------------

1. **Ethereum Mainnet**  
   Shib Wallet fully supports all ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum, including SHIB, BONE, LEASH, and $TREAT, with real-time balance updates and DeFi integrations.
2. **Polygon (Matic)**  
   Enjoy low-fee transactions and fast confirmations on Polygon, where Shib Wallet tracks MATIC-based holdings seamlessly.
3. **Shibarium (Layer-2)**  
   As the Shiba Inu ecosystem's proprietary Layer-2, Shibarium is natively integrated—enabling direct access to ShibaSwap, BONE staking, and ultra-cheap, gas-free transfers.
4. **BNB Smart Chain (BSC)**  
   Leverage BSC's low-cost network for BEP-20 versions of SHIB tokens; Shib Wallet automatically recognizes and manages assets on BSC.
5. **Avalanche**  
   Shib Wallet connects you to Avalanche's C-Chain, letting you hold and swap SHIB-pegged assets at sub-second speeds.
6. **Arbitrum One**  
   Access Arbitrum's scaling solution with Arbitrum-based SHIB bridges and dApp integrations directly within Shib Wallet.
7. **Optimism**  
   Fast, OP-based transactions are supported—view and manage your assets on Optimism Layer-2 without leaving the wallet.
8. **Base**  
   Benefit from Coinbase's Layer-2 ecosystem; Shib Wallet recognizes Base-deployed SHIB tokens and delivers portfolio insights.
9. **Fantom**  
   Tap into Fantom's Opera network for instant, low-fee swaps of SHIB and related tokens.
10. **Solana**  
    Although non-EVM, Shib Wallet extends support to Solana-wrapped SHIB, enabling cross-chain asset tracking.
11. **Blast**  
    Shib Wallet integrates with high-throughput networks like Blast for next-gen DeFi experiences.
12. **Gnosis (xDAI)**  
    Manage SHIB on the Gnosis chain (formerly xDAI) for stable, low-cost transfers.
13. **Harmony**  
    Harmony's fast-finality chain is included, letting users handle SHIB-pegged assets on its network.
14. **KuCoin Community Chain (KCC)**  
    Shib Wallet's KCC support provides another low-fee environment for SHIB holders.
15. **Cronos**  
    Cronos' EVM-compatible chain is seamlessly supported for SHIB and community token management.
16. **Oasis**  
    For privacy-focused transfers, Shib Wallet includes Oasis network support for SHIB assets.

---

Why Multichain Support Matters
------------------------------

* **Unified Dashboard:** Track and manage tokens—regardless of blockchain—in one place, eliminating wallet-hopping.
* **Cost Efficiency:** Switch to low-fee chains (e.g., Polygon, BSC, Shibarium) on-the-fly to optimize gas costs.
* **DeFi Access:** Directly interact with dApps across ecosystems—Staking on Shibarium, swapping on Arbitrum, providing liquidity on Avalanche—all within Shib Wallet.
* **Future-Proofing:** As new chains gain traction, Shib Wallet's architecture makes adding support straightforward, ensuring long-term usability.

---

How to Add and Use a New Chain in Shib Wallet
---------------------------------------------

1. **Open Settings → Chains** in Shib Wallet's sidebar.
2. **Select** your desired network (e.g., Arbitrum, Avalanche).
3. **Confirm** network parameters—no manual RPC entry needed for supported chains.
4. **Switch** between chains via the network dropdown to view corresponding token balances.
