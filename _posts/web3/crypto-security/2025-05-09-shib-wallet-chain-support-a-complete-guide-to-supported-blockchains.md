<p>Shib Wallet is the Shiba Inu ecosystem’s official multichain smart wallet, designed to aggregate and manage your SHIB, BONE, LEASH, and $TREAT tokens across a wide range of blockchains. Beyond Ethereum and Polygon, Shib Wallet extends native support to Shibarium (the Shiba Inu Layer-2), as well as major EVM-compatible networks such as BNB Smart Chain, Avalanche, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Fantom, and more. It even reaches non-EVM chains like Solana and Blast, consolidating 14 chains in one intuitive interface. This breadth of support makes Shib Wallet a true one-stop hub for portfolio tracking, DeFi interaction, and dApp access across the Shiba Inu network and beyond.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Official Supported Chains</h2>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Ethereum Mainnet</strong><br>Shib Wallet fully supports all ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum, including SHIB, BONE, LEASH, and $TREAT, with real-time balance updates and DeFi integrations.</li>

<li><strong>Polygon (Matic)</strong><br>Enjoy low-fee transactions and fast confirmations on Polygon, where Shib Wallet tracks MATIC-based holdings seamlessly.</li>

<li><strong>Shibarium (Layer-2)</strong><br>As the Shiba Inu ecosystem’s proprietary Layer-2, Shibarium is natively integrated—enabling direct access to ShibaSwap, BONE staking, and ultra-cheap, gas-free transfers.</li>

<li><strong>BNB Smart Chain (BSC)</strong><br>Leverage BSC’s low-cost network for BEP-20 versions of SHIB tokens; Shib Wallet automatically recognizes and manages assets on BSC.</li>

<li><strong>Avalanche</strong><br>Shib Wallet connects you to Avalanche’s C-Chain, letting you hold and swap SHIB-pegged assets at sub-second speeds.</li>

<li><strong>Arbitrum One</strong><br>Access Arbitrum’s scaling solution with Arbitrum-based SHIB bridges and dApp integrations directly within Shib Wallet.</li>

<li><strong>Optimism</strong><br>Fast, OP-based transactions are supported—view and manage your assets on Optimism Layer-2 without leaving the wallet.</li>

<li><strong>Base</strong><br>Benefit from Coinbase’s Layer-2 ecosystem; Shib Wallet recognizes Base-deployed SHIB tokens and delivers portfolio insights.</li>

<li><strong>Fantom</strong><br>Tap into Fantom’s Opera network for instant, low-fee swaps of SHIB and related tokens.</li>

<li><strong>Solana</strong><br>Although non-EVM, Shib Wallet extends support to Solana-wrapped SHIB, enabling cross-chain asset tracking.</li>

<li><strong>Blast</strong><br>Shib Wallet integrates with high-throughput networks like Blast for next-gen DeFi experiences.</li>

<li><strong>Gnosis (xDAI)</strong><br>Manage SHIB on the Gnosis chain (formerly xDAI) for stable, low-cost transfers.</li>

<li><strong>Harmony</strong><br>Harmony’s fast-finality chain is included, letting users handle SHIB-pegged assets on its network.</li>

<li><strong>KuCoin Community Chain (KCC)</strong><br>Shib Wallet’s KCC support provides another low-fee environment for SHIB holders.</li>

<li><strong>Cronos</strong><br>Cronos’ EVM-compatible chain is seamlessly supported for SHIB and community token management.</li>

<li><strong>Oasis</strong><br>For privacy-focused transfers, Shib Wallet includes Oasis network support for SHIB assets.</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Why Multichain Support Matters</h2>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Unified Dashboard:</strong> Track and manage tokens—regardless of blockchain—in one place, eliminating wallet-hopping.</li>

<li><strong>Cost Efficiency:</strong> Switch to low-fee chains (e.g., Polygon, BSC, Shibarium) on-the-fly to optimize gas costs.</li>

<li><strong>DeFi Access:</strong> Directly interact with dApps across ecosystems—Staking on Shibarium, swapping on Arbitrum, providing liquidity on Avalanche—all within Shib Wallet.</li>

<li><strong>Future-Proofing:</strong> As new chains gain traction, Shib Wallet’s architecture makes adding support straightforward, ensuring long-term usability.</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">How to Add and Use a New Chain in Shib Wallet</h2>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Open Settings → Chains</strong> in Shib Wallet’s sidebar.</li>

<li><strong>Select</strong> your desired network (e.g., Arbitrum, Avalanche).</li>

<li><strong>Confirm</strong> network parameters—no manual RPC entry needed for supported chains.</li>

<li><strong>Switch</strong> between chains via the network dropdown to view corresponding token balances.</li>
</ol>
