As of May 9, 2025, Unichain commands an unprecedented 75 percent of all transaction volume on Uniswap v4, eclipsing Ethereum's share of just under 20 percent. This seismic shift reflects Unichain's combination of ultra-low fees—95 percent cheaper than Ethereum—one-second block times (with sub-second on the roadmap), and a $45 million liquidity incentive program that has driven massive user migration. Unichain's design, tailored for Uniswap v4's Hooks-enabled, single-contract architecture, delivers superior gas efficiency and custom on-chain logic capabilities, reshaping traders' and builders' preferences in decentralized finance. This article explores the technical underpinnings behind Unichain's dominance, analyzes its catalysts, and discusses the broader implications for the DeFi ecosystem.
