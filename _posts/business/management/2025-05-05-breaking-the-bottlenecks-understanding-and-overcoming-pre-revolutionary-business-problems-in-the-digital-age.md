Every major business revolution—whether powered by the internet, mobile technology, blockchain, or AI—emerges to solve **deeply entrenched inefficiencies** in existing systems. These inefficiencies, often referred to as **pre-revolutionary problems**, create the perfect storm for disruption.

These problems include **information overload**, **non-scalable gatekeepers**, **high market fragmentation**, **extreme information asymmetries**, **heavy regulation**, **high failure costs**, and **resource intensity**. When these conditions are present, they signal **massive opportunity** for startups, disruptors, and forward-thinking investors to step in and **reshape the playing field**.

This guide explores the **key traits of pre-revolutionary markets**, offers real-world examples, and outlines strategies entrepreneurs can use to recognize and capitalize on these transformation points.
