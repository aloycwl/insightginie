As businesses scale and evolve, they face increasing complexity in their operations. One critical decision that entrepreneurs and investors must make is how to structure the organization to optimize efficiency, foster innovation, and drive growth. One popular approach to structuring a business is organizing it by **product, function, process, customer, and territory**. This strategy allows businesses to streamline operations, better serve their customers, and create a clear path for growth.

In this guide, we'll explore the key characteristics of these five methods of structuring a business, highlight real-world examples, and provide actionable insights on how to implement each structure effectively for small businesses, startups, and growing enterprises.
