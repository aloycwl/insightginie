In a fast-paced business world where quality, consistency, and efficiency are vital, organizations need frameworks that help them streamline processes and minimize risks. One of the most widely recognized models for this is **CMMI (Capability Maturity Model Integration)**—a comprehensive approach to **process improvement and organizational maturity**.

Understanding the **structure of CMMI** is essential for successful implementation and long-term improvement. In this article, we explore the **core components of the CMMI model**, including maturity and capability levels, process areas, goals, common features, and specific practices.
