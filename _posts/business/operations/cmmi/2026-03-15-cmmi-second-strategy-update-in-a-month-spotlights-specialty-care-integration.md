CMMI: Second Strategy Update in a Month Spotlights Specialty Care Integration
=============================================================================

In a rapid succession of policy shifts that signals a pivotal transformation in American healthcare delivery, the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Innovation (CMMI) has released its second major strategy update within a single month. This latest installment places an unprecedented focus on specialty care integration, aiming to bridge the long-standing divide between primary care management and the often-fragmented specialty medical landscape. As healthcare stakeholders digest these updates, it is becoming increasingly clear that the federal government is doubling down on value-based care models that incentivize coordination across the entire patient journey.
