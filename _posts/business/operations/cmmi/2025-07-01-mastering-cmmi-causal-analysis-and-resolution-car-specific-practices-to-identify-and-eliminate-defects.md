In today's competitive business environment, maintaining high-quality products and processes is essential. The **Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI)** framework addresses this need by emphasizing continuous process improvement and defect prevention. One crucial component within CMMI is **Causal Analysis and Resolution (CAR)**, which helps organizations identify the root causes of defects and implement effective corrective actions.

This article explores the **specific practices associated with CAR**, focusing on how to systematically determine the causes of defects, address those causes, and evaluate the impact of improvements. Whether you're new to CMMI or looking to deepen your understanding, mastering CAR practices is key to sustaining quality and achieving long-term success.
