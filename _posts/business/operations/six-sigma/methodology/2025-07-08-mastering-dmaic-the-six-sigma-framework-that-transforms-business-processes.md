In today's fiercely competitive business environment, companies are under constant pressure to improve performance, reduce waste, and meet customer expectations consistently. **Six Sigma**, a data-driven methodology for eliminating defects and enhancing process quality, has become a gold standard across industries. At the heart of Six Sigma lies the **DMAIC framework**—a structured, five-phase approach used to improve existing business processes.

This article explores each phase of DMAIC in detail—**Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, and Control**—equipping you with the knowledge to apply it effectively and drive measurable improvements in your organization.
