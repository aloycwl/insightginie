In today's **Lean Agile software development**, delivering high-quality software efficiently is a top priority. One approach that significantly enhances both **quality and process efficiency** is **Acceptance Criteria-Driven Development (ACDD)**.

ACDD combines the principles of **Test-Driven Development (TDD)** with clear **acceptance criteria**, ensuring that all functional requirements are covered by automated tests before development begins. This approach is especially valuable for **Six Sigma Agile projects**, where reducing defects, improving process efficiency, and maximizing customer value are key objectives.
