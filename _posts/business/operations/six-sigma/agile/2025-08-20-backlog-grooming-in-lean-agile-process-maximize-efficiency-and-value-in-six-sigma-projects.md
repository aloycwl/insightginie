In the world of **Lean Agile software development**, managing priorities and maintaining a clear workflow is critical for **maximizing value and reducing waste**. One of the most essential practices for achieving this is **backlog grooming**—also called backlog refinement.

Backlog grooming ensures that a team's work items are **well-defined, prioritized, and actionable**, keeping the development process lean and aligned with business and customer goals. When implemented effectively, backlog grooming contributes to smoother **interim releases (IRs)**, faster sprints, and better overall project outcomes.
