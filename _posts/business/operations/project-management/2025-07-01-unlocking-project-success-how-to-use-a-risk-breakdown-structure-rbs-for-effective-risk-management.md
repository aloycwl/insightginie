Risk management is a cornerstone of successful project delivery. One of the most powerful tools in a project manager's toolkit is the **Risk Breakdown Structure (RBS)** — a hierarchical framework that organizes potential risks into categories, making them easier to identify, analyze, and mitigate.

In this article, we'll explore what an RBS is, why it matters, and how you can use it to better manage both internal and external project risks.
