Effective risk management is essential for ensuring project success. A well-crafted **Risk Management Plan** defines how risks will be identified, analyzed, managed, and controlled throughout the project lifecycle. Without a clear plan, projects risk delays, cost overruns, and quality issues that could have been anticipated and mitigated.

This article provides a comprehensive overview of how to develop a robust Risk Management Plan — including methodology, roles, budgeting, risk categories, risk scoring, and documentation — along with insights on the critical **Risk Register** tool that tracks and drives risk management activities.
