What if you could walk through a digital building blueprint, practice surgery without touching a patient, or play games that immerse you in another universe? Welcome to the world of **Extended Reality (XR)**—a fast-evolving ecosystem that merges the physical and digital worlds through **Augmented Reality (AR)**, **Virtual Reality (VR)**, and **Mixed Reality (MR)**.

XR is no longer science fiction. It's becoming a central component of **education, entertainment, business, healthcare, and the metaverse**. In this comprehensive guide, we'll break down what XR is, its core components, use cases across industries, and why this immersive technology is poised to become the next digital revolution.
