In the world of technical analysis, chart patterns provide vital clues about potential market movements. Among these, the **Double Top** is a time-tested formation used to identify bearish reversals. But not all double tops are created equal. One advanced variation—the **Overbalanced Double Top**—offers even stronger confirmation of trend exhaustion and a higher likelihood of reversal.

This refined pattern combines the classic double top with the **principle of price and time overbalance**, giving traders deeper insights into the strength of trend changes. Understanding and mastering the overbalanced double top can improve trade entries, risk management, and overall profitability.
