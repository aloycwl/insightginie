<h1>How Pattern by Tracee Ellis Ross Is Bringing Your Favorite Girlfriends Back Together</h1>
<p>When Tracee Ellis Ross launched her fashion line Pattern, she did more than introduce another celebrity clothing brand. She created a movement that celebrates the power of female friendship, the joy of dressing together, and the confidence that comes from wearing clothes that truly fit every body. In this article we explore how Pattern is reuniting girlfriends everywhere, why the collection feels like a warm hug from your closest pals, and how you can style its pieces for every occasion—from lazy brunches to glamorous nights out.</p>
<h2>The Story Behind Pattern</h2>
<p>Tracee Ellis Ross has long been known for her bold red‑carpet looks, her infectious laughter on television, and her advocacy for inclusivity in beauty and fashion. After years of hearing from fans who struggled to find trendy pieces that fit their curves, she decided to design a line that would put comfort, versatility, and celebration front and center. Pattern debuted in 2021 with a simple promise: to create clothing that makes women feel seen, heard, and fabulous—no matter their size, shape, or style preference.</p>
<h3>From Actress to Entrepreneur</h3>
<p>Transitioning from acting to fashion design is never easy, but Ross approached the challenge with the same authenticity she brings to her roles. She spent months fitting prototypes on a diverse group of women, listening to feedback about waistbands, sleeve lengths, and fabric feel. The result is a collection that feels less like a celebrity endorsement and more like a collaborative wardrobe built by friends, for friends.</p>
<h2>Why Pattern Respects Female Friendship</h2>
<p>At its core, Pattern is built around the idea that style is best enjoyed in good company. The brand’s marketing campaigns often feature groups of women laughing, sharing clothes, and encouraging each other to try bold colors or daring silhouettes. This emphasis on camaraderie is reflected in three key design philosophies:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Inclusive sizing:</strong> Pattern offers extended sizes ranging from XS to 3X, ensuring that every girlfriend can find her perfect fit without needing to hunt through multiple stores.</li>
<li><strong>Versatile pieces:</strong> Many items are designed to be mixed and matched—think a relaxed‑fit blazer that works over a tee for brunch or paired with a silk camisole for a dinner date.</li>
<li><strong>Bold, confidence‑boosting colors:</strong> From vibrant emerald greens to deep plum purples, the palette encourages groups to coordinate or contrast, turning a simple outing into a mini‑fashion show.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Styling Tips for a Girlfriend Gathering</h2>
<p>Whether you’re planning a casual weekend brunch or a night out on the town, Pattern has the building blocks for a coordinated yet individual look. Below are two detailed outfit guides that show how to take advantage of the line’s versatility while letting each friend shine.</p>
<h3>Casual Brunch Look</h3>
<p>Start with Pattern’s soft‑cotton, relaxed‑fit tee in a muted sage green. Pair it with the brand’s high‑waisted, wide‑leg trousers in a complementary oatmeal shade. Add a lightweight denim jacket from the line for those breezy patio mornings. Complete the ensemble with white leather sneakers and a shared straw tote bag where each friend can stash her essentials. To personalize the look, invite each girlfriend to swap accessories—one might opt for a patterned silk scarf, another for layered gold necklaces, and a third for a pair of hoop earrings.</p>
<h3>Night‑Out Glam</h3>
<p>For an evening affair, reach for Pattern’s sequined slip dress in a deep midnight blue. The dress’s adjustable straps and subtle side slit make it comfortable for dancing while still feeling luxe. Layer a cropped, velvet blazer in a contrasting burgundy for added warmth and texture. Finish with strappy heeled sandals in a metallic nude and a clutch that matches the blazer’s hue. Encourage each friend to experiment with makeup—perhaps a bold red lip for one, a smoky eye for another, and a glowing highlighter for the third—to create a cohesive yet individual glam squad.</p>

<h2>Comparing Pattern to Other Celebrity Lines</h2>
<p>Many celebrities have launched fashion collections, but few place the same emphasis on inclusivity and friendship as Pattern does. Below is a quick comparison that highlights what sets Pattern apart.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Size Range:</strong> While many celebrity brands stop at L or XL, Pattern’s 3X top size ensures that curvier friends are never left out.</li>
<li><strong>Price Point:</strong> Pattern positions itself in the accessible luxury bracket—most items fall between $48 and $198—making it easy for a group to purchase multiple pieces without breaking the bank.</li>
<li><strong>Design Process:</strong> Ross personally fits every prototype on a diverse panel, a practice rarely seen in celebrity lines that often rely solely on external design teams.</li>
<li><strong>Community Focus:</strong> Pattern’s social media campaigns regularly feature user‑generated content of friend groups, fostering a sense of belonging that goes beyond the product itself.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li><strong>Production:</strong> The brand partners with factories that adhere to fair‑wage standards and prioritize waste reduction.</li>
<li><strong>Packaging:</strong> Orders ship in recycled cardboard boxes with soy‑based ink printing.</li>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
<h3>What sizes does Pattern offer?</h3>
<p>Pattern offers an inclusive size range from XS to 3X, with detailed measurements provided on each product page to help you find the perfect fit.</p>
<h3>Is Pattern true to size?</h3>
<p>Most customers report that Pattern runs true to size, especially when referencing the brand’s size guide. If you fall between sizes, consider sizing up for a relaxed fit or down for a more tailored look.</p>
<h3>Can I find Pattern in physical stores?</h3>
<p>Pattern is primarily sold online through its official website and select retail partners such as Nordstrom and Revolve. Occasionally, pop‑up events appear in major cities; follow the brand’s Instagram for announcements.</p>
<h3>Are the fabrics suitable for sensitive skin?</h3>
<p>Many Pattern pieces use organic cotton and Tencel, which are known for being soft and hypoallergenic. However, always check the specific fabric content if you have known sensitivities.</p>
<h3>How do I care for Pattern garments?</h3>
<p>Most items are machine washable on a gentle cycle with cold water. To preserve colors and elasticity, lay flat to dry or tumble dry low. Refer to the care label on each piece for exact instructions.</p>
<h3>Does Pattern offer plus‑size activewear?</h3>
<p>Yes, the line includes leggings, sports bras, and relaxed‑fit tops designed for movement, all available in the full size range.</p>
<h3>Is there a loyalty program or discount for first‑time buyers?</h3>
<p>Pattern frequently offers a 10 % discount for first‑time email subscribers. Sign up on the website to receive the welcome code.</p>
<h3>Can I return or exchange items if they don’t fit?</h3>
<p>Pattern provides a 30‑day return window for unworn items with original tags. Exchanges are processed as a new order after the return is received.</p>
