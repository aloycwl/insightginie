In technical analysis, understanding various chart patterns can significantly enhance a trader's ability to predict price movements. One such pattern that often signals market volatility and price breakout opportunities is the **Expanding Triangle**. Although less common than traditional chart patterns, the Expanding Triangle can provide valuable insights into market dynamics and potential trend reversals.

The Expanding Triangle pattern is characterized by a series of higher highs and lower lows, forming a widening range of price action. This pattern typically indicates uncertainty in the market, with increasing volatility and indecision among traders. However, when traded correctly, it can lead to profitable breakout opportunities.

In this guide, we'll explore how to identify the Expanding Triangle pattern, how to trade it effectively, and tips for managing risk to ensure a high probability of success.
