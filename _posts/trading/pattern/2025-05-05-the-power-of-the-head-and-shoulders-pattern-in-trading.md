In the world of technical analysis, the **Head and Shoulders pattern** is one of the most widely recognized and reliable chart formations used by traders. It signals potential trend reversals and offers a clear entry and exit strategy for those looking to profit from price movements in various markets—whether in forex, stocks, or commodities.

In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the **Head and Shoulders pattern**, how to identify it, and how to use it to inform your trading decisions. Whether you're a seasoned trader or a beginner, understanding this classic chart pattern can significantly enhance your market predictions.
