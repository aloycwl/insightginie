In the world of technical analysis, the **double bottom pattern** is one of the most recognized bullish reversal setups. While the traditional version is widely known, more advanced traders often look for nuanced variations that offer early signals and better trade positioning. One such variation is the **Double Bottom (Running)** pattern—a faster, momentum-driven version of the classic double bottom that indicates **strong institutional buying pressure** and **minimal price hesitation**.

This guide explores the **Double Bottom (Running)** pattern in detail: how to identify it, when to trade it, and why it can provide early entry opportunities during high-volatility market environments.
