In technical analysis, the **Double Top** is one of the most reliable chart patterns used to **identify potential trend reversals** in financial markets. Whether you're trading stocks, forex, or crypto, recognizing this pattern can help you spot when an uptrend is losing momentum—and when it might be time to sell or short.

This guide will walk you through the essentials of how to trade the traditional double top pattern, including **how to spot it, confirm it, enter and exit trades**, and manage risk effectively. With practical insights and visual cues, this article is designed for traders who want to sharpen their edge and make better-informed decisions in volatile markets.
