In technical analysis, chart patterns are used to predict potential price movements based on historical price behavior. One such pattern that traders rely on is the **Cup and Handle** formation, which is considered a strong bullish continuation pattern. This pattern signals that an asset, whether it's a stock, forex pair, or cryptocurrency, is likely to experience upward price momentum after a period of consolidation.

The **Cup and Handle** pattern is characterized by a rounded “cup” shape followed by a consolidation phase that resembles the handle of a cup. When the price breaks above the handle's resistance, it signals a strong buying opportunity, often leading to a significant upward trend.

In this guide, we'll walk you through the key elements of the Cup and Handle pattern, how to trade it effectively, and tips for maximizing your trading success.
