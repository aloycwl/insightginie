<p>The Drive, Chip and Putt National Finals is the championship event of a nationwide junior golf competition for boys and girls aged 7-15. It is held annually at Augusta National Golf Club, featuring the top qualifiers from regional and sub-regional competitions competing in driving, chipping, and putting skills challenges.</p>
<h3>Who won the 2026 Drive, Chip and Putt National Finals?</h3>
<p>The 2026 champions were crowned in four age divisions: Boys 10-11, Girls 10-11, Boys 12-13, and Girls 12-13. <p>Players must progress through a three-tier qualification process. They start at local sub-regional qualifiers, advance to regional finals, and the top scorers from each region earn an invitation to the National Finals at Augusta National Golf Club.</p>
