Between fluctuating ticker prices, breaking news, and the opinions of market pundits, it is easy for a retail investor to become overwhelmed. However, at its core, investing is not about predicting the next short-term price movement; it is about owning a piece of a business. To do this effectively, investors rely on a technique known as **fundamental analysis**.
