AI vs. Quant Trading: Unpacking the Real Differences & Future of Finance
========================================================================

In the dynamic world of finance, terms like Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Quantitative Trading (Quant Trading) are often thrown around, sometimes interchangeably, sometimes as distinct revolutionary forces. While both leverage advanced technology and data to inform investment decisions, they represent fundamentally different approaches with unique methodologies, strengths, and applications. Understanding these distinctions isn't just an academic exercise; it's crucial for investors, traders, and anyone looking to navigate the increasingly tech-driven financial markets.

This article will demystify the relationship between AI and Quant Trading, dissecting their core definitions, exploring their unique characteristics, highlighting their overlaps, and ultimately, peering into a future where their synergy promises to reshape the financial landscape.
