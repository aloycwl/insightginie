C&W Launches Quantitative Insights Group to Leverage AI Tech
============================================================

The commercial real estate industry is undergoing a seismic shift, moving away from traditional gut-feeling decision-making toward a model driven by data, precision, and velocity. In a major move signaling the future of the sector, global real estate services firm Cushman & Wakefield has officially announced the launch of its Quantitative Insights Group. This new initiative is designed to harness the power of artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, and advanced predictive analytics to provide clients with a distinct competitive advantage in an increasingly complex global market.
