The **Ichimoku Cloud**, or *Ichimoku Kinko Hyo*, is a comprehensive indicator that provides insight into **trend direction, momentum, and potential support/resistance zones**—all in a single glance. While the system may appear complex at first, it becomes a powerful tool when you understand how to interpret its **cloud formations (Kumo)** and the relative position of price lines.

This article focuses on a specific and simplified strategy using Ichimoku to determine **weak and strong buy/sell signals**, based on whether the **cloud and key lines (like price)** are above or below each other.
