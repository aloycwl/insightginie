Global Entry vs. TSA PreCheck: Which Prescreen Will Get You Through Airport Security the Fastest?
=================================================================================================

For frequent travelers, the experience of navigating airport security is often the most stressful part of the journey. The long lines, the frantic removal of shoes, the requirement to pull laptops out of bags—it all adds up to a taxing experience. Thankfully, the U.S. Department of Homeland Security has introduced Trusted Traveler Programs (TTPs) designed to expedite the process for pre-vetted passengers. The two most prominent programs are TSA PreCheck and Global Entry. But for the average traveler, understanding the nuances between these two can be confusing. Which one do you actually need? Which one is faster? In this guide, we break down everything you need to know to decide which program will get you through airport security with the least amount of friction.
