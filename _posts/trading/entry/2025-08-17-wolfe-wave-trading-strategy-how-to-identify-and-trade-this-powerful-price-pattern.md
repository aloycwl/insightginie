### Introduction

In technical analysis, traders rely on chart patterns to predict price movements and find profitable trade setups. Among the less commonly understood yet highly effective patterns is the **Wolfe Wave**. When spotted and traded correctly, Wolfe Waves can provide high-probability entries and precise price targets. This guide will walk you through what Wolfe Waves are, how to identify them, and proven strategies to trade them effectively.
