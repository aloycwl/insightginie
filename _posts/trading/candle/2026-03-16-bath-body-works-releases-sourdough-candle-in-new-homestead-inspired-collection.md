Bath & Body Works Releases Sourdough Candle in New Homestead-Inspired Collection
================================================================================

The home fragrance world is no stranger to eccentric scents, but Bath & Body Works has truly pushed the boundaries of olfactory innovation with its latest release. As part of their brand-new 'Homestead' collection, the retail giant has officially launched a candle that has everyone talking: the Sourdough scent. Yes, you read that correctly—you can now make your entire living room smell like a bustling artisanal bakery, complete with the tang of fermentation and the warmth of a freshly baked crust.
