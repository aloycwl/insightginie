<h3>What is CYBERSEC 2023?</h3>
<p>CYBERSEC 2023 is an annual international cybersecurity conference organized by the International Cybersecurity Association, featuring keynote speeches, technical workshops, panel discussions, and an exhibition hall focused on the latest trends and solutions in information security.</p>
<h3>Where and when is CYBERSEC 2023 taking place?</h3>
<p>The conference is being held at the Metropolitan Convention Center from October 10‑12, 2023. The grand opening ceremony took place on the morning of October 10.</p>
<h3>Who should attend CYBERSEC 2023?</h3>
<p>The event is ideal for security analysts, engineers, managers, CISOs, CIOs, government officials, academic researchers, and anyone interested in strengthening cybersecurity posture and staying current on industry developments.</p>
