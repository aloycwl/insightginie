In a world grappling with mounting waste and an insatiable demand for energy, a powerful solution is emerging from the very refuse we discard: **Waste-to-Energy (WTE) systems**. These innovative technologies transform non-recyclable waste into usable heat, electricity, or fuel, offering a dual benefit of waste management and power generation. But as with any large-scale industrial process, the question of its environmental balance is paramount. Are WTE systems truly a green solution, or do they pose hidden environmental challenges? Let's delve into the intricate relationship between waste, energy, and our planet.
