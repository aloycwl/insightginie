Is Duke Energy's $6 Billion ATM Equity Plan Reshaping the Investment Case for Utilities?
========================================================================================

In the world of regulated utilities, capital expenditure (CapEx) is the lifeblood of growth. For industry titan Duke Energy, the scale of current infrastructure requirements has reached a turning point that recently prompted a bold financial maneuver: the announcement of an At-the-Market (ATM) equity issuance program valued at up to $6 billion. As investors digest this news, many are left asking whether this move is a standard operational adjustment or a fundamental shift in how utility giants are funding the energy transition.
