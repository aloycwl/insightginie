<p>Had an amazing time at <strong>AWS Summit 2025</strong> at Marina Bay Sands on 29 May! It was a high-energy day packed with <strong>learning, innovation, and networking</strong>.</p>

<p>AWS once again showcased why they’re the leader in <strong>cloud computing</strong> and <strong>AI as a Service</strong>, offering seamless solutions that connect <strong>developers, vendors</strong>, and <strong>end users</strong>. From infrastructure to innovation, there’s almost nothing you <em>can’t</em> build on AWS — and do it with ease.</p>

<p>The event was full of surprises — including a giant slide that took you from the 5th to the 4th floor in seconds (yes, with style!). Despite a slight delay at registration, the experience was incredibly well-organized and immersive.</p>

<p>While most of the content focused on AI and real-world use cases, Web3 was still underrepresented. I’ll be sharing some of the highlights soon, including:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Vendor showcases and how they’re using AWS in practical ways</li>

<li>Key AWS services discussed</li>

<li>Insights into adoption strategies across industries</li>
</ul>

<p>If you’re a startup or builder, don’t forget to check out <strong><a class="" href="https://aws.amazon.com/activate/">AWS Activate</a></strong> — a great way to get <strong>free AWS credits</strong> to kickstart your journey.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Bonus Insight from the Asia Blockchain Association Side Event</strong></h2>

<p>Exciting discussions around <strong>High-Performance Computing (HPC)</strong> and its role in the future of Web3!</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Projects like <strong>Golem Chain</strong> are pioneering decentralized HPC, creating alternatives to traditional cloud infrastructures.</li>

<li><strong>HPC is poised to enhance quantum-proofing</strong> of blockchain systems — a critical step toward future-proof Web3 security.</li>

<li>By <strong>2030</strong>, Web3 apps may no longer rely on centralized cloud providers. Instead, they’ll thrive in <strong>decentralized HPC marketplaces</strong>, offering better speed, privacy, and cost-efficiency.</li>

<li>Web3 + HPC = The next evolution in decentralized computing.</li>
</ul>
