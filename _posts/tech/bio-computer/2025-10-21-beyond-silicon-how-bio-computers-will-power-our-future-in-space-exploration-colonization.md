Silicon-based computers, while powerful, grapple with the harsh realities of cosmic radiation, extreme temperatures, and the sheer vastness of distance. But what if our computational power could evolve, adapt, and even heal itself, much like life itself? Enter the revolutionary world of **bio-computers** – a paradigm shift poised to unlock the next frontier in space exploration and human colonization.
