Federated learning promises to reshape artificial intelligence by enabling collaborative model training without raw data ever leaving its source. Yet, as organizations rush to adopt this decentralized approach, a critical bottleneck emerges: how to preserve privacy while maintaining computational efficiency. Enter **PIM (Processing-In-Memory)** for federated learning—a paradigm shift that addresses these challenges at the hardware level. This isn't just another incremental improvement; it's a fundamental rethinking of how AI systems handle sensitive data in distributed environments.
