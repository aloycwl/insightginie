Financial systems thrive on trust, precision, and security. Every second, trillions of dollars move across global markets, protected by encryption and monitored by high-speed trading systems. But the dawn of **quantum computing** introduces a powerful new variable—quantum algorithms capable of disrupting everything from stock markets to international banking.

While quantum breakthroughs promise faster analytics and optimized trading strategies, they also open the door to **destabilizing financial manipulation** on a scale never seen before. The same technology that could unlock economic growth might also shatter confidence in global markets.
