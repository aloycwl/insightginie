Quantum computing has long been hailed as the next great leap in technology. With the potential to solve complex problems beyond the reach of classical machines, quantum breakthroughs promise medical advances, new materials, and global efficiencies. Yet, hidden beneath this promise lies a darker reality: the potential weaponization of quantum power.

From **state-level cyberwarfare** to the **erosion of privacy** through mass decryption, quantum technologies could destabilize financial systems, empower espionage, and trigger new geopolitical rivalries. As the world enters the dawn of the quantum era, the risks demand as much attention as the opportunities.
