Quantum technologies are rapidly redefining the boundaries of computation, communication, and sensing. Nations worldwide are engaged in an intense **race for quantum supremacy**, aiming to achieve computational power far beyond classical systems. This race is not only about national prestige but also about shaping the future of industries, cybersecurity, and scientific research. Central to this global competition are **collaborative innovation hubs**—centers where academia, industry, and government converge to accelerate breakthroughs.
