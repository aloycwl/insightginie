Brisbane is setting its sights on the global stage with one of the most ambitious projects in the history of computing: the construction of a **utility-scale quantum computer with 1 million qubits**. This groundbreaking initiative not only positions Australia as a major player in the international quantum race but also sets the foundation for solving some of the world's most complex scientific, industrial, and societal challenges.
