<h3>2. What are Ising models in the context of quantum computing?</h3>
<p>Ising models are mathematical models used in physics to describe ferromagnetism. In quantum computing, particularly quantum annealing, they are used to map complex optimization problems (like logistics routing or financial portfolio optimization) onto the quantum processor. Solving the Ising model equates to finding the optimal solution to the problem.</p>
