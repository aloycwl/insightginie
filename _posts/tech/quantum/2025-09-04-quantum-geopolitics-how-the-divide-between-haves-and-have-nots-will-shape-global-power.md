Every major technological revolution reshapes global power. The Industrial Revolution propelled Western dominance. The nuclear age redefined military strategy. Today, quantum computing is poised to create a **new geopolitical divide**—between those who harness its power and those left behind.

This quantum gap between **“haves” and “have-nots”** could fundamentally alter the balance of global dominance, impacting economics, security, and diplomacy in ways both visible and invisible.
