Two titans of technology—**IBM** and **AMD**—have announced a groundbreaking strategic partnership to develop **hybrid quantum-classical supercomputing systems**. This collaboration aims to merge IBM's leadership in quantum computing with AMD's expertise in high-performance computing (HPC) and advanced processors. Together, the companies are setting the stage for a new computing paradigm where quantum and classical architectures complement each other, enabling breakthroughs in AI, materials science, cryptography, and beyond.
