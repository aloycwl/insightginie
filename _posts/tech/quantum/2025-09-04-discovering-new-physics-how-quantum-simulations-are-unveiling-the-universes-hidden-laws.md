The frontiers of physics are expanding rapidly, fueled by technological advances in quantum computing. Traditional experiments and classical simulations can only probe so far into the behaviors of subatomic particles, exotic materials, and complex quantum systems. **Quantum simulations** are now offering scientists an unprecedented tool to explore the unknown, potentially revealing new physical laws that could redefine our understanding of the universe.

This article examines how quantum simulations are transforming fundamental physics research, the applications of these methods, and the profound implications for science and technology.
