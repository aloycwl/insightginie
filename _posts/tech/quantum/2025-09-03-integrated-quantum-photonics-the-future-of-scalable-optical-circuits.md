Quantum technology is transforming how we process, transmit, and secure information. Among the many approaches to building practical quantum systems, **integrated quantum photonics** stands out as a promising solution for scalable optical circuits. By leveraging light as the carrier of quantum information, researchers are developing compact, efficient, and highly scalable devices that may one day underpin a global quantum internet and next-generation quantum computers.
