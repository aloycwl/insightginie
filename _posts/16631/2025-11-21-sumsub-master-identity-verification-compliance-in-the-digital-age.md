The Imperative: Why Identity Verification & Compliance Matter More Than Ever

Cybercriminals are becoming more sophisticated, exploiting vulnerabilities to commit fraud, launder money, and facilitate illicit activities. Businesses need a proactive, adaptive solution to stay ahead of these challenges, and this is where Sumsub truly shines.

Sumsub’s Core Pillars: Features That Build Trust
------------------------------------------------

Sumsub’s platform is built on a foundation of cutting-edge technology and deep expertise in regulatory compliance. Here are some of its key features and benefits:

### Know Your Customer (KYC) & Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

Sumsub streamlines the entire KYC process, ensuring businesses know exactly who they are dealing with. This includes:

* **Document Verification:** Automated checks of passports, ID cards, driver’s licenses, and residency permits from over 220 countries and territories. AI-powered tools detect forged documents and ensure authenticity.
* **Facial Biometrics & Liveness Detection:** Advanced liveness checks confirm that a user is a real person, present at the time of verification, preventing spoofing attempts using photos, videos, or masks.
* **Address Verification:** Verifying user addresses through various methods to comply with regulations.
* **Age Verification:** Essential for industries with age restrictions, ensuring compliance and responsible service provision.

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Checks

Combating financial crime is a critical component of Sumsub’s offering. Its AML solutions include:

* **Sanctions Screening:** Real-time checks against global sanctions lists (OFAC, UN, EU, etc.) to identify high-risk individuals and entities.
* **PEP (Politically Exposed Persons) Screening:** Identifying individuals who hold prominent public functions and are therefore subject to enhanced scrutiny.
* **Adverse Media Screening:** Scanning news articles and public records for any negative information associated with an applicant, indicating potential risks.
* **Ongoing Monitoring:** Continuous checks to ensure that once verified, users remain compliant and do not appear on new watchlists.

### Advanced Fraud Prevention & Liveness Detection

Beyond basic verification, Sumsub employs sophisticated fraud detection mechanisms to protect businesses from a wide array of threats:

* **Cross-Client Fraud Prevention:** Identifying fraudsters attempting to create multiple accounts or use stolen identities across different platforms.
* **Device Fingerprinting:** Analyzing device data to detect suspicious patterns and block fraudulent access attempts.
* **Behavioral Biometrics:** Monitoring user behavior during the verification process to spot anomalies indicative of fraud.
* **Deepfake Detection:** Advanced AI algorithms to identify sophisticated deepfake attacks used to bypass liveness checks.
