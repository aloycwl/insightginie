The Core Technologies Powering Jumio’s Robust Solutions
-------------------------------------------------------

Jumio’s effectiveness stems from its sophisticated blend of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and biometric capabilities. Here’s a closer look at the key technologies that make Jumio a powerhouse in identity verification:

### 1. AI-Powered Document Verification

* **Global Document Coverage:** Jumio’s technology can verify over 3,500 types of ID documents from more than 200 countries and territories. This extensive coverage is crucial for businesses operating on a global scale.
* **Automated Data Extraction:** Using advanced AI and OCR (Optical Character Recognition), Jumio rapidly extracts data from identity documents, minimizing manual data entry and potential human error.
* **Fraud Detection:** The system scrutinizes documents for signs of tampering, forgery, or alteration. It can detect subtle inconsistencies, mismatched fonts, altered images, and other indicators of fraudulent activity that would be missed by the human eye.
* **Security Feature Analysis:** Jumio’s AI is trained to recognize and validate the security features embedded in legitimate documents, such as holograms, watermarks, and microprinting.

### 2. Biometric Facial Recognition & Liveness Detection

* **Face Match:** After verifying the authenticity of the ID document, Jumio’s technology performs a biometric comparison between the photo on the ID and a real-time selfie taken by the user. This ensures that the person presenting the document is indeed its rightful owner.
* **Advanced Liveness Detection:** This is a critical component for preventing sophisticated spoofing attacks. Jumio employs sophisticated algorithms to detect whether the person in the selfie is a live human being and not a photo, video, or 3D mask. This liveness detection is passive, meaning users typically don’t need to perform specific actions (like turning their head), making the process seamless and user-friendly.
* **Deepfake & AI Spoofing Prevention:** With the rise of deepfake technology, Jumio continuously invests in cutting-edge liveness detection that can differentiate between a real person and highly convincing synthetic media.

### 3. Identity Verification Orchestration & Platform Intelligence

* **Customizable Workflows:** Jumio’s platform allows businesses to design tailored identity verification workflows based on their specific risk appetite, regulatory requirements, and user experience goals.
* **Continuous Fraud Monitoring:** Beyond initial onboarding, Jumio offers solutions for ongoing authentication and fraud monitoring, helping businesses detect suspicious activities throughout the customer lifecycle.
* **Data Insights & Analytics:** The platform provides valuable insights into verification rates, fraud attempts, and user behavior, enabling businesses to continuously optimize their processes.
* **API & SDK Integration:** Jumio’s robust APIs and SDKs allow for seamless integration into existing applications and platforms, ensuring a smooth implementation process.
