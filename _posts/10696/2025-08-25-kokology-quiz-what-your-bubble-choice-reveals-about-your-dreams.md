### **Quiz Questions**

In a playground, you are playing with bubbles. What scene do you imagine?

1. Bubble floating high into the sky
2. Hundreds of tiny bubbles blowing out
3. You are blowing an enormous bubble
4. The bubbles are carried behind you

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Bubble floating high into the sky**

* **Answer:** Your dreams are elusive and unattainable — **Reasoning:** Choosing a single bubble soaring reflects lofty aspirations that feel out of reach or difficult to grasp.

**2. Hundreds of tiny bubbles blowing out**

* **Answer:** Your dreams are attainable — **Reasoning:** Many small bubbles represent realistic and achievable goals that you can steadily work toward.

**3. You are blowing an enormous bubble**

* **Answer:** You have a focused and important dream — **Reasoning:** Concentrating on one large bubble shows that you dedicate your energy to one central, meaningful ambition.

**4. The bubbles are carried behind you**

* **Answer:** You have been disappointed and hence you do not dream much — **Reasoning:** Bubbles trailing behind suggest past setbacks have made you cautious or hesitant to pursue new dreams.
