### **Quiz Question**

You’re standing by the ocean, watching a group of whales gliding gracefully through the water.  
Look closer—what do you see the baby whale doing?

1. Baby whale swims behind its mother
2. Baby whale snuggles closely to its mother
3. Father and mother whale swimming with their baby
4. Baby whale swims off alone

Take a moment to choose your answer before reading the interpretation below.

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Baby whale swims behind its mother**

* **Reasoning:** Your mum played a huge part in your life. Her influence shapes many of your choices, and her presence is deeply tied to your growth.

**2. Baby whale snuggles closely to its mother**

* **Reasoning:** You are too dependent on your mother. While love and comfort are important, you may need to learn independence and self-trust.

**3. Father and mother whale swimming with their baby**

* **Reasoning:** You equally appreciate both father and mother. You recognize the value of balance, unity, and shared support within family.

**4. Baby whale swims off alone**

* **Reasoning:** You are rather independent. You prefer to walk your own path, even if it means distancing from parental ties.
