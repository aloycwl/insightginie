### **Quiz Question**

You are a scientist and have discovered a new plant species.  
You plant 100 seeds in a harsh, inhospitable desert.

**Question:** Out of 100 seeds, how many do you think will sprout?

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. 99–100 seeds sprout**

* **Reasoning:** You may have self-confidence issues. You expect perfection and fear failure, which can sometimes overwhelm your judgment.

**2. 81–98 seeds sprout**

* **Reasoning:** You are aware of your abilities and confident. You trust your skills while realistically anticipating minor obstacles.

**3. 61–80 seeds sprout**

* **Reasoning:** You are a cautious optimist. You hope for the best but plan for setbacks and stay prepared for challenges.

**4. 41–60 seeds sprout**

* **Reasoning:** You are still searching. You are exploring your potential and learning how to balance hope and realism.

**5. 21–40 seeds sprout**

* **Reasoning:** You tend to be pessimistic. You may underestimate yourself and expect difficulties more than opportunities.

**6. 1–20 seeds sprout**

* **Reasoning:** You need to work on recognizing your value. Self-doubt may be limiting your sense of achievement.

**7. 0 seeds sprout**

* **Reasoning:** You cannot tolerate mistakes, even minor ones. This perfectionist tendency can hinder your ability to take risks.
