### **Quiz Questions**

If you were an actor playing in a courtroom drama, which character would you see yourself as?

1. **The Lawyer**
2. **The Detective**
3. **The Accused**
4. **The Witness**

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. The Lawyer**

* **Answer:** You are hotheaded and unable to hold your cool.
* **Reasoning:** Just like a lawyer passionately arguing a case, you have a fiery spirit and a tendency to let emotions drive your reactions.

**2. The Detective**

* **Answer:** People will turn to you for help.
* **Reasoning:** The detective role reflects your natural problem-solving skills and reliability, making others trust you in times of need.

**3. The Accused**

* **Answer:** You tend to second-guess and judge yourself.
* **Reasoning:** Taking the place of the accused suggests self-doubt and a habit of internal criticism, even when you’re not at fault.

**4. The Witness**

* **Answer:** You try to get along with everyone.
* **Reasoning:** As a witness, you stay on the sidelines, preferring harmony and avoiding unnecessary conflict with others.
