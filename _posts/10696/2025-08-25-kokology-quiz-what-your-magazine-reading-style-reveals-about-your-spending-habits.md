### **Quiz Questions**

You bought a popular magazine. How do you read it?

1. Do you read from the first page to the last?
2. Do you go straight to the article you’re interested in only?
3. Do you flip randomly and read only articles worthwhile?
4. Do you read the features in the same order as you always do?

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

1. **Answer:** You make your money worth
   * **Reasoning:** Reading the magazine thoroughly shows you value your purchases and like to get the full experience for your money.
2. **Answer:** You burn a hole in your pocket
   * **Reasoning:** Skipping straight to the articles you want reflects impulsive tendencies and a preference for instant gratification in spending.
3. **Answer:** You spend economically
   * **Reasoning:** Flipping randomly and focusing only on worthwhile content shows you are selective and practical with your money.
4. **Answer:** Your spending habit is very strict
   * **Reasoning:** Following the same reading order consistently indicates discipline and a controlled, structured approach to finances.
