### **Quiz Questions**

You are a magician performing a magic show. The curtain is rising.

1. How do you feel before the show begins?
2. Whom do you call to assist you, a person you know?
3. You mismanage your trick, what do you say to your assistant?
4. Back in your dressing room, how do you feel?

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

1. **Answer:** How you feel in an affair
   * **Reasoning:** Your emotions before the show reflect your excitement, anxiety, or guilt in a secretive or risky romantic situation.
2. **Answer:** Someone you think is naive
   * **Reasoning:** The assistant you choose represents a person you perceive as innocent, unsuspecting, or easily influenced in real life.
3. **Answer:** What you will say to your partner if caught cheating
   * **Reasoning:** Your reaction to a mismanaged trick shows how you would defend, excuse, or respond when confronted about mistakes in sensitive situations.
4. **Answer:** How you feel after the whole cheating ordeal
   * **Reasoning:** Your post-show feelings mirror your inner emotions once a risky affair or secret is concluded, whether relief, regret, or satisfaction.
