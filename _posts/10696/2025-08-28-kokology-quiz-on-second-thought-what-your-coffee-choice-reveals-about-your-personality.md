### **Quiz Question**

You ordered a sandwich and a cup of coffee without thinking, but after she leaves you suddenly have the mood for hot chocolate. What would you do?

1. Keep looking through the menu thinking how good the taste of hot cocoa
2. Look around to see if the waitress will come back
3. Get up and find the waitress so you can change the order
4. Give up and just wait for the coffee

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Keep looking through the menu thinking how good the taste of hot cocoa** – You don’t know how to call it quits

* **Reasoning:** Lingering indecision shows a tendency to overthink and difficulty moving on from what’s already chosen.

**2. Look around to see if the waitress will come back** – You are not thrilled about the breakup and worry about what others might think

* **Reasoning:** Waiting for a second chance demonstrates hesitation and concern about external opinions, reflecting cautious or anxious tendencies.

**3. Get up and find the waitress so you can change the order** – You will be shocked in breakup and will try to distract yourself

* **Reasoning:** Immediate action shows you respond actively to surprises or disruptions, using activity as a coping mechanism.

**4. Give up and just wait for the coffee** – You get over breakup easily

* **Reasoning:** Accepting the situation without fuss reflects adaptability and an ability to move on without dwelling.
