### **Quiz Questions**

You are in the lobby of a medical clinic, waiting for your name to be called. A small boy is racing around the waiting area unattended and you warn him to be careful and slow down.

1. How does he react?
2. You saw a sickly person through a door opening, who is this person?
3. A doctor’s aide comes out and calls your name, a very familiar face, who is this?
4. The doctor mutters to himself when looking at your chart, what do you do?

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. How does he react?** – How you react to criticism

* **Reasoning:** Your response to the boy shows whether you are patient, strict, or easily frustrated—mirroring how you handle criticism in real life.

**2. You saw a sickly person through a door opening, who is this person?** – Someone you could never rely on during a crisis

* **Reasoning:** The sickly person represents vulnerability; your identification of them reflects who you subconsciously see as unreliable in stressful situations.

**3. A doctor’s aide comes out and calls your name, a very familiar face, who is this?** – Someone you always look up to or fear

* **Reasoning:** The familiar aide symbolizes authority or admiration; your choice shows who commands respect or instills fear in your life.

**4. The doctor mutters to himself when looking at your chart, what do you do?** – How you handle being bullied or teased

* **Reasoning:** Your reaction to the doctor’s muttering reflects how you cope with judgment, criticism, or subtle intimidation from others.
