### **Quiz Question**

You have a super lucky day and went to a local bakery to buy a jelly doughnut. You open it at home and find the jelly is missing inside. How would you react?

1. Take it back for a new one
2. Eat the doughnut still
3. Eat something else
4. Fill it with maple syrup

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Take it back for a new one**

* **Answer:** You played a leadership role among your friends.
* **Reasoning:** Standing up for what you expect mirrors your natural tendency to lead and set standards in your circle.

**2. Eat the doughnut still**

* **Answer:** You accept your fate and don’t make any fuss.
* **Reasoning:** Willingness to accept things as they are reflects your easygoing nature and adaptability in social groups.

**3. Eat something else**

* **Answer:** You make quick decisions among your friends.
* **Reasoning:** Moving on fast shows decisiveness and a practical mindset that others rely on when choices need to be made.

**4. Fill it with maple syrup**

* **Answer:** You are innovative and people turn to you when things happen.
* **Reasoning:** Finding a creative fix reveals your resourceful nature and the ability to provide unique solutions for your peers.
