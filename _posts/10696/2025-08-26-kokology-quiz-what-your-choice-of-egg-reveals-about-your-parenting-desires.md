### **Quiz Questions**

Imagine cracking open an egg on a table in front of you. The egg can be of any shape, size, color, or species.

1. What kind of egg do you choose?
   * Snake
   * Turtle
   * Dinosaur
   * Chicken

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Snake**

* **Answer:** You want your children to have wisdom and wealth
* **Reasoning:** Choosing a snake egg reflects a desire for your children to be clever, resourceful, and capable of navigating life with intelligence and success.

**2. Turtle**

* **Answer:** You want your children to have health and longevity
* **Reasoning:** The turtle symbolizes longevity and steadiness, showing your focus on ensuring your children grow up healthy and live long lives.

**3. Dinosaur**

* **Answer:** You want your children to be unique
* **Reasoning:** A dinosaur egg indicates a wish for your children to stand out, be strong, and leave a distinctive mark in the world.

**4. Chicken**

* **Answer:** You want your children to be normal
* **Reasoning:** Choosing a chicken egg reflects a desire for a simple, happy, and stable life for your children without extraordinary expectations.
