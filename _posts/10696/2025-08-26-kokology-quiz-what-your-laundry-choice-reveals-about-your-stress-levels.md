### **Quiz Questions**

You do all your laundry by hand. Looking outside, the sky is filled with dark, threatening clouds. What goes through your mind?

1. “You’ve got to be kidding, what am I going to wear?”
2. “Wait awhile to see if the weather will clear.”
3. “Not going to do laundry today.”
4. “Do laundry regardless.”

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. “You’ve got to be kidding, what am I going to wear?”**

* **Answer:** Stress level 80 – easily annoyed
* **Reasoning:** This choice shows a tendency to get frustrated quickly when unexpected challenges appear, reflecting high stress and low patience.

**2. “Wait awhile to see if the weather will clear.”**

* **Answer:** Stress level 50 – you keep balance of your stress level
* **Reasoning:** Waiting indicates measured patience and the ability to maintain emotional balance, showing moderate stress handling.

**3. “Not going to do laundry today.”**

* **Answer:** Stress level 0 – easy going
* **Reasoning:** Opting out reflects a relaxed, go-with-the-flow attitude and the ability to let minor obstacles roll off your back.

**4. “Do laundry regardless.”**

* **Answer:** Stress level 100 – you try to achieve the impossible
* **Reasoning:** Persisting despite obstacles indicates extreme determination but also a tendency to take on too much stress.
