### **Quiz Question**

If you could join the best orchestra, which instrument would you play?

1. Violin
2. String bass
3. Trumpet
4. Flute

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

1. **Answer:** You are good in fingering your partner
   * **Reasoning:** Choosing the violin reflects dexterity, precision, and skill. It indicates you are attentive to details and excel at tasks requiring finesse and coordination.
2. **Answer:** You are good in the back
   * **Reasoning:** Picking the string bass shows stability and support. You prefer foundational roles, providing reliability and strength, often working quietly but effectively behind the scenes.
3. **Answer:** You are good with mouth work
   * **Reasoning:** Selecting the trumpet represents expressive energy and communication. You are confident, outspoken, and excel in situations requiring projection or influence.
4. **Answer:** Most complicated style
   * **Reasoning:** Choosing the flute indicates versatility and complexity. You enjoy mastering intricate challenges and are capable of balancing multiple skills simultaneously.
