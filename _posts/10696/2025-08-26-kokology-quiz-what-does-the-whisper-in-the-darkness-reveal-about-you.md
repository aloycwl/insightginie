### **Quiz Questions**

You are lost in a deep cavern when a bat flies up and whispers something in your ear. What does it say?

1. **“I know where the exit is”**
2. **“Let me show you the way out”**
3. **“Keep looking”**
4. **“You’re never, ever going to get out of here”**

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. “I know where the exit is”**

* **Answer:** You are a busybody and a know-it-all.
* **Reasoning:** Always quick to assert what you know, you may unintentionally come across as someone who meddles in other people’s affairs.

**2. “Let me show you the way out”**

* **Answer:** You have a big heart and are selfless.
* **Reasoning:** This response reflects compassion and a natural tendency to help others, even if it means putting them before yourself.

**3. “Keep looking”**

* **Answer:** You encourage people to learn themselves.
* **Reasoning:** Instead of providing direct answers, you prefer motivating others to grow through their own experiences.

**4. “You’re never, ever going to get out of here”**

* **Answer:** You jeer and kick at people who are already down.
* **Reasoning:** This choice points to a more cynical or harsh side, where you may criticize or discourage those who are struggling.
