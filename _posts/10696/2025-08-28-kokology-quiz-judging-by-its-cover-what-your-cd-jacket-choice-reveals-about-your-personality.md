### **Quiz Questions**

You’re a new artist about to release your first CD. What type of jacket design would you pick?

1. A **soothing scene** using calming imagery.
2. A **fun, cartoonish design** or playful image.
3. An **abstract pattern** that makes people think.
4. A **picture of yourself** on the cover.

Pick your cover. Now, let’s uncover what it says about your personality.

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Soothing Scene**

* **Answer:** You are a caring and gentle person but have a wall around you.
* **Reasoning:** The serene imagery reflects your compassionate nature, while your choice of scenery instead of yourself shows emotional protection.

**2. Fun Cartoonish Design**

* **Answer:** You are sociable and talkative but unreliable.
* **Reasoning:** Your playful cover mirrors your outgoing personality, yet the whimsical style hints at a lack of consistency or follow-through.

**3. Abstract Pattern**

* **Answer:** You like to express your talent.
* **Reasoning:** Abstract designs signal creativity and a desire to showcase your unique abilities and thoughts to the world.

**4. Picture of Yourself**

* **Answer:** You are straightforward and honest.
* **Reasoning:** Placing yourself on the cover reflects transparency and confidence—you show the world exactly who you are.
