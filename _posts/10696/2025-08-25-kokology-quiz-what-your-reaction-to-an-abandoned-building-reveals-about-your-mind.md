### **Quiz Question**

You are entering an old abandoned building.

1. How many steps do you go down a staircase to the basement?
2. You hear a sound—what is it? Weeping? Speaking?
3. What is your reaction to that sound?
4. You hear someone calling your name from above the stairs—who is it?

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

1. **Answer:** How deep your psychological scar
   * **Reasoning:** The number of steps you descend reflects the depth of emotional or psychological wounds you carry.
2. **Answer:** How you got through a bad experience
   * **Reasoning:** The type of sound you perceive indicates your coping mechanisms for past difficulties—whether you are sensitive, cautious, or resilient.
3. **Answer:** How you approach your darkness
   * **Reasoning:** Your reaction to the sound shows how you confront fear, stress, or inner challenges.
4. **Answer:** Who you can rely on during trouble
   * **Reasoning:** The person you hear calling your name represents your support system—the people you trust in difficult times.
