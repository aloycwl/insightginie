<h2>Understanding Stroke and Current Treatment Challenges</h2>
<p>Before delving into the specifics of the CereGlide catheters, it&#8217;s crucial to understand the context of stroke treatment. Stroke is the fifth leading cause of death in the United States and a major cause of long-term disability. Acute ischemic stroke, which accounts for about 87% of all stroke cases, occurs when a blood clot blocks blood flow to the brain.</p>
<p>Current treatment methods for acute ischemic stroke include intravenous thrombolysis and mechanical thrombectomy. While these treatments have improved outcomes for many patients, they still face limitations in terms of effectiveness, safety, and accessibility.</p>
