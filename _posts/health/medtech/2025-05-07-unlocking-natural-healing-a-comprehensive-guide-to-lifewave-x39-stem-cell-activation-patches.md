<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Understanding LifeWave X39: The Basics</h2>



<p>The LifeWave X39 patch is a non-transdermal, adhesive patch that utilizes phototherapy to reflect specific wavelengths of light onto the skin. This process is intended to stimulate the production of GHK-Cu (glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine copper), a naturally occurring peptide associated with various regenerative functions in the body. Unlike traditional patches, X39 does not deliver any substances into the body; instead, it aims to harness the body&#8217;s own energy to promote healing.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Science Behind GHK-Cu and Phototherapy</h2>



<p>GHK-Cu is a copper-binding peptide known for its role in tissue repair, anti-inflammatory responses, and activation of stem cells. Research indicates that increasing GHK-Cu levels can lead to improved skin health, accelerated wound healing, and enhanced cognitive function. A double-blind study demonstrated a significant increase in GHK-Cu concentration in individuals aged 40 to 80 after wearing the X39 patch for one week, suggesting the patch&#8217;s potential in stimulating this beneficial peptide.</p>
