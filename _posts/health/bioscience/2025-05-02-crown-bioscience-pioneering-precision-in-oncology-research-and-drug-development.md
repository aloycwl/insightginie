<p>In the rapidly evolving landscape of cancer research and drug development, Crown Bioscience stands out as a global leader, offering comprehensive preclinical and translational services that accelerate the journey from discovery to clinical application. Headquartered in San Diego, California, and operating across 11 sites in the United States, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific region, Crown Bioscience has established itself as a trusted partner for biotech and pharmaceutical companies worldwide.</p>

<p>Founded in 2006, Crown Bioscience has consistently expanded its capabilities to meet the growing demands of oncology and immuno-oncology research. The company&#8217;s integration into JSR Life Sciences in 2018 further bolstered its resources and global reach, enabling a more robust offering of services and technologies.</p>

<p>At the core of Crown Bioscience&#8217;s offerings is its extensive portfolio of preclinical models, including the world&#8217;s largest commercially available patient-derived xenograft (PDX) collection. These models, along with cell line-derived xenografts (CDX), organoids, and 3D cultures, provide researchers with physiologically relevant systems to evaluate drug efficacy and safety. The company&#8217;s strategic partnership with Hubrecht Organoid Technology (HUB) has further enhanced its capabilities in organoid-based research, offering over 600 models across 22 cancer indications.</p>

<p>Crown Bioscience&#8217;s services extend beyond model development. The company offers a suite of laboratory services, including genomics, proteomics, spatial biology, and bioanalytical testing. These services are designed to provide comprehensive insights into drug mechanisms of action, biomarker discovery, and patient stratification. Advanced technologies such as next-generation sequencing, mass spectrometry-based proteomics, and spatial multi-omics profiling are employed to generate high-quality, interpretable data that inform critical decision-making in drug development.</p>

<p>A significant milestone in Crown Bioscience&#8217;s growth was the acquisition and integration of Indivumed Services, completed in February 2025. This merger unified the strengths of both entities, enhancing Crown Bioscience&#8217;s biospecimen solutions and expanding its capabilities in oncology drug development. The integration has allowed for a more seamless offering of services from discovery through to clinical stages, reinforcing the company&#8217;s commitment to delivering comprehensive solutions to its clients.</p>

<p>Quality and customer satisfaction are paramount at Crown Bioscience. The company holds multiple ISO 9001:2015 certifications across its global facilities, reflecting its dedication to maintaining high standards in its operations. <p>Crown Bioscience&#8217;s mission is to empower its customers to improve human health. This is achieved by engaging top scientific talent, upholding rigorous quality standards, and relentlessly pursuing innovation. The company&#8217;s vision is a world where every patient receives the right treatment at the right time, a goal that drives its continuous efforts in advancing cancer research and therapy development.</p>

<p>As the field of oncology continues to evolve, Crown Bioscience remains at the forefront, providing the tools, expertise, and collaborative spirit necessary to translate scientific discoveries into effective treatments. Through its comprehensive services and unwavering commitment to excellence, Crown Bioscience is not only supporting the development of new cancer therapies but also shaping the future of personalized medicine.</p>
