Ghee—clarified butter derived from cow's milk—has long been celebrated in Ayurveda for its nourishing and healing properties. While ghee is often used in cooking or as a supplement, an ancient and increasingly popular practice is **drinking ghee water on an empty stomach**. This simple concoction, made by mixing a teaspoon of ghee in warm water, is hailed as a powerful way to balance the doshas, detoxify the body, and boost overall health.

In this article, we explore **10 scientifically and ayurvedically supported benefits** of ghee water, how to prepare it properly, and why it's becoming a go-to wellness ritual for those seeking holistic vitality.
