Microplastics—tiny plastic particles less than 5 millimeters in size—have infiltrated nearly every aspect of our environment, from oceans and air to food and water supplies. Recent studies have raised alarming concerns about their potential impact on human fertility. This article delves into the emerging evidence linking microplastic exposure to reproductive health issues and explores how artificial intelligence (AI) is being harnessed to address this global challenge.[a](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.17391?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
