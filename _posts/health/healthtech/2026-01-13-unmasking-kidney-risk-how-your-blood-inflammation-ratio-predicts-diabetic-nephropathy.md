Unmasking Kidney Risk: How Your Blood Inflammation Ratio Predicts Diabetic Nephropathy
======================================================================================

Diabetic nephropathy, a severe complication of diabetes, remains the leading cause of end-stage renal disease (ESRD) worldwide. Affecting millions, this progressive kidney disease often develops silently, with symptoms only emerging once significant damage has occurred. For years, medical science has focused on blood sugar control and blood pressure management as primary strategies to prevent and slow its progression. However, emerging research is shedding light on another critical, often overlooked, player: *inflammation*. Specifically, the blood inflammation ratio is proving to be a powerful, early predictor of kidney damage in individuals with diabetes.
