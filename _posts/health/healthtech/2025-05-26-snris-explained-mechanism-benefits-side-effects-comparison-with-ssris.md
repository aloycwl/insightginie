Serotonin-Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors (SNRIs) are a class of antidepressant medications that have gained prominence in treating various mental health conditions. Understanding how SNRIs function, their benefits, potential side effects, and how they compare to other antidepressants like Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors (SSRIs) is crucial for patients and healthcare providers alike.
