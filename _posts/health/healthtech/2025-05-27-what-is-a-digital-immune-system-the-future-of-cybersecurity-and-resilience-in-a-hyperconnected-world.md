In today's interconnected world, businesses face **cybersecurity risks at every touchpoint**—from cloud infrastructure to mobile applications, to IoT devices. As digital systems grow more complex, the need for proactive, adaptive, and intelligent protection becomes not just a priority but a necessity. This is where the **Digital Immune System (DIS)** comes into play.

Coined and popularized by **Gartner**, the Digital Immune System is an emerging concept that fuses multiple disciplines—**observability, AI, testing, automation, and security**—to create a system that can not only detect and respond to threats but also anticipate and adapt to them. Think of it as the human immune system, but for your digital environment.
