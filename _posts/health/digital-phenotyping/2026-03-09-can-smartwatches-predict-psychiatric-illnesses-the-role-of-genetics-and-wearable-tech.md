The Future of Mental Health: How Smartwatches are Changing the Diagnostic Landscape
===================================================================================

For years, wearable technology like smartwatches and fitness trackers has been primarily associated with counting steps, tracking heart rates during workouts, and monitoring sleep patterns. However, a revolutionary shift is occurring in the medical field. Recent studies suggest that these ubiquitous devices may hold the key to predicting psychiatric illnesses, especially those with a strong genetic component. By leveraging the power of longitudinal data, artificial intelligence, and genomics, researchers are beginning to uncover patterns that could alert patients and doctors to mental health crises long before they manifest clinically.
