The advent of **digital phenotyping** has revolutionized healthcare by enabling continuous, real-time monitoring of individuals' health status through mobile apps and other digital tools. Real-time monitoring platforms, combined with mobile apps, offer a wealth of data that can be analyzed to gain insights into an individual's behavior, physiological status, and environmental interactions. This real-time data collection plays a crucial role in **personalized medicine**, disease prevention, and chronic condition management.

In this article, we will explore the significant contributions of real-time monitoring platforms and mobile apps in digital phenotyping. We will discuss their potential for improving healthcare delivery, their applications in disease management, and the challenges and opportunities they present for the future of healthcare.
