Leveraging EHR Data: A Game-Changer for Chronic Kidney Disease Detection and Management
=======================================================================================

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is often referred to as a 'silent killer' because it progresses insidiously, with patients rarely showing symptoms until the disease has reached advanced stages. For decades, healthcare providers have struggled to identify CKD early enough to intervene effectively. However, the widespread adoption of Electronic Health Records (EHR) has unlocked a transformative opportunity to improve patient outcomes. By leveraging the vast, longitudinal data stored within EHRs, healthcare systems can now identify, monitor, and manage CKD with unprecedented precision.
