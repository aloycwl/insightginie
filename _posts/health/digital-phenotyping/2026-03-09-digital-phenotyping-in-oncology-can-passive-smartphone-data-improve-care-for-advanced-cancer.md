The Future of Monitoring: Digital Phenotyping in Advanced Cancer Care
=====================================================================

In the evolving landscape of oncology, the integration of digital health tools is transforming how we track patient outcomes. A particularly compelling area of research is the feasibility and acceptability of collecting passive smartphone data to facilitate digital phenotyping among patients with advanced cancer and their family caregivers. By analyzing data collected without active user input—such as movement patterns, screen time, and social interaction metrics—researchers hope to gain a nuanced understanding of a patient's daily quality of life.
