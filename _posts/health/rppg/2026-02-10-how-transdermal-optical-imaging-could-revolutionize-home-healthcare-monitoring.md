Imagine a world where a simple glance at your smartphone could reveal your blood pressure, stress levels, or even early signs of cardiovascular disease—no needles, no cuffs, no invasive procedures. Transdermal optical imaging (TOI) is poised to make this vision a reality, bridging the gap between clinical precision and at-home convenience. As healthcare shifts toward preventive and personalized models, this emerging technology could redefine how we monitor vital signs, detect anomalies, and manage chronic conditions outside traditional medical settings.
