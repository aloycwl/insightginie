Imagine unlocking your smartphone with a glance—not just to verify your identity, but to measure your blood pressure, stress levels, or even detect early signs of cardiovascular disease. This isn't science fiction; it's the emerging reality of **transdermal optical imaging (TOI)**, a technology poised to redefine how consumer electronics interact with human biology. By leveraging advanced camera sensors and machine learning, TOI transforms everyday devices like smartphones and wearables into non-invasive health monitors, bridging the gap between convenience and clinical-grade diagnostics.
