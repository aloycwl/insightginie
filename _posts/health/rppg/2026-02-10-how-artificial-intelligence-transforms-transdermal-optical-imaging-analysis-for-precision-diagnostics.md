Imagine a world where a simple glance at your skin could reveal hidden cardiovascular risks, stress levels, or even early signs of neurological disorders—no needles, no invasive procedures, just light and intelligence. This isn't science fiction; it's the emerging reality of **transdermal optical imaging (TOI)** powered by artificial intelligence. As healthcare shifts toward non-invasive, real-time diagnostics, the fusion of AI and TOI is redefining what's possible in medical imaging. But how exactly does this technology work, and why is AI the missing link that turns raw optical data into actionable insights?
