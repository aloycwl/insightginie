<h2>The Significance of the IAPR 2024 Maria Petrou Prize</h2>
<p>The <strong>Maria Petrou Prize</strong> was established to honor the memory of Professor Maria Petrou, a renowned figure in computer vision who passed away in 2011. The prize is awarded every two years to a female researcher who has made significant contributions to pattern recognition. It serves a dual purpose: recognizing scientific excellence and promoting gender diversity in STEM fields.</p>
