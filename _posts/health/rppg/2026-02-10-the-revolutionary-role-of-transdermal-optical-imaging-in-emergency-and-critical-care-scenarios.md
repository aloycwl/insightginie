Imagine a world where healthcare professionals can assess a patient's vital signs without touching them—no probes, no wires, and no invasive procedures. This isn't science fiction; it's the promise of **transdermal optical imaging (TOI)** in emergency and critical care scenarios. As hospitals and emergency responders grapple with the need for faster, more accurate diagnostics, TOI emerges as a game-changer, offering real-time, contactless monitoring that could redefine patient care.
