The intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and healthcare continues to spark groundbreaking innovations. One of the most promising frontiers is **AI-driven rPPG (remote Photoplethysmography) avatars**—personalized digital twins designed to monitor and enhance cardiovascular health in real-time. These virtual representations of individuals use non-invasive, camera-based technology to analyze vital signs and provide actionable health insights, offering unprecedented potential for entrepreneurs, startups, and investors looking to enter the AI-healthtech space.

As healthcare moves toward prevention, personalization, and digital-first experiences, rPPG avatars are poised to become an essential tool in remote diagnostics, wellness monitoring, and chronic disease management.
