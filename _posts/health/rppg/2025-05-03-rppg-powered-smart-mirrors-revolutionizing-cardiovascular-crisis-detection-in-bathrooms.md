### Introduction: Smart Mirrors Meet Heart Health

Imagine walking into your bathroom, looking into your mirror—and receiving an alert that your heart rate variability is abnormal, signaling a possible cardiovascular event. This is no longer science fiction. Thanks to **remote photoplethysmography (rPPG)** technology, **smart mirrors** are being reimagined as life-saving tools, capable of **early detection of heart attacks, strokes, and other circulatory issues**, all without physical contact.

rPPG leverages standard RGB cameras to detect subtle color changes in the face caused by blood flow. When integrated into smart mirrors, it transforms ordinary bathroom routines into powerful health monitoring sessions. For **entrepreneurs, small business owners**, and **healthtech investors**, this convergence of AI and wellness hardware represents a new frontier: **non-invasive, passive, and ambient health surveillance** that can scale in homes, hotels, hospitals, and even eldercare facilities.
