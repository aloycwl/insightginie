### Core Features of the Skill

The flexibility of this skill is its greatest asset. By utilizing the **each::sense AI**, users can request specific aesthetic styles. Here is a breakdown of the primary visual modes available:

* **Waveform Visualizers:** The timeless choice for voice content and podcasts. These clean, classic oscilloscope patterns provide a professional look that keeps the focus on the speaker.
* **Spectrum Analyzers:** Perfect for high-energy electronic music. These frequency bars offer a precise, rhythm-driven experience that visualizes the intensity of your tracks.
* **Circular Visualizers:** Ideal for album art and social media squares. These radial, audio-reactive rings turn any track into a stunning piece of digital art.
* **Particle Systems:** For those seeking a dramatic impact, these systems create explosions of light and color that respond specifically to bass hits and transients.
* **3D Visualizations:** Perhaps the most immersive option, these tools generate 3D landscapes that rise and fall with the audio frequencies, creating a cinematic experience.
* **Abstract Reactives:** For artistic projects, these fluid, morphing shapes offer a meditative and highly unique aesthetic.

### How to Get Started

Integrating this skill into your workflow is surprisingly straightforward. Because it operates via a REST API, you can trigger visual generation directly from your own applications or simple terminal commands. The process is handled through a POST request to the **each::sense API**. By providing a direct link to your audio file and describing your desired style in plain English, the AI interprets your request and renders the video.

For example, to generate a neon waveform, you simply provide the audio URL, specify the color scheme (e.g., purple and cyan), and set the output format. The system supports multiple modes—’eco’ for faster, lighter renders or ‘max’ for high-fidelity, complex animations—ensuring you have control over both production speed and quality.
