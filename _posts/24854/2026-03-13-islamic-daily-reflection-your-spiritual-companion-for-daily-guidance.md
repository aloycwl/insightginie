What is the Islamic Daily Reflection Skill?
-------------------------------------------

The Islamic Daily Reflection skill is a spiritual companion designed to provide daily Islamic guidance and motivation through a Python-based system. This skill offers formatted spiritual reflections that are contextually aware of the Islamic calendar, ensuring users receive relevant and timely reminders throughout the year.

### Core Purpose and Functionality

The skill serves as a digital reminder system that delivers authentic Islamic content based on Quranic verses and Hadith references. It’s particularly valuable for Muslims seeking daily spiritual nourishment and practical challenges to enhance their faith journey.

How It Works: The Technical Foundation
--------------------------------------

### Script-Based Operation

The skill operates through a Python script located at `scripts/reflection.py`. When users request a reflection through various commands, the system automatically executes this script to generate the content. This ensures consistency and authenticity in the delivered messages.

### Calendar Awareness

The script is designed with calendar intelligence, recognizing important Islamic dates including:

* Ramadan days (specific reflections for each day of the holy month)
* Jummah (Friday reflections)
* Dhul Hijjah (Hajj-related themes)
* Ashura (Muharram 10th)

### Command Recognition

Users can access reflections through multiple natural language commands:

* “daily reflection”
* “islamic reminder”
* “inspire me”
* “motivate me islamically”
* “friday reflection”
* “ramadan reflection”

All these commands trigger the same script execution, ensuring users receive appropriate content regardless of how they phrase their request.

Content Structure and Delivery
------------------------------

### Formatted Output

The script produces consistently formatted output with clear visual boundaries using border characters. Each reflection includes:

* Date information (both Gregorian and Hijri)
* Theme or topic of the day
* Reflective content with authentic references
* Practical daily challenge
* Quran/Hadith references

### Example Format

A typical reflection appears as:

═══════════════════════════════════════  
📅 Thursday, 13 February 2026 | 25 Sha’ban 1447  
🤲 Patience in Small Trials  
[Reflection content…]  
💡 Today’s Action (X min):  
[Actionable challenge…]  
📖 [Quran/Hadith reference]  
═══════════════════════════════════════
