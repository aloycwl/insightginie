What is the Catholic Grounding Skill?
-------------------------------------

The Catholic Grounding skill is a tool that helps users answer questions about Catholicism accurately and respectfully, using the Catechism of the Catholic Church (CCC) as its primary reference. It provides a structured response format, a common topic map with CCC references, and short prayer/reference snippets. This skill ensures that responses are not only accurate but also grounded in the teachings of the Church.

How Does the Catholic Grounding Skill Work?
-------------------------------------------

The Catholic Grounding skill operates through a set of scripts and reference materials that provide quick and accurate answers to questions about Catholicism. Here’s a breakdown of its components:

### Quick Start

Users can quickly access CCC pointers for a topic or print a prayer snippet using simple commands:

* `./scripts/ccc.sh "eucharist"` – Retrieves CCC references for the topic of the Eucharist.
* `./scripts/prayer.sh "hail mary"` – Prints a snippet of the Hail Mary prayer.
* `./scripts/status.sh` – Checks what is included in the skill.

### Default Answer Format

The skill uses a structured format for answers, ensuring clarity and accuracy:

* **Short answer** – A concise response of 1–3 sentences.
* **What the Church teaches** – A clear, neutral explanation of Catholic teaching.
* **Citations** – References to CCC sections and Scripture.
* **Practical next step** – A suggestion for further action, such as talking to a priest or reading a specific section of the CCC.

### Manual Reference Access

Users can access various reference materials locally:

* **CCC topic map** – `references/ccc-topic-map.md`
* **Prayer snippets** – `references/prayers.md`
* **Tone/style** – `references/style.md`

### Guardrails

The skill includes guardrails to ensure accurate and respectful responses:

* **Distinguishing concepts** – Differentiates between dogma, doctrine, discipline, and prudential judgment.
* **Primary sources** – Prefers the CCC for concise teaching, Scripture for biblical grounding, and Councils/encyclicals when necessary.
* **Respect for other religions** – Maintains respect for other religions and denominations.
* **Avoiding misinformation** – Avoids making medical or legal claims and encourages real pastoral support when appropriate.
