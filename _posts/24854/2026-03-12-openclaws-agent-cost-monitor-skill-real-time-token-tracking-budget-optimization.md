The Cost Challenge in Multi-Agent Systems
-----------------------------------------

Modern AI systems often deploy multiple specialized agents working together to solve complex problems. While this multi-agent approach delivers superior results, it introduces substantial cost management challenges:

* Which agent is burning through the most tokens?
* Are background processes like heartbeats consuming expensive model resources unnecessarily?
* Is your caching strategy actually delivering promised savings?
* When will you unexpectedly hit your weekly rate limits?

These unanswered questions can lead to unpleasant budget shocks. OpenClaw’s Agent Cost Monitor Skill provides complete visibility into your agent costs.

Introducing the Agent Cost Monitor Skill
----------------------------------------

Almost completely overlooked in most AI systems, running costs eventually become a major issue. That’s where OpenClaw’s real-time cost monitoring system makes a game-changing difference by:

* Checking session status for each agent
* Calculating per-agent and total costs
* Comparing usage against budget thresholds
* Sending alerts when approaching set limits
* Providing tailored optimization suggestions

### Bringing Financial Clarity to Your AI Infrastructure

The Agent Cost Monitor transforms your agent costs from an opaque expense into a transparent, manageable component of your system:

* **Per-Agent Breakdown**: Detailed usage and cost metrics for each individual agent
* **Projected Costs**: Estimates extending to weekly and monthly totals
* **Budget Comparison**: Visual indications when approaching thresholds
* **Actionable Recommendations**: Specific suggestions for immediate improvements
