Understanding the 5-Slide Flow Architecture
-------------------------------------------

The methodology relies on a strict but effective structure consisting of five distinct slides. This format is optimized for the carousel feature on TikTok, which currently sees high engagement rates due to the platform’s algorithm favoring long-time-on-page metrics.

* **Slides 1-4 (Visual Storytelling):** These slides utilize the Nano Banana image generation tool to maintain visual consistency and narrative flow. By telling a visual story rather than just displaying a static ad, the user is kept engaged through each swipe.
* **Slide 5 (The Powerhouse CTA):** The final slide is strictly text-based. It serves as the Call to Action (CTA), directing users straight to the app. By eliminating visual noise on the final slide, the viewer’s focus is locked entirely on the next steps they need to take.

The Nano Banana Aesthetic Rules
-------------------------------

One of the most critical aspects of this skill is its adherence to the ‘TikTok Aesthetic.’ In an era of over-polished, studio-quality ads, TikTok users are increasingly gravitating toward authentic, ‘real’ looking content. The system instructions for this skill mandate specific visual parameters:

### Mandatory Aesthetic Elements:

* **Candid Vertical Smartphone Photos:** Everything must look like it was captured on a personal device.
* **Natural Indoor Lighting:** Avoids the ‘staged’ feel of professional ads.
* **Slightly Grainy Texture:** Adds a layer of realism and grit that feels native to the app.
* **Realistic Skin:** A rejection of airbrushed perfection in favor of human skin textures.
* **Messy Backgrounds:** Creates a sense of relatability and ‘at-home’ comfort.

By explicitly forbidding flash, studio lighting, and cartoonish filters, the skill ensures that the generated imagery blends seamlessly into a user’s feed, increasing the chances of organic discovery.

To avoid disjointed visuals, the skill utilizes fixed character profiles. By selecting a specific archetype—such as a woman with messy curly hair and glasses or a woman with a sleek black bob—the AI agent ensures that the same ‘protagonist’ appears across all four imagery slides. This character consistency is vital for building trust. When a viewer sees the same person from the first slide to the fourth, they feel they are following a real person’s journey rather than watching a disconnected advertisement.
