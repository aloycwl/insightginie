AI Embedded Across the Enterprise: Strategic Insights from Mass General Brigham
===============================================================================

The promise of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in healthcare has long been discussed, yet the transition from experimental pilots to enterprise-wide implementation remains a significant hurdle for many health systems. Mass General Brigham (MGB), a leader in academic medicine and research, has emerged as a vanguard in this space, providing a blueprint for how large healthcare organizations can successfully embed AI into their core operations, clinical workflows, and research paradigms. By moving beyond isolated use cases to a systemic approach, MGB is demonstrating the tangible value that AI can bring to the modern healthcare enterprise.
