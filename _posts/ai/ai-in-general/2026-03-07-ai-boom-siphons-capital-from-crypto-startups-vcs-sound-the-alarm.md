AI Boom Siphons Capital From Crypto Startups: VCs Sound the Alarm
=================================================================

The landscape of venture capital is undergoing a seismic shift. For years, the promise of blockchain technology, decentralized finance (DeFi), and the metaverse dominated the investment priorities of the world's top venture capital firms. However, the meteoric rise of generative artificial intelligence has redirected the flow of money, leaving many crypto-native startups scrambling for liquidity. As AI becomes the new darling of Silicon Valley, industry insiders are warning that the capital drain could stall innovation in the Web3 sector for years to come.
