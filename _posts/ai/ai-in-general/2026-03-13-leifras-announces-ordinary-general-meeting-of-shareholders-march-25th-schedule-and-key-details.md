Leifras Announces Ordinary General Meeting of Shareholders for March 25
=======================================================================

In a significant move for its corporate governance calendar, Leifras has officially announced that its Ordinary General Meeting (OGM) of Shareholders will take place on March 25. This annual gathering represents a critical juncture for the company, providing a platform for management to engage with shareholders, review the fiscal performance of the previous year, and outline the strategic trajectory for the months ahead. As we approach this important date, investors and stakeholders alike are preparing to evaluate the company's recent trajectory, governance standards, and long-term value proposition.
