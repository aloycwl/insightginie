The rapid evolution of Artificial Intelligence (AI) has ushered in an era of unprecedented potential, but it also presents a host of ethical, legal, and societal challenges. One of the most pressing concerns is how to govern this powerful technology effectively. As AI systems become increasingly sophisticated, the need for a robust and nuanced approach to AI governance becomes paramount. This article explores key aspects of AI governance, including jurisdiction, copyright, intellectual property, and risk mitigation strategies.
