As artificial intelligence (AI) becomes a cornerstone of innovation and global economic growth, governments worldwide are racing to establish clear and enforceable AI laws. The *Global AI Law and Policy Tracker* provides an invaluable snapshot of how different countries are shaping their legal frameworks to govern AI technologies. From comprehensive national AI strategies to specific legislative proposals and regulatory sandboxes, understanding the nuances of these policies is essential for businesses, developers, legal professionals, and policymakers. This guide breaks down the most significant updates and developments in global AI governance, offering clarity and insight into this rapidly evolving legal domain.
