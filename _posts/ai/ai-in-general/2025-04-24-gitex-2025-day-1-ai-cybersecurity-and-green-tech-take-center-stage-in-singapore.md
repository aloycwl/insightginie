GITEX 2025 launched with a dynamic start, uniting global tech leaders, innovators, and policymakers across two major venues: Dubai and Singapore. The first day set the tone for a week of groundbreaking announcements, strategic collaborations, and visionary discussions, emphasizing the transformative power of technology in shaping our future.​

In Dubai, the event commenced with a keynote address by H.H Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, highlighting the UAE's commitment to fostering innovation and digital transformation. The venue buzzed with excitement as attendees explored the latest advancements in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and sustainable technologies. Notably, the “AI 100” program showcased a curated selection of 100 award-winning AI startups from around the globe, reflecting the event's focus on cutting-edge innovation. ​

Simultaneously, GITEX Asia 2025 in Singapore marked a significant milestone, with Vietnam making its strategic debut in Southeast Asia's tech scene. The conference delved into pressing regional issues, including green tech and smart cities, aligning with Southeast Asia's sustainability goals. Discussions also covered health tech, biotech, and the challenges of cybersecurity in today's digital landscape. The event's thematic structure provided a focused lens on impactful technological domains, ensuring comprehensive coverage of the region's unique challenges and opportunities. ​

A highlight of the day was the “Crystal Ball” forum, part of the Counter Ransomware Initiative, which concentrated on enhancing resilience against ransomware threats. This initiative underscored the event's commitment to addressing critical digital threats and fostering potent debates on global technological shifts.​

The inaugural GITEX Cyber Valley Asia gathered global experts, influential companies, and security agencies to bolster regional cyber readiness. This collaborative effort emphasized the importance of cybersecurity solutions tailored to Southeast Asia's specific needs, highlighting the region's proactive approach to digital threats.​

With over 700 global tech enterprises and startups from more than 70 countries, GITEX Asia x Ai Everything Singapore convened a truly global powerhouse of technology players. The event's diverse participation underscored its significant international draw, fostering cross-border collaborations and knowledge exchange.

As GITEX 2025 progresses, the momentum from the first day promises a week filled with innovation, collaboration, and transformative ideas. Attendees can look forward to exploring the latest technological advancements and engaging in discussions that will shape the digital landscape for years to come.​
