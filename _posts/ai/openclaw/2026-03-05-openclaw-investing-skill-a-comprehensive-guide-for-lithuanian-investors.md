OpenClaw Investing Skill: A Comprehensive Guide for Lithuanian Investors
========================================================================

Welcome to the ultimate guide on the OpenClaw Investing Skill, a personal investing assistant tailored for Lithuanian investors. This tool is designed to help you monitor markets, ETFs, crypto, pension funds (III pakopa), and provide monthly investment suggestions based on research and market conditions. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced investor, this guide will walk you through what the skill does, how it works, its use cases, and the benefits it offers.

What is the OpenClaw Investing Skill?
-------------------------------------

The OpenClaw Investing Skill is an AI-powered assistant that provides a structured approach to investing, inspired by evidence-based, passive investing principles. It is designed to simplify the investment process and help Lithuanian investors make informed decisions.

Core Philosophy
---------------

The skill is based on the following core principles:

* **VWCE & Chill**: A simple, low-cost global ETF strategy.
* **Long-term focus**: Emphasizes a 10+ year investment horizon.
* **Diversification**: Encourages diversification across asset classes and geographies.
* **Low costs**: Aims to minimize fees to maximize returns.

How the Skill Works
-------------------

The OpenClaw Investing Skill uses a combination of scripts and cron jobs to provide timely investment advice and monitor market conditions.

### Scripts

* **check-etf-prices.sh**: Checks key ETF prices and provides updates.
* **monthly-dca.sh**: Calculates monthly Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) amounts based on your budget.

### Cron Jobs

* **Daily Market Check**: Runs at 9 AM on weekdays to check VWCE and BTC prices.
* **Monthly DCA Reminder**: Runs at 10 AM on the first of each month to remind you to execute your DCA plan.
* **Quarterly Portfolio Review**: Runs at 10 AM on the first of January, April, July, and October to review your portfolio and suggest rebalancing.
