<h3>2. Classic Ciphers (Decoder Ring Era)</h3>
<p>SecretCodex offers a range of simple yet nostalgic ciphers perfect for fun or educational purposes:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Caesar Cipher</strong>: Alphabetic shift with a fixed number. Weak security (⭐☆☆☆☆).</li>
<li><strong>ROT13</strong>: Special case of Caesar cipher with a shift of 13. Quick obfuscation (⭐☆☆☆☆).</li>
<li><strong>Atbash Cipher</strong>: Reverse alphabet substitution. Simple reversal (⭐☆☆☆☆).</li>
<li><strong>Pigpen Cipher</strong>: Symbol substitution based on grids. Visual encoding (⭐⭐☆☆☆).</li>
</ul>
<h3>3. Intermediate Ciphers (Getting Stronger)</h3>
<p>For those seeking something more robust, SecretCodex includes:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Polybius Square</strong>: Encodes letters into grid coordinates. Modular strength (⭐⭐☆☆☆).</li>
<li><strong>Vigenère Cipher</strong>: Keyword-based shift for each letter. Moderate security (⭐⭐⭐☆☆).</li>
<li><strong>Rail Fence Cipher</strong>: Transposition cipher with zigzag patterns. Stronger complexity (⭐⭐⭐☆☆).</li>
</ul>
