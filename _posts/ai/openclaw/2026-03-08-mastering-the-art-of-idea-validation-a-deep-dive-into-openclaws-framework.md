Why Idea Validation is the Secret Weapon for Modern Solopreneurs
================================================================

Every entrepreneur has felt the itch of a 'million-dollar idea.' You see a problem, you imagine a perfect solution, and you feel the sudden urge to start building immediately. But there is a silent killer in the startup world: premature construction. Investing months of engineering or thousands of dollars into a product that nobody wants is the fastest way to kill your entrepreneurial dreams. This is where the OpenClaw *Idea Validation* skill becomes your most critical asset. By providing a structured, no-nonsense framework to stress-test your assumptions, it ensures you only build what people actually need.
