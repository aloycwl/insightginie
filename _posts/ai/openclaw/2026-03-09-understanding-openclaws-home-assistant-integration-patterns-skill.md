What is Home Assistant?
-----------------------

Home Assistant is an open-source home automation platform that allows you to control and automate various aspects of your home. It supports a wide range of devices and protocols, making it a popular choice for smart home enthusiasts. With Home Assistant, you can create custom integrations to connect new devices or services to your home automation setup.
