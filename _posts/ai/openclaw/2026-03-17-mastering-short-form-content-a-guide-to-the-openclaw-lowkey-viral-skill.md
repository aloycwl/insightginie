<h2>Key Features and Capabilities</h2>
<p>This skill provides two primary methods for content creation, catering to different viral formats:</p>

<p>The skill excels at generating 1080&#215;1920 vertical MP4 videos that are perfectly optimized for mobile feeds. By utilizing a 2&#215;2 image grid, it creates a fast-paced viewing experience that keeps users engaged. Features include:</p>
<ul>

</ul>
<h3>2. High-Engagement Photo Slideshows</h3>
<p>Photo carousels are currently one of the most effective ways to drive engagement on Instagram and TikTok. The Lowkey Viral skill makes generating these slideshows simple. <h2>How the Workflow Works</h2>
<p>The beauty of this skill lies in its structured workflow, which relies on the Lowkey Viral REST API. The process generally follows a four-step pipeline:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Generate AI Briefs:</strong> You define the topic, and the AI returns professional-grade content briefs.</li>
<li><strong>Generate Assets:</strong> Based on the brief, the system generates high-quality images in the correct orientation.</li>
<li><strong>Render:</strong> With a single command, you trigger the final rendering process.</li>
<li><strong>Polling/Retrieval:</strong> The API handles the heavy lifting, allowing you to monitor the progress and grab your final file once complete.</li>
</ol>
