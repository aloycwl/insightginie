<p>The Seedance 2.0 Storyboard Director offers several advanced techniques to enhance the creative process:</p>

<h2>Avoiding Common Pitfalls</h2>
<p>When working with Seedance 2.0, it&#8217;s important to avoid common pitfalls that can compromise the quality of your storyboard prompts:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vague References</strong>: Avoid just writing <code>reference @video1</code>; specify what to reference (camera movement, action, or lighting). Clear instructions lead to more accurate results.</li>
<li><strong>Conflicting Instructions</strong>: Do not request contradictory instructions, such as &#8220;fixed camera&#8221; and &#8220;orbiting movement&#8221; at the same time. Ensuring that your instructions are consistent and logical.</li>
<li><strong>Overload</strong>: Avoid cramming too many complex action descriptions into a short timeframe (e.g., 3 seconds). Spreading out complex actions over a longer duration for better clarity and execution.</li>
</ul>
