<h2>What is the Instagram Marketing Generator Skill?</h2>
<p>The Instagram Marketing Generator is a powerful skill that transforms any product URL from e-commerce platforms like Amazon, Shopify, Taobao, and more into comprehensive Instagram marketing content packages. This skill extracts product information and generates platform-native content designed to drive engagement and conversions.</p>
<h2>Core Features</h2>
<p>The skill provides a complete content generation workflow including:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Product Information Extraction</strong>: Automatically fetches product details using scripts/extract_product.py</li>
<li><strong>Content Frameworks</strong>: Tailored templates for different product types (fashion, tech, food, home, services)</li>
<li><strong>Multi-format Support</strong>: Generates content for Feed posts, Carousels, Stories, and Reels</li>
<li><strong>Hashtag Strategy</strong>: Optimized 30-hashtag combinations for maximum reach</li>
<li><strong>Visual Guidelines</strong>: Image/video briefs with composition and aesthetic direction</li>
</ul>
<h2>How It Works</h2>
<p>The skill follows a three-step workflow:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Input</strong>: Provide a product URL from supported e-commerce sites</li>
<li><strong>Extract</strong>: The system fetches product details including name, price, features, target audience, and brand tone</li>
<li><strong>Generate</strong>: Apply Instagram content frameworks to create ready-to-post content packages</li>
</ol>
<h2>Content Elements Included</h2>
<p>Each generated package contains:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Image/Video Brief</strong>: Visual style, composition guidelines, and product angle recommendations</li>
<li><strong>Caption Structure</strong>: Hook, body, and CTA following optimal engagement patterns</li>
<li><strong>Hashtag Strategy</strong>: 30 optimized hashtags mixing branded, trending, and broad tags</li>
<li><strong>Posting Strategy</strong>: Timing recommendations and cross-post suggestions</li>
</ul>
<h2>Content Frameworks by Product Type</h2>
<p>The skill adapts to different product categories:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Fashion/Beauty</strong>: Aesthetic + Lifestyle framework focusing on visual appeal</li>
<li><strong>Tech/Gadgets</strong>: Feature-First + Demo framework highlighting product capabilities</li>
<li><strong>Food/Beverage</strong>: Sensory + Lifestyle framework emphasizing experience and taste</li>
<li><strong>Home/Decor</strong>: Transformation + Tips framework showing before/after results</li>
<li><strong>Services</strong>: Trust + Results framework building credibility through testimonials</li>
</ul>
<h2>Instagram Style Guidelines</h2>
<p>The skill incorporates Instagram best practices:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Visual Consistency</strong>: Cohesive color palettes and recognizable brand aesthetics</li>
<li><strong>Quality Standards</strong>: High-resolution images and good lighting requirements</li>
<li><strong>Native Content</strong>: Authentic, non-stock looking content that feels organic</li>

</ul>
