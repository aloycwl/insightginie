What This Skill Does
--------------------

The Prayer Times skill provides instant, accurate Islamic prayer times for any location worldwide. Whether you're at home or traveling, this skill helps you stay on schedule with your daily prayers by automatically detecting your location or accepting any city/country name you specify.

### Key Features

* **Auto-location detection** – Automatically detects your city using your IP address
* **Global coverage** – Works for any city in the world, from Makkah to New York
* **Smart typo handling** – Handles misspellings like “Meca” or “Dubay” with fuzzy search
* **ISNA calculation method** – Uses the widely accepted Islamic Society of North America calculation
* **12-hour format** – Shows times in easy-to-read AM/PM format
* **Complete prayer schedule** – Displays all five daily prayers plus sunrise

How to Use
----------

### Auto-Detect Your Location

The simplest way to use this skill is to just ask for prayer times without specifying a location:

```
prayer times
prayer times today
what time is prayer?
```

The skill will automatically detect your current location and provide accurate prayer times for today.

### Specify Any City Worldwide

You can also get prayer times for any city around the world:

```
prayer times Makkah

prayer times New York

prayer times Jakarta
```

Technical Features
------------------

The skill uses several reliable data sources to provide accurate information:

* **Location Detection**: ipapi.co for auto-detecting your location from IP address
* **Geocoding**: OpenStreetMap Nominatim for finding coordinates of any city
* **Prayer Times**: Aladhan API using the ISNA calculation method

The skill requires internet access to fetch location and prayer time data, but it doesn't store any personal data or access your file system.
