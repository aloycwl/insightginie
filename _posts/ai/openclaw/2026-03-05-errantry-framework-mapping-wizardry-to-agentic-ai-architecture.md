Errantry Framework: Mapping Wizardry to Agentic AI Architecture
===============================================================

In the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence, the need for robust architectural patterns and ethical frameworks has never been more critical. The Errantry Framework, inspired by Diane Duane's Young Wizards series, offers a unique approach to designing agent workflows and multi-agent systems. By mapping magical concepts to AI architecture patterns, this framework provides a shared vocabulary, ethical guardrails, and heuristics for recognizing and mitigating entropic patterns such as drift, collapse, and misalignment.
