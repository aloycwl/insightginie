<h2>Tips for Better Results</h2>
<p>To maximize the quality of generated images, consider these best practices:</p>
<h3>Be Descriptive</h3>
<p>Instead of vague prompts like &#8220;woman in office,&#8221; provide specific details: &#8220;Confident woman in her 40s, silver blazer, modern glass-walled office, warm afternoon light.&#8221;</p>
<h3>Specify Style</h3>
<p>Clearly indicate the desired artistic style, whether it&#8217;s photorealistic, digital illustration, watercolor, or anime.</p>
<h3>Describe Lighting</h3>
<p>Lighting dramatically affects the mood and quality of images. Specify lighting conditions such as &#8220;soft natural light,&#8221; &#8220;dramatic side lighting,&#8221; or &#8220;golden hour glow.&#8221;</p>
<h3>Character Consistency</h3>
<p>When creating character series, describe the character in detail first, then reference &#8220;the same character&#8221; in subsequent prompts to maintain consistency.</p>
<h3>Include Composition</h3>
<p>Specify compositional elements like &#8220;rule of thirds,&#8221; &#8220;close-up portrait,&#8221; or &#8220;wide establishing shot&#8221; to guide the image generation process.</p>
