Introduction to the Kling AI Skill for OpenClaw
===============================================

In the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence, content creators and developers are constantly seeking ways to streamline their production pipelines. One of the most significant leaps forward is the integration of high-quality AI video generation directly into automated workflows. The OpenClaw project has recently introduced a powerful tool in its repository: the Kling AI Video Generation skill. This skill allows users to leverage the advanced capabilities of the Kling 2.6 model—accessed via Kie.ai—to transform text prompts into cinematic video content seamlessly.
