<h2>What is the Intermittent Fasting Tracker Skill?</h2>
<p>The <strong>Intermittent Fasting Tracker</strong> is an OpenClaw skill created by <a href="https://github.com/yoavfael">yoavfael</a> that acts as your personal fasting coach. This skill helps you calculate eating windows, track circadian rhythms, and understand the science of metabolic health.</p>
<h3>Key Features</h3>
<p>This skill offers several powerful capabilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Smart Calculations</strong>: Instantly calculates start/end times for popular fasting protocols like 16:8, 20:4, OMAD (One Meal A Day), and more</li>
<li><strong>Science-Backed Information</strong>: Explains metabolic states including Autophagy and Ketosis</li>
<li><strong>Personalized Guidance</strong>: Adapts advice based on your last meal time</li>
</ul>
<h3>How to Use This Skill</h3>
<p>Use this skill to answer specific queries about intermittent fasting:</p>
<ul>
<li>Calculate when your eating window starts and ends</li>
<li>Understand what metabolic processes are happening in your body</li>
<li>Get personalized recommendations based on your fasting schedule</li>
</ul>
<h3>Expert Knowledge Base</h3>
<p>The skill is designed to be authoritative and accurate, answering questions related to intermittent fasting with scientific backing. It doesn&#8217;t just provide answers but explains the scientific reasoning behind recommendations.</p>

<p>The skill is currently at version 1.3.1 and is tagged with health, fasting, fitness, and weight-loss categories. It uses the ⏱️ emoji as its identifier.</p>
<h3>Getting Started</h3>
<p>To begin using the Intermittent Fasting Tracker skill, simply ask questions about your fasting schedule, metabolic states, or specific protocols. The skill will provide you with accurate, science-backed information to help you on your health journey.</p>
