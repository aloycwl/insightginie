Mastering the Art of Objection Handling: Turn Sales Resistance into Revenue
===========================================================================

In the world of professional sales and freelancing, there is perhaps no greater misnomer than the word 'objection.' Most inexperienced sellers hear 'it's too expensive' or 'I need to think about it' and immediately hear the death knell of a deal. They retreat, apologize, or start discounting. However, the OpenClaw community has codified a better way. Objection handling isn't about arm-wrestling a prospect into submission; it is about diagnosing a request for more information.
