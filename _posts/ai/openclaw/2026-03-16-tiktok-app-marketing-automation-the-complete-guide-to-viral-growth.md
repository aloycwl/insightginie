### Critical First Step: TikTok Account Warmup

Before anything else, check if you already have a TikTok account with posting history. If you're creating a fresh account, you MUST warm it up first or TikTok will treat you like a bot and throttle your reach from day one.

Here's why this matters: accounts that skip warmup consistently get 80-90% less reach on their first posts. The difference between your first post getting 200 views and 20,000 could be this simple step.

If your account is new or barely used, spend 7-14 days warming it up:

* Spend 30-60 minutes daily on the app
* Scroll the For You page naturally
* Watch some videos all the way through, skip others halfway
* Like sparingly (maybe 1 in 10 videos)
* Follow accounts in your niche
* Watch niche content intentionally
* Leave a few genuine comments
* Post 1-2 casual videos (nothing promotional)

The signal to look for: when almost every video on your For You page is in your niche, the account is warmed up. The algorithm understands you.
