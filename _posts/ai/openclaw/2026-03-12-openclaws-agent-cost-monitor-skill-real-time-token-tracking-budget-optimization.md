<h2>The Cost Challenge in Multi-Agent Systems</h2>
<p>Modern AI systems often deploy multiple specialized agents working together to solve complex problems. While this multi-agent approach delivers superior results, it introduces substantial cost management challenges:</p>
<ul>
<li>Which agent is burning through the most tokens?</li>
<li>Are background processes like heartbeats consuming expensive model resources unnecessarily?</li>
<li>Is your caching strategy actually delivering promised savings?</li>
<li>When will you unexpectedly hit your weekly rate limits?</li>
</ul>
<p>These unanswered questions can lead to unpleasant budget shocks. OpenClaw&#8217;s Agent Cost Monitor Skill provides complete visibility into your agent costs.</p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Introducing the Agent Cost Monitor Skill</h2>
<p>Almost completely overlooked in most AI systems, running costs eventually become a major issue. That&#8217;s where OpenClaw&#8217;s real-time cost monitoring system makes a game-changing difference by:</p>
<ul>
<li>Checking session status for each agent</li>
<li>Calculating per-agent and total costs</li>
<li>Comparing usage against budget thresholds</li>
<li>Sending alerts when approaching set limits</li>
<li>Providing tailored optimization suggestions</li>
</ul>
