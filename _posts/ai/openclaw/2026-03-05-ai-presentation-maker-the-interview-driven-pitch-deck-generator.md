<h1>AI Presentation Maker: The Interview-Driven Pitch Deck Generator</h1>
<p>In today&#8217;s fast-paced digital world, creating compelling presentations can be a time-consuming task. <h2>What is AI Presentation Maker?</h2>
<p>AI Presentation Maker is an innovative tool that creates presentation decks based on user-provided information. Unlike traditional presentation tools that rely on templates and placeholder text, AI Presentation Maker builds slides from factual data, ensuring that every number and claim in your deck is accurate and based on real work.</p>
<h2>How Does AI Presentation Maker Work?</h2>
<p>AI Presentation Maker operates through a structured interview process. Here&#8217;s a step-by-step breakdown of how it works:</p>
<h3>1. The Interview Process</h3>
<p>The tool begins by asking a series of questions to gather essential information about your project, audience, and presentation goals. This interview is divided into six phases:</p>
<h4>Phase 1: The Subject</h4>
<p>What&#8217;s this presentation about? Give me the short version — what did you build, do, or accomplish?</p>
<h4>Phase 2: The Audience</h4>
<p>Who&#8217;s in the room? How many people? What do they do? What are they hoping to learn or get from this?</p>
<h4>Phase 3: The Speaker</h4>
<p>Quick — your name, title, and one sentence of credibility. What makes you the person to give this talk?</p>
<h4>Phase 4: The Work</h4>
<p>What did you actually do? I need real details: What was built or created? How long did it take? What results do you have so far? What did it cost? What went wrong?</p>
<h4>Phase 5: The Angle</h4>
<p>Based on the gathered information, AI Presentation Maker suggests 3-5 presentation angles. You can choose one or describe your own.</p>
<h4>Phase 6: Resources &#038; CTA</h4>
<p>Any resources to include? Links to share? Coupon codes or special offers? What&#8217;s the ONE thing you want people to do after this talk?</p>
<h3>2. Deck Generation</h3>
<p>After the interview, AI Presentation Maker generates a presentation deck based on the selected angle and gathered data. The deck includes slides with speaker notes, factual validation, and real cost breakdowns.</p>
