<h2>Success Metrics for Product Promotion</h2>
<p>When evaluating campaigns, focus on:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Conversion Rate</strong>: The percentage of viewers who purchase after watching</li>
<li><strong>Sales per Video</strong>: The number of units or revenue generated from one post</li>
<li><strong>Click-Through Rate</strong>: How many people follow links to your product</li>
<li><strong>Customer Lifetime Value</strong>: Long-term impact of the promotion on repeat purchases</li>
<li><strong>Return on Ad Spend</strong>: How much revenue each dollar of payment generates</li>
</ul>
