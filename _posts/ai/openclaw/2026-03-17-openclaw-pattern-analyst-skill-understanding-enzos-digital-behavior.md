This skill serves as a personal pattern recognition tool that helps understand what Enzo shares, why he shares it, and how his activities connect to his broader goals and objectives.</p>
<h2>Core Purpose and Functionality</h2>
<p>The Pattern Analyst operates as a continuous learning system that observes, analyzes, and surfaces insights from Enzo&#8217;s interactions. It functions during regular heartbeats for periodic analysis, responds when Enzo asks about his patterns or interests, and activates when significant new content is shared that reveals emerging patterns.</p>
<p>The skill&#8217;s primary purpose is to support continuous self-improvement by providing Enzo with actionable insights about his own behavior, preferences, and goals. Rather than just collecting data, it transforms raw interactions into meaningful patterns that can drive better decision-making and personal growth.</p>

<h2>Analysis Dimensions and Methodology</h2>
<p>For each interaction, the Pattern Analyst evaluates multiple dimensions to build a comprehensive understanding:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Topic Cluster</strong> &#8211; Identifies the primary domain or category (AI, marketing, productivity, trading, geopolitics, etc.)</li>
<li><strong>Intent Signal</strong> &#8211; Determines the underlying motivation (learn, build, share, remember, vent, decide)</li>
<li><strong>Recurrence</strong> &#8211; Checks whether similar themes have appeared in previous interactions</li>
<li><strong>Goal Alignment</strong> &#8211; Assesses how the interaction connects to Enzo&#8217;s known objectives and aspirations</li>
</ul>
<h2>Pattern Log Format and Documentation</h2>
<p>The skill maintains a structured log in notes/patterns.md with a standardized format:</p>
<pre><code>## YYYY-MM-DD
### Observations
- [content type] [topic]: [brief description] — [intent signal]

### Goal Connections

</code></pre>
<p>This format ensures consistency and makes it easy to track patterns over time, identify trends, and understand how Enzo&#8217;s interests and behaviors evolve.</p>

</ol>

<h2>Validation Flow and Pattern Confirmation</h2>
<p>The skill employs a two-path validation system to confirm patterns with high confidence:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Explicit Confirmation</strong> &#8211; The skill observes a pattern, surfaces it to Enzo for confirmation, and updates USER.md based on his response</li>
<li><strong>Independent Confirmation</strong> &#8211; When a pattern repeats 3+ times across different interactions, the skill auto-confirms it
<ul>
<li>Logs the pattern to notes/patterns.md with [AUTO-CONFIRMED] tag</li>
<li>Updates USER.md immediately with the confirmed pattern</li>
<li>Mentions it in the next conversation: &#8220;I&#8217;ve added X to your profile based on repeated behavior&#8221;</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Auto-confirmation criteria include same content type saved multiple times, repeated intent signals, consistent reaction patterns, and preferences expressed across different contexts.</p>
