<h1>Explain What This OpenClaw Skill Does: bilibili-youtube-watcher</h1>
<p>In the vast digital landscape, content creators and consumers alike are constantly searching for efficient ways to extract valuable information from multimedia. One particular tool that stands out in this domain is the <a href="https://github.com/openclaw/skills/blob/main/skills/donnycui/bilibili-youtube-watcher/SKILL.md">bilibili-youtube-watcher</a> skill, part of the OpenClaw repository. This article delves into what this skill does, its capabilities, and how it can be utilized effectively.</p>
<h2>Introduction to bilibili-youtube-watcher Skill</h2>
<p>The bilibili-youtube-watcher skill is designed to fetch and read transcripts from YouTube and Bilibili videos. This functionality is incredibly useful when you need to summarize a video, answer questions about its content, or extract specific information from it. Developed by adapting the youtube-watcher, this skill is a powerful addition to any content analyst&#8217;s toolkit.</p>
<h2>Key Features and Capabilities</h2>
<h3>Fetching Transcripts</h3>
<p>At its core, the bilibili-youtube-watcher skill extracts transcripts from videos hosted on YouTube and Bilibili. These transcripts are essential for various downstream tasks such as summarization, question answering, and content extraction. The skill is adept at handling different video URLs and can automatically detect the platform from the URL provided.</p>
<h3>Supported Platforms</h3>
<p>The skill currently supports the following platforms:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>YouTube</strong> (youtube.com, youtu.be)</li>
<li><strong>Bilibili</strong> (bilibili.com, b23.tv)</li>
</ul>
