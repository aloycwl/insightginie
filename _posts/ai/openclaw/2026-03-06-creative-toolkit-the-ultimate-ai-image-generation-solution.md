Creative Toolkit: The Ultimate AI Image Generation Solution
===========================================================

In the rapidly evolving world of AI-generated art, Creative Toolkit stands out as a comprehensive solution for generating high-quality images from text prompts. This powerful tool integrates multiple AI image generation providers, offers advanced prompt enhancement capabilities, and provides access to a curated gallery of over 1,300 professional prompts. What is Creative Toolkit?
-------------------------

Creative Toolkit is an AI image generation platform that acts as a unified interface for multiple image generation providers. It supports Nanobanana Pro, GPT Image, Seedream, and local ComfyUI workflows, allowing users to generate images from text prompts while routing requests to the most suitable provider.

How Creative Toolkit Works
--------------------------

### Multi-Provider Routing

Creative Toolkit's core functionality is its ability to route image generation requests to the most appropriate provider based on availability, cost, and quality considerations. The system automatically detects and prioritizes providers in this order: MeiGen > ComfyUI > OpenAI-compatible endpoints.

### Prompt Enhancement

One of Creative Toolkit's most valuable features is its prompt enhancement capability. This tool takes a simple, brief idea and expands it into a detailed, production-ready prompt with specific directions for lighting, composition, materials, and style. The system supports three enhancement styles:

* **Realistic:** Focuses on camera settings, lighting, and material textures (ideal for product photos, portraits, and architecture)
* **Anime:** Emphasizes character details and visual composition (perfect for anime illustrations and character design)
* **Illustration:** Highlights art medium, color palette, and brush texture (great for concept art and digital paintings)

### Curated Prompt Gallery

Creative Toolkit provides access to a gallery of over 1,300 curated prompts, making it easy to find inspiration for your projects. The gallery supports semantic search, allowing you to find conceptually similar prompts even if they don't share exact keywords. You can also browse by category and view high-resolution images with their full prompts.

Key Features and Tools
----------------------

Creative Toolkit offers a wide range of tools to assist with image generation and workflow management:

### Free Tools (No API Key Required)

* **search\_gallery:** Semantic search across AI image prompts with category filtering
* **get\_inspiration:** Retrieve full prompts and high-res images from gallery entries
* **enhance\_prompt:** Expand brief ideas into detailed, style-aware prompts
* **list\_models:** View all available models across configured providers

### Provider-Required Tools

* **generate\_image:** Create images from text prompts with aspect ratio, seed, and reference image support
* **upload\_reference\_image:** Compress and upload local images for use as style references
* **comfyui\_workflow:** Manage ComfyUI workflow templates and parameters
