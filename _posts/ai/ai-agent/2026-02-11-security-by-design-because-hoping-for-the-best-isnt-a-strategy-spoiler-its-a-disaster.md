Oh, look—another day, another headline about some poor soul who thought *security by design* was just a fancy buzzword their CTO threw around to sound smart. Spoiler alert: it's not. If you're still treating security like an afterthought, congratulations! You've officially turned your agent execution and data into a buffet for cybercriminals. Bon appétit.

But hey, why bother safeguarding your systems when you can just cross your fingers and pray? Oh wait, that's not how this works. Security by design a luxury—it's the bare minimum. And if you're not baking it into your architecture from day one, you're basically handing hackers the keys to your kingdom while whispering, “Please don't break anything.” Good luck with that.
