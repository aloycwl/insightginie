The education sector is undergoing a profound transformation, driven by advances in technology. Among these innovations, **Generative AI (GAI)** is standing out as a game-changer, offering new ways to deliver personalized learning experiences to students of all ages. With the ability to create customized content, generate real-time feedback, and adapt to individual learning styles, GAI is reshaping the future of education. This article explores how GAI is revolutionizing education and the ways it enhances personalized learning, offering a glimpse into the future of teaching and learning.
