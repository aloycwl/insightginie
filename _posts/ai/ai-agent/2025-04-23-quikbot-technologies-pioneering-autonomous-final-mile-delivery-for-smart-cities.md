<p>In the rapidly evolving landscape of urban logistics, QuikBot Technologies has emerged as a trailblazer, introducing the world&#8217;s first Autonomous Final-Mile Delivery Platform-as-a-Service (AFMD PaaS) . Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Singapore, the company is redefining last-mile delivery by integrating advanced robotics, artificial intelligence, and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies to create efficient, sustainable, and smart delivery solutions.</p>



<p>QuikBot&#8217;s AFMD platform is designed to address the complexities of urban logistics by automating the final segment of the delivery process. By deploying autonomous delivery robots within urban infrastructures, the company aims to streamline deliveries, reduce carbon emissions, and alleviate traffic congestion . These electric-powered robots are equipped with AI-driven route optimization capabilities, ensuring timely and efficient deliveries while minimizing environmental impact.​<br>+2</p>



<p>The company&#8217;s commitment to sustainability is evident in its operations. By reducing reliance on traditional delivery vehicles, QuikBot not only cuts down on greenhouse gas emissions but also supports local businesses through efficient last-mile solutions . Their approach aligns with Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles, emphasizing eco-friendly practices and community engagement.</p>



<p>QuikBot&#8217;s innovative solutions have garnered attention and partnerships from major logistics players, including FedEx, SingPost, and DHL Express. The company has successfully deployed its services in prominent Singaporean locations such as South Beach Development, Suntec City, Mapletree Business City, One North, and Punggol Digital District . These deployments showcase the practical applications of their technology in real-world settings, highlighting improvements in delivery efficiency and reductions in operational costs.​</p>



<p>Looking beyond Singapore, QuikBot has ambitious plans for international expansion. The company is set to launch its services in cities like Tokyo, Osaka, Fukuoka, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Doha, Seoul, Busan, Sydney, Melbourne, and Canberra . Each expansion is guided by a commitment to ESG principles, ensuring that their growth contributes positively to the communities and environments they operate in.​</p>



<p>In addition to its technological advancements, QuikBot places a strong emphasis on corporate governance and leadership. The recent appointment of seasoned executives to its global leadership team underscores the company&#8217;s dedication to robust corporate governance and international expansion . This strategic move aims to enhance board oversight, investor confidence, and institutional readiness as QuikBot accelerates commercialization and scales its global operations.​</p>
