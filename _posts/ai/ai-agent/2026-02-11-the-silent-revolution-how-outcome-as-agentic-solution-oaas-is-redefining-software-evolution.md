Imagine a world where software doesn't just respond to commands but anticipates needs, where lines of code evolve into living entities that grow alongside human ambition. This is not the plot of a sci-fi novel—it's the quiet revolution unfolding in the heart of modern technology. At its core lies the **role of Outcome-as-Agentic-Solution (OaAS)**, a paradigm shift that is transforming how we conceive, build, and interact with software. No longer a passive tool, software is becoming an active participant in problem-solving, blurring the boundaries between creator and creation.
