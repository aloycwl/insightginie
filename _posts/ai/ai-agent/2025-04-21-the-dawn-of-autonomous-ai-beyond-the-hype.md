While this futuristic vision may still be a ways off, autonomous AI systems are already quietly transforming our world. From self-driving cars to intelligent factory robots, these systems are capable of operating independently, learning from their surroundings, and making decisions without direct human intervention. As such, it is crucial to understand the intricacies of autonomous AI beyond the common misconceptions to navigate the potential of this transformative technology.*
