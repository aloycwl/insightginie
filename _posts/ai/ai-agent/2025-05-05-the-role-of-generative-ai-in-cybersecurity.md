In today's interconnected world, **cybersecurity** has become more critical than ever. As cyber threats evolve in sophistication and scale, traditional defense mechanisms are increasingly struggling to keep up. Enter **Generative AI (GAI)**—a transformative technology that holds the potential to redefine cybersecurity practices. By utilizing machine learning models that can adapt and generate new strategies, GAI is enhancing everything from threat detection to automated incident response, improving both speed and accuracy in defending against cyberattacks. This guide explores how generative AI is shaping the future of cybersecurity, its key features, and how industries can effectively harness it to enhance their defenses.
