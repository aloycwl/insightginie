The healthcare industry is experiencing a paradigm shift—driven by the rise of **Generative AI (GAI)**. No longer limited to back-office analytics or patient scheduling, GAI is now making bold strides in **clinical decision support, diagnostics, personalized medicine, and administrative efficiency**. As providers face increasing pressure to deliver higher-quality care at lower costs, GAI emerges as a game-changing ally, bridging the gap between massive datasets and actionable insights. This guide explores how generative AI is reshaping the healthcare landscape and what it means for professionals, patients, and organizations alike.
