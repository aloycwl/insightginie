In today's digital-first economy, consumers expect more than just products—they demand **relevant, seamless, and personalized experiences**. Whether browsing an online store or stepping into a retail outlet, personalization has become a key differentiator in how brands attract, engage, and retain customers. **Retail and e-commerce personalization** leverages data, AI, and behavioral insights to deliver customized content, product recommendations, pricing, and communication. As competition intensifies and customer loyalty becomes harder to earn, investing in personalization is no longer optional—it's essential for growth.
