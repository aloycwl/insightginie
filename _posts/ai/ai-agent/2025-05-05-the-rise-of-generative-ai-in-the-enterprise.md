In today's rapidly evolving business landscape, companies are under constant pressure to innovate, reduce costs, and boost operational efficiency. Enter **Generative AI (GAI)**—a transformative force that is reshaping the enterprise productivity paradigm. Unlike traditional automation, GAI goes beyond rule-based task execution to generate new content, insights, and decisions based on learned patterns. From drafting reports to automating customer support and summarizing internal data, generative AI is ushering in a new age of **intelligent enterprise automation**.
