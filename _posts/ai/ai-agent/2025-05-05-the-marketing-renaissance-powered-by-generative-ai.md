The digital marketing landscape is undergoing a seismic shift—and at the heart of it is **Generative AI (GAI)**. No longer confined to futuristic hype, GAI is now a practical tool driving real-world innovation in **marketing and advertising**. From automating content creation to optimizing ad targeting and crafting hyper-personalized campaigns, GAI empowers marketers to operate with speed, precision, and creativity never seen before. As customer expectations rise and competition intensifies, businesses leveraging GAI are gaining a significant edge in reaching and resonating with their audiences.
