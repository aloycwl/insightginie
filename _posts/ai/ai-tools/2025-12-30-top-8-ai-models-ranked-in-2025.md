<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>1. Gemini 3</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Reasoning mode solves hard math and science tasks</li>

<li>Native video understanding processes huge files instantly</li>

<li>Huge context window reads whole books and codebases</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Currently holds the highest ELO score on major benchmarks</li>

<li>Seamlessly integrates with Google Docs and Drive files</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Safety filters are often too cautious and preachy to users</li>

<li>UI feels cluttered with too many Google product add-ons</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>2. ChatGPT 5.1</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Deep reasoning engine plans ahead before answering tasks</li>

<li>Active memory retains your preferences across all chats</li>

<li>Native voice mode detects emotional shifts with zero lag</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Feels the most polished and human-like interaction today</li>

<li>Massive ecosystem of custom GPTs and third-party plugins</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Still suffers from laziness on very complex coding tasks</li>

<li>Most expensive monthly subscription in the top-tier plan</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>3. Claude 4.5</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Computer Use tool controls your mouse and keyboard inputs</li>

<li>Live coding window visualizes and edits code in real time</li>

<li>Project Context keeps relevant files active for deep work</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The absolute favorite model for software coding and logic</li>

<li>Best at avoiding hallucinations and the lowest rate of lying or errors</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Strict daily message limits apply even for paid subscribers</li>

<li>Slower text generation speed than Gemini or GPT-5 models</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>4. Perplexity</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Switches between GPT, Claude, and Gemini freely</li>

<li>Deep Research mode writes full reports with accurate citations</li>

<li>Knowledge Graph visually maps connections between topics</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The most trustworthy AI that cites every single source used</li>

<li>Effectively replaces the need for traditional Google Search</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Not designed for creative writing or storytelling requests</li>

<li>Struggles significantly if the web source is wrong</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>5. DeepSeek-V4</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Expert architecture uses less power to run efficiently</li>

<li>GPT-5 class performance at a fraction of the cost</li>

<li>Small enough to run locally on high-end consumer laptops</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Best value-for-money API for developers building AI tools</li>

<li>Fully open weights allow you to own and modify the system</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>English language nuance is slightly behind US-based models</li>

<li>Data privacy concerns due to servers located in China</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>6. Grok 4</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Analyzes visual data and video feeds faster than anyone</li>

<li>Real-time access to tweets and breaking news data streams</li>

<li>Generates personalized and photorealistic images via Flux</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Unbeatable for tracking live trends and real-time politics</li>

<li>Latest filtered AI allows for edgy and controversial topics</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Requires premium subscription to access the capability</li>

<li>Personality can be rude or unexpected</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>7. Copilot</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Lives directly inside Word, Excel, and PowerPoint apps</li>

<li>Pages features let you turn meetings into notes</li>

<li>Auto-joins Teams meetings to write minutes and summaries</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Unbeatable utility for corporate and Office 365 workflows</li>

<li>Enterprise data protection guarantees your data stays private</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Heavy and aggressive filtering on non-work related topics</li>

<li>Can feel bloated and slow running inside Office apps</li>
</ul>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>8. Meta AI</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Imagine me feature puts your face into generated images</li>

<li>Llama Stack allows the model to run locally on phones</li>

<li>Built directly into WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook chats</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Pros</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Industry standard for high-performance open-source AI</li>

<li>Completely free to use and easily accessible in your apps</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Cons</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Difficult to export your work or chat history out of apps</li>

<li>Shorter context memory compared to Gemini or Claude</li>
</ul>
