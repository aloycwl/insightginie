### **1. Gemini 3**

**Key Features**

* Reasoning mode solves hard math and science tasks
* Native video understanding processes huge files instantly
* Huge context window reads whole books and codebases

**Pros**

* Currently holds the highest ELO score on major benchmarks
* Seamlessly integrates with Google Docs and Drive files

**Cons**

* Safety filters are often too cautious and preachy to users
* UI feels cluttered with too many Google product add-ons

---

### **2. ChatGPT 5.1**

**Key Features**

* Deep reasoning engine plans ahead before answering tasks
* Active memory retains your preferences across all chats
* Native voice mode detects emotional shifts with zero lag

**Pros**

* Feels the most polished and human-like interaction today
* Massive ecosystem of custom GPTs and third-party plugins

**Cons**

* Still suffers from laziness on very complex coding tasks
* Most expensive monthly subscription in the top-tier plan

---

### **3. Claude 4.5**

**Key Features**

* Computer Use tool controls your mouse and keyboard inputs
* Live coding window visualizes and edits code in real time
* Project Context keeps relevant files active for deep work

**Pros**

* The absolute favorite model for software coding and logic
* Best at avoiding hallucinations and the lowest rate of lying or errors

**Cons**

* Strict daily message limits apply even for paid subscribers
* Slower text generation speed than Gemini or GPT-5 models

---

### **4. Perplexity**

**Key Features**

* Switches between GPT, Claude, and Gemini freely
* Deep Research mode writes full reports with accurate citations
* Knowledge Graph visually maps connections between topics

**Pros**

* The most trustworthy AI that cites every single source used
* Effectively replaces the need for traditional Google Search

**Cons**

* Not designed for creative writing or storytelling requests
* Struggles significantly if the web source is wrong

---

### **5. DeepSeek-V4**

**Key Features**

* Expert architecture uses less power to run efficiently
* GPT-5 class performance at a fraction of the cost
* Small enough to run locally on high-end consumer laptops

**Pros**

* Best value-for-money API for developers building AI tools
* Fully open weights allow you to own and modify the system

**Cons**

* English language nuance is slightly behind US-based models
* Data privacy concerns due to servers located in China

---

### **6. Grok 4**

**Key Features**

* Analyzes visual data and video feeds faster than anyone
* Real-time access to tweets and breaking news data streams
* Generates personalized and photorealistic images via Flux

**Pros**

* Unbeatable for tracking live trends and real-time politics
* Latest filtered AI allows for edgy and controversial topics

**Cons**

* Requires premium subscription to access the capability
* Personality can be rude or unexpected

---

### **7. Copilot**

**Key Features**

* Lives directly inside Word, Excel, and PowerPoint apps
* Pages features let you turn meetings into notes
* Auto-joins Teams meetings to write minutes and summaries

**Pros**

* Unbeatable utility for corporate and Office 365 workflows
* Enterprise data protection guarantees your data stays private

**Cons**

* Heavy and aggressive filtering on non-work related topics
* Can feel bloated and slow running inside Office apps

---

### **8. Meta AI**

**Key Features**

* Imagine me feature puts your face into generated images
* Llama Stack allows the model to run locally on phones
* Built directly into WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook chats

**Pros**

* Industry standard for high-performance open-source AI
* Completely free to use and easily accessible in your apps

**Cons**

* Difficult to export your work or chat history out of apps
* Shorter context memory compared to Gemini or Claude
