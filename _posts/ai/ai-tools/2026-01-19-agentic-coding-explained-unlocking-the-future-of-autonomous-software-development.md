Agentic Coding Explained: Unlocking the Future of Autonomous Software Development
=================================================================================

In the rapidly evolving landscape of technology, a new paradigm is emerging that promises to revolutionize how we conceive, build, and interact with software. This paradigm is known as **Agentic Coding**. Far from the traditional, step-by-step instructions we're accustomed to, agentic coding empowers software systems with a degree of autonomy, allowing them to pursue goals, adapt to changing environments, and even learn from their experiences. But what exactly is agentic coding, and why is it considered the next frontier in software development?
