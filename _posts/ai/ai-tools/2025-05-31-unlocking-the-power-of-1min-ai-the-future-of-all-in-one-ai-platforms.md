<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What is 1min.AI?</h2>



<p>Launched in 2023, 1min.AI is a comprehensive AI aggregator platform that integrates various large language models (LLMs) and generative AI tools into one cohesive system. Instead of juggling multiple subscriptions and platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Midjourney, users can access these functionalities through 1min.AI&#8217;s unified dashboard. This consolidation simplifies the user experience and offers a more efficient approach to leveraging AI technologies. </p>
