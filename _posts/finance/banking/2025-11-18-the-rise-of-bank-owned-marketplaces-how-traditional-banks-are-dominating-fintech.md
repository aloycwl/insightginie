The financial world is undergoing a seismic shift, driven by technological advancements and evolving customer expectations. For decades, traditional banks operated within a clearly defined perimeter, offering a suite of core services. However, the advent of fintech startups challenged this status quo, introducing agility, specialization, and user-centric design. In response, a fascinating trend has emerged: banks are not just competing with fintechs; they are becoming fintechs themselves, launching sophisticated **bank-owned marketplaces** and digital platforms.
