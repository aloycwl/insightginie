<h2>Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)</h2>
<h3>What is the cost of Chime Prime?</h3>
<p>Chime Prime is not a paid subscription in the traditional sense. Instead, it is a status tier unlocked by meeting specific activity criteria, such as maintaining a certain volume of direct deposits or transaction frequency. This ensures that the benefits are accessible to active users without a rigid monthly fee.</p>
<h3>Can I keep my current Chime account if I qualify for Prime?</h3>
<p>Yes. When you qualify for Chime Prime, your existing account is upgraded seamlessly. You do not need to open a new account or transfer funds. The premium features and benefits will automatically activate within your current app interface.</p>
<h3>Does Chime Prime offer a credit card?</h3>
<p>Chime offers the Chime Credit Builder Secured Visa® Credit Card, which helps users build credit without interest or annual fees. Chime Prime members may receive enhanced benefits related to this card, such as higher reporting frequency or exclusive rewards categories, further integrating credit building into their premium experience.</p>
<h3>Is my money safe with Chime?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Chime is a financial technology company, not a bank. Banking services are provided by The Bancorp Bank, N.A. or Stride Bank, N.A., Members FDIC. This means your funds are FDIC-insured up to the maximum limit allowed by law, offering the same security as traditional banks.</p>
<h3>How do I know if I qualify for Chime Prime?</h3>
<p>Eligibility is typically determined by your account activity within the Chime app. Factors often include the amount of direct deposit received monthly and the frequency of debit card usage. Users can check their eligibility status directly in the &#8220;Settings&#8221; or &#8220;Profile&#8221; section of the Chime mobile application.</p>
