The financial landscape, once characterized by staid brick-and-mortar institutions, is experiencing a seismic shift. At the epicenter of this transformation is **Fintech** – a portmanteau of financial technology – a dynamic force that is not just disrupting but fundamentally redefining the role of traditional banks. For decades, established banks enjoyed unparalleled market dominance, built on trust, extensive branch networks, and a comprehensive suite of services. However, the advent of agile, tech-driven startups has challenged this status quo, pushing traditional players to innovate or risk obsolescence. This article delves into the profound impact of Fintech on traditional banking, exploring key areas of transformation, the challenges faced, and the strategic pathways for adaptation and growth.
