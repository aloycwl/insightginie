The financial landscape is in constant flux, driven by technological advancements and evolving customer expectations. In this era of rapid digital transformation, traditional banks are no longer just observers; many have become active participants in the fintech revolution. One of the most powerful mechanisms for this engagement is **Corporate Venture Capital (CVC)**. Bank sponsorship of fintech startups through CVC isn't just about investing capital; it's a strategic imperative that's redefining banking, fostering innovation, and shaping the future of financial services.
