In an increasingly interconnected world, the threats posed by terrorism remain a persistent and evolving challenge. While much attention is rightly given to preventing attacks, a silent, relentless war is being waged behind the scenes: the fight to cut off the financial lifelines that fuel these destructive acts. This is the realm of **Countering the Financing of Terrorism (CFT)**, a critical component of global security that often operates out of the public eye but impacts every facet of our financial systems.

Understanding CFT isn't just for financial professionals; it's crucial for anyone seeking to grasp the complex mechanisms nations employ to safeguard peace and protect their economies. This deep dive will unravel what CFT truly means, why it's indispensable, how terror groups fund their operations, and the global efforts underway to stop them.
