In today's complex global economy, businesses and financial institutions constantly navigate a landscape riddled with risks. Among the most insidious threats are shell companies – entities that exist on paper but typically have no significant assets, employees, or legitimate business operations. While not all shell companies are illegal, they are frequently exploited for illicit purposes, including money laundering, tax evasion, fraud, and financing terrorism. Understanding how to detect shell companies is not just good practice; it's an essential shield for your organization's integrity and financial security.
