In today's fast-paced digital economy, the customer onboarding process is your business's front door. It's the critical first impression, the gateway to new relationships, and unfortunately, often the primary target for sophisticated fraudsters. While a smooth, rapid onboarding experience is paramount for customer acquisition, neglecting robust fraud detection during this phase can lead to devastating financial losses, severe reputational damage, and crippling regulatory penalties. This guide will equip you with essential strategies and insights to fortify your defenses and ensure only legitimate customers walk through your digital doors.
