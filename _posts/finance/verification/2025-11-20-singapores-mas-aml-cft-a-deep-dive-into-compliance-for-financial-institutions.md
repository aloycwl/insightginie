Singapore, a global financial hub, prides itself on a robust regulatory framework designed to maintain its integrity and combat financial crime. At the heart of this framework lie the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Countering the Financing of Terrorism (CFT) guidelines. For any financial institution operating within or interacting with Singapore's financial landscape, a thorough understanding and stringent adherence to these guidelines are not just regulatory obligations but foundational to sustainable operations and reputation.
