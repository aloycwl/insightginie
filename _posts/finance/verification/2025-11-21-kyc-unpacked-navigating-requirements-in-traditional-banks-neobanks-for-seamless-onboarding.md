In an increasingly digital world, the financial sector stands as a bedrock of trust and security. At the heart of maintaining this trust, while simultaneously combating financial crime, lies a critical process known as **Know Your Customer (KYC)**. Whether you're opening an account with a century-old institution or a cutting-edge digital-only bank, KYC requirements are an unavoidable and essential part of the journey. But how do these requirements differ, and where do they converge, especially when comparing traditional banks with their agile neobank counterparts?

This comprehensive guide will demystify KYC, exploring its fundamental principles, the specific challenges and approaches of traditional banking, and the innovative solutions employed by neobanks. Understanding these nuances is crucial not only for financial institutions striving for compliance but also for customers seeking a smoother, more secure onboarding experience.
