In an increasingly interconnected and digital world, businesses face a myriad of threats, not all of which come from external sources. While cybersecurity measures and external fraud prevention are paramount, a significant and often underestimated risk lurks closer to home: the insider threat. This is where **Know Your Employee (KYE)** comes into play, an essential framework designed to safeguard organizations from internal risks, foster a secure work environment, and ensure regulatory compliance.

Much like its more famous cousin, Know Your Customer (KYC), which focuses on understanding clients to prevent financial crime, KYE applies a similar level of due diligence to an organization's most valuable — and potentially vulnerable — asset: its people. But what exactly does KYE entail, and why has it become an indispensable part of modern corporate governance?
