Technical Challenges and Limitations
------------------------------------

Implementing a comprehensive age verification system like Discord's comes with its own set of technical challenges and limitations.

### Accuracy of Age Estimation

While facial recognition technology has advanced significantly, accurately estimating a person's age based solely on their facial features remains a challenging task. Factors such as lighting, facial expressions, and image quality can affect the accuracy of age estimation.

### Spoofing and Circumvention

Despite the 269 hidden checks, there is always a risk of users attempting to spoof or circumvent the age verification system. This could involve using photos of other people, deepfake technology, or other methods to trick the system.

### Scalability and Performance

Processing facial recognition and database matching for millions of users requires significant computational resources. Discord must ensure that its age verification system can scale effectively without compromising performance or user experience.
