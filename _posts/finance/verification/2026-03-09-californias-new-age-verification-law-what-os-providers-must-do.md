California's New Age Verification Law: What OS Providers Must Do
================================================================

The digital landscape is undergoing a profound transformation, particularly regarding the privacy, safety, and regulation of minors online. As state governments across the United States grow increasingly concerned about the influence of social media and digital environments on children, California has stepped to the forefront with ambitious legislative efforts. A significant, yet controversial, upcoming California law aims to mandate that operating system (OS) providers enforce basic, mandatory age verification for all users. This initiative represents a seismic shift in how tech giants—from mobile software developers to computer OS creators—will have to architect their platforms moving forward.
