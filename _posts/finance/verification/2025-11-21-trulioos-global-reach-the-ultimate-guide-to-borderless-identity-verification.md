---------------------------------------------------------

* **Customer Expectation for Speed and Convenience:** Users expect instant onboarding and frictionless experiences, making lengthy manual verification processes unacceptable.

These challenges underscore the critical need for an identity verification partner that can offer both broad global reach and deep localized intelligence.
