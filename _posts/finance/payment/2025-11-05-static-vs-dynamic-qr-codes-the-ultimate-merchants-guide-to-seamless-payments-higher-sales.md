In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, mobile payments are no longer a novelty but a necessity. Among the myriad options, QR code payments have emerged as a powerful, cost-effective solution for businesses of all sizes. From bustling market stalls to high-street retailers, QR codes offer a quick, convenient, and secure way for customers to pay. But for merchants looking to integrate this technology, a crucial distinction often arises: **static vs. dynamic QR codes**. Understanding their differences, respective merchant flows, and ideal use cases is paramount to optimizing your payment system, enhancing customer experience, and ultimately driving sales.
