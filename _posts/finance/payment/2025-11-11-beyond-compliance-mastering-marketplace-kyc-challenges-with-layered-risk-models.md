The digital marketplace has revolutionized how we buy and sell, connecting millions globally. From e-commerce giants to niche service platforms, these ecosystems thrive on trust and seamless transactions. However, beneath the surface of convenience lies a formidable challenge: Know Your Customer (KYC) compliance. As marketplaces grow in scale and complexity, so do the risks of fraud, money laundering, and illicit activities. A one-size-fits-all approach to KYC is no longer sufficient; the modern marketplace demands a sophisticated, adaptive strategy. Enter the [layered risk model](#layered-risk-models) – a strategic imperative for robust security, compliance, and sustained growth.
