In our increasingly interconnected world, money moves across borders at an astonishing pace. From international trade settlements to individual remittances, the global financial system relies heavily on a complex yet robust network that ensures these transactions are smooth, secure, and efficient. At the heart of this intricate system lies **correspondent banking**, a fundamental practice powered by specialized accounts known as **nostro and vostro accounts**, all culminating in the critical process of **cross-border clearing**.
