The traditional 'lone wolf' approach to fraud detection and prevention is no longer sufficient. To truly combat this pervasive threat, a paradigm shift is required: one that embraces collective intelligence and collaborative defense. This is where **shared fraud databases** and **consortium approaches** emerge as game-changers, revolutionizing how we identify, prevent, and respond to fraudulent activities.
