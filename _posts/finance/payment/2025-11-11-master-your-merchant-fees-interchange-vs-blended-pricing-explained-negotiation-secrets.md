For many merchants, payment processing fees are a necessary evil – an unavoidable cost of doing business in a card-driven economy. Yet, these fees often feel like a black box, a complex jumble of percentages and surcharges that eat into your profits without clear explanation. The good news? You don't have to be a passive recipient of these charges. By understanding the two primary pricing models – **Interchange++ (IC++)** and **Blended Pricing** – and mastering the art of negotiation, you can significantly reduce your costs and boost your bottom line.
