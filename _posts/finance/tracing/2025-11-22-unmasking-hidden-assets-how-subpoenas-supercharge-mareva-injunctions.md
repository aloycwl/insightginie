In the intricate world of asset recovery and civil litigation, legal tools are often deployed individually to achieve specific objectives. However, true mastery lies in understanding how these tools can be combined synergistically to amplify their impact. Two such powerful instruments, the subpoena and the Mareva injunction (also known as a freezing order), often appear distinct in their primary functions. A subpoena compels disclosure of information or testimony, while a Mareva injunction freezes assets to prevent their dissipation. Yet, when wielded together, they form an formidable alliance, dramatically increasing the chances of identifying, securing, and ultimately recovering assets that might otherwise remain hidden or moved beyond reach.
