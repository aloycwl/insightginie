The Core Pillars of TRM Labs' Platform: Solutions for Every Challenge

TRM Labs offers a modular yet integrated platform, each component designed to address specific aspects of crypto risk management and compliance. Understanding these tools is key to appreciating the platform's comprehensive approach.

### TRM Forensics: Unraveling Complex Crypto Crime

*TRM Forensics* is the flagship investigative tool, providing a powerful interface for tracing funds across multiple blockchains and identifying illicit actors. It allows investigators to visualize transaction flows, identify clusters of addresses belonging to the same entity, and link on-chain activity to real-world identities when possible. Its capabilities include:

* **Graphical Transaction Visualization:** Intuitive maps show how funds move, making complex chains of transactions easy to follow.
* **Entity Recognition:** Identifying known illicit actors, darknet markets, ransomware groups, and sanctioned entities.
* **Cross-Chain Analysis:** Tracing funds that move between different blockchains, a common tactic for obfuscation.
* **Reporting Tools:** Generating comprehensive reports for regulatory filings or law enforcement referrals.

For law enforcement and financial intelligence units, TRM Forensics is an invaluable asset in the fight against crypto-enabled crime, significantly reducing the time and resources required for complex investigations.

### TRM Navigator: Proactive Risk Scoring for Virtual Assets

*TRM Navigator* provides real-time risk scoring for cryptocurrency addresses and transactions. This tool is essential for businesses that need to assess the risk profile of their customers or incoming funds before processing transactions. By integrating Navigator into their workflows, organizations can:

* **Automate Risk Assessments:** Instantly check addresses or transactions against TRM's vast database of illicit activity.
* **Identify High-Risk Entities:** Flag funds originating from or destined for known illicit sources, mixers, or gambling sites.
* **Tailor Risk Policies:** Customize risk thresholds and rules to align with internal compliance policies and regulatory requirements.

Navigator acts as a proactive shield, preventing risky transactions from entering an organization's ecosystem and significantly bolstering AML compliance efforts.

As governments globally expand sanctions lists to include virtual asset addresses, managing compliance becomes a moving target. *TRM Sanctions* offers automated, real-time screening against global sanctions lists, including those from OFAC, EU, UN, and others. * **Real-Time Sanctions Screening:** Instantaneously check crypto addresses against continuously updated sanctions lists.
* **Proactive Alerts:** Receive notifications if new sanctions are imposed on addresses interacting with your platform.

TRM Sanctions is vital for any entity operating in the crypto space, helping them navigate the complex and ever-changing landscape of international sanctions.

For businesses onboarding new users or managing customer wallets, ensuring that these wallets are not associated with illicit activities is paramount. *TRM Wallet Screening* provides a robust solution for vetting virtual asset wallets at the point of creation or during ongoing monitoring.
