The digital revolution has swept across finance, transforming everything from currencies to company shares. Now, it's setting its sights on the oldest forms of wealth: commodities. Imagine owning a fraction of a barrel of oil, a precise gram of silver, or a share in a harvest of wheat, all managed and traded with the speed and transparency of blockchain technology. This isn't a futuristic fantasy; it's the rapidly emerging reality of **tokenized commodities** and **fungible commodity tokens**.

In an era where traditional investment barriers are crumbling, tokenization offers an unprecedented bridge between the tangible world of physical assets and the digital frontier of blockchain. This guide will explore how silver, oil, and agricultural products are being transformed into accessible, liquid, and transparent digital assets, reshaping how we invest in and interact with the foundational elements of our global economy.
