<p>RWAs represent the tokenization of tangible assets—such as treasury bills, private credit, and real estate—bringing reliable, income-generating instruments on-chain. YieldNest’s ynRWAx acts as a bridge, tokenizing these off-chain credit yields and wrapping them into an interoperable DeFi primitive.</p>
