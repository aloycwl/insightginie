<h2>Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)</h2>
<h3>What is Real-World Asset (RWA) Tokenization?</h3>
<p>RWA tokenization is the process of converting rights to a physical or traditional financial asset (like real estate, gold, or bonds) into a digital token on a blockchain. This allows for fractional ownership, easier transferability, and increased liquidity.</p>
<h3>How does AG META ensure the safety of tokenized assets?</h3>
<p>AG META partners with regulated custodians and legal entities to hold the underlying physical assets. Additionally, smart contracts are audited by third-party security firms, and the platform adheres to strict KYC/AML regulations to prevent fraud and ensure compliance.</p>
<h3>Can I sell my tokenized assets anytime?</h3>
<p>Yes, one of the primary benefits of AG META&#8217;s platform is enhanced liquidity. Unlike traditional assets that may take weeks to sell, tokenized assets can typically be traded on secondary markets 24/7, subject to market availability.</p>
<h3>Do I need cryptocurrency to invest on AG META?</h3>
<p>No. AG META has designed its platform to be user-friendly for non-crypto natives. Users can often purchase tokenized assets using fiat currency through integrated on-ramps, with the platform handling the conversion to blockchain tokens seamlessly.</p>
<h3>What are the risks involved in RWA investing?</h3>
<p>While RWA tokens offer stability, they are not risk-free. Risks include market volatility of the underlying asset, regulatory changes, smart contract vulnerabilities, and liquidity risks if there are no buyers on the secondary market. Investors should always conduct their own research.</p>
