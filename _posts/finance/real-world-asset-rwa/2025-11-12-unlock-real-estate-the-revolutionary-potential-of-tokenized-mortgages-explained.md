The world of finance is in constant evolution, driven by technological advancements that redefine traditional assets and investment strategies. One of the most exciting and disruptive innovations emerging from the convergence of blockchain technology and real estate is the concept of **tokenized mortgages**, often referred to as mortgage-backed tokens. Imagine owning a slice of a mortgage pool, with all its associated returns, as a digital asset on a blockchain. This isn't science fiction; it's the dawning reality of onchain real estate investment, promising to democratize access, enhance liquidity, and transform the way we think about property finance.

For centuries, real estate investment has been characterized by high entry barriers, illiquidity, and complex intermediary processes. Mortgages, while foundational to property ownership, have remained largely within traditional financial silos. Tokenization, however, is poised to shatter these paradigms, offering a transparent, efficient, and globally accessible alternative. But what exactly are tokenized mortgages, and how do they work to create a new era of investment?
