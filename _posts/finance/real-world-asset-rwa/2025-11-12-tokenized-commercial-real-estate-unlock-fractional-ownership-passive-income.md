The world of commercial real estate (CRE) has long been a domain reserved for institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals, characterized by high entry barriers, illiquidity, and complex transaction processes. However, a revolutionary shift is underway, propelled by blockchain technology: **tokenized commercial real estate**. This innovation is democratizing access to lucrative CRE investments, transforming how we buy, sell, and manage property assets. By breaking down large, indivisible properties into digital tokens, fractional ownership becomes a reality for a global pool of investors, promising a future of enhanced liquidity, transparency, and accessibility.

In this comprehensive guide, we'll delve into what tokenized CRE entails, how it works, its myriad benefits, the challenges it faces, and what the future holds for this exciting frontier in real estate investment. Prepare to explore how blockchain is reshaping the very foundations of property ownership and wealth creation.
