<h2>What Is Real-World Asset Tokenization?</h2>
<p>Real-World Asset Tokenization refers to the process of representing ownership of physical or financial assets on a blockchain through digital tokens. Each token carries a verifiable claim on the underlying asset, enabling fractional ownership, faster settlement, and easier transferability. Unlike purely crypto-native assets, RWAs are tied to real economic value, which can reduce volatility and provide yield-generating opportunities.</p>
