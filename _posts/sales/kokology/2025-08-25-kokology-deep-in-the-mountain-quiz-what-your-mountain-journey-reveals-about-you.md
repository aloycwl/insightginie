<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>



<p>You journey to a mountain. Answer the following:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>You search for a rare stone. What is your impression of the mountain?</li>



<li>The sun has set and you haven’t found the stone. What will you do next?</li>



<li>You finally find the stone. What is its size, weight, and value?</li>



<li>What parting words will you say to the mountain?</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> What is the impression of your dad
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your initial impression of the mountain reflects your subconscious view of your father. A majestic or intimidating mountain may indicate respect or awe, while a calm or approachable mountain shows warmth and closeness.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How do you deal with life situations
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your actions when the sun sets reveal your coping style. Persisting shows determination, giving up reflects resignation, and adapting indicates flexibility in life’s challenges.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> What is your self-worth
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The size, weight, and value of the stone represent how you perceive your own abilities and value. A large, heavy, valuable stone reflects confidence, while a smaller or less valuable stone may suggest humility or self-doubt.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> What you always wanted to say to your dad
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your parting words to the mountain symbolize unspoken thoughts or emotions toward your father, representing admiration, gratitude, or unresolved feelings.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
