<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You are walking halfway and it suddenly starts pouring. What do you do?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Wait under a tree</li>

<li>Run as fast as you can to your destination</li>

<li>Share or buy an umbrella if someone has one</li>

<li>You always carry an umbrella</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. Wait under a tree</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You usually wait for someone to cool down first before arguing — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing patience in the rain reflects your tendency to avoid immediate confrontation and allow tempers to settle.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. Run as fast as you can</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You will argue till the end — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Acting decisively and persistently in a rainstorm mirrors your determination to pursue your point without backing down in conflicts.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. Share or buy an umbrella</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You will avoid all conflict and confrontation — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Finding a compromise or avoiding direct discomfort shows your preference for harmony and evading disputes.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. Always carry an umbrella</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You always have an excuse for anything — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Being fully prepared in advance reflects your careful planning and readiness to justify yourself in any situation.</li>
</ul>
