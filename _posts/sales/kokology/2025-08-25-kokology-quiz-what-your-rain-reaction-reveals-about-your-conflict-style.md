### **Quiz Questions**

You are walking halfway and it suddenly starts pouring. What do you do?

1. Wait under a tree
2. Run as fast as you can to your destination
3. Share or buy an umbrella if someone has one
4. You always carry an umbrella

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Wait under a tree**

* **Answer:** You usually wait for someone to cool down first before arguing — **Reasoning:** Choosing patience in the rain reflects your tendency to avoid immediate confrontation and allow tempers to settle.

**2. Run as fast as you can**

* **Answer:** You will argue till the end — **Reasoning:** Acting decisively and persistently in a rainstorm mirrors your determination to pursue your point without backing down in conflicts.

**3. Share or buy an umbrella**

* **Answer:** You will avoid all conflict and confrontation — **Reasoning:** Finding a compromise or avoiding direct discomfort shows your preference for harmony and evading disputes.

**4. Always carry an umbrella**

* **Answer:** You always have an excuse for anything — **Reasoning:** Being fully prepared in advance reflects your careful planning and readiness to justify yourself in any situation.
