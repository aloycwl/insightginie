### **Quiz Questions**

You are in the lobby of a medical clinic, waiting for your name to be called. A small boy is racing around the waiting area unattended and you warn him to be careful and slow down.

1. How does he react?
2. You saw a sickly person through a door opening, who is this person?
3. A doctor's aide comes out and calls your name, a very familiar face, who is this?
4. The doctor mutters to himself when looking at your chart, what do you do?
