<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You are in the lobby of a medical clinic, waiting for your name to be called. A small boy is racing around the waiting area unattended and you warn him to be careful and slow down.</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>How does he react?</li>

<li>You saw a sickly person through a door opening, who is this person?</li>

<li>A doctor’s aide comes out and calls your name, a very familiar face, who is this?</li>

<li>The doctor mutters to himself when looking at your chart, what do you do?</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. How does he react?</strong> – How you react to criticism</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your response to the boy shows whether you are patient, strict, or easily frustrated—mirroring how you handle criticism in real life.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. You saw a sickly person through a door opening, who is this person?</strong> – Someone you could never rely on during a crisis</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The sickly person represents vulnerability; your identification of them reflects who you subconsciously see as unreliable in stressful situations.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. A doctor’s aide comes out and calls your name, a very familiar face, who is this?</strong> – Someone you always look up to or fear</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The familiar aide symbolizes authority or admiration; your choice shows who commands respect or instills fear in your life.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. The doctor mutters to himself when looking at your chart, what do you do?</strong> – How you handle being bullied or teased</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your reaction to the doctor’s muttering reflects how you cope with judgment, criticism, or subtle intimidation from others.</li>
</ul>
