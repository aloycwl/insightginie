<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You do all your laundry by hand. Looking outside, the sky is filled with dark, threatening clouds. What goes through your mind?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>&#8220;You’ve got to be kidding, what am I going to wear?&#8221;</li>

<li>&#8220;Wait awhile to see if the weather will clear.&#8221;</li>

<li>&#8220;Not going to do laundry today.&#8221;</li>

<li>&#8220;Do laundry regardless.&#8221;</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. &#8220;You’ve got to be kidding, what am I going to wear?&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Stress level 80 – easily annoyed</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This choice shows a tendency to get frustrated quickly when unexpected challenges appear, reflecting high stress and low patience.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. &#8220;Wait awhile to see if the weather will clear.&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Stress level 50 – you keep balance of your stress level</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Waiting indicates measured patience and the ability to maintain emotional balance, showing moderate stress handling.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. &#8220;Not going to do laundry today.&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Stress level 0 – easy going</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Opting out reflects a relaxed, go-with-the-flow attitude and the ability to let minor obstacles roll off your back.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. &#8220;Do laundry regardless.&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Stress level 100 – you try to achieve the impossible</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Persisting despite obstacles indicates extreme determination but also a tendency to take on too much stress.</li>
</ul>
