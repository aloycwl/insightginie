<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You have decided to try skydiving and are ready to jump.</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>While waiting for your turn, what is going through your mind?</li>

<li>While jumping out of the doorway, what do you scream?</li>

<li>What did the instructor say to you when you’ve landed safely?</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. While waiting for your turn, what is going through your mind?</strong> – You are waiting for sex</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your thoughts during the anticipation phase reflect patience, eagerness, or desire—mirroring your level of anticipation in romantic or sexual situations.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. While jumping out of the doorway, what do you scream?</strong> – How you feel about sex</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your immediate reaction to the thrill shows whether you approach intimacy with excitement, hesitation, or fear—revealing your emotional attitude toward sexual experiences.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. What did the instructor say to you when you’ve landed safely?</strong> – What will your partner say after sex</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your perception of the instructor’s feedback mirrors how you anticipate or interpret your partner’s response after intimate moments, reflecting your insecurities or confidence in relationships.</li>
</ul>
