<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>You have a super lucky day and went to a local bakery to buy a jelly doughnut. You open it at home and find the jelly is missing inside. How would you react?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Take it back for a new one</li>

<li>Eat the doughnut still</li>

<li>Eat something else</li>

<li>Fill it with maple syrup</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. Take it back for a new one</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You played a leadership role among your friends.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Standing up for what you expect mirrors your natural tendency to lead and set standards in your circle.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. Eat the doughnut still</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You accept your fate and don’t make any fuss.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Willingness to accept things as they are reflects your easygoing nature and adaptability in social groups.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. Eat something else</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You make quick decisions among your friends.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Moving on fast shows decisiveness and a practical mindset that others rely on when choices need to be made.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. Fill it with maple syrup</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are innovative and people turn to you when things happen.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Finding a creative fix reveals your resourceful nature and the ability to provide unique solutions for your peers.</li>
</ul>
