<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Imagine you are a spider sitting in the center of a large web.<br><strong>Draw a picture of your web and the insects in it.</strong></li>

<li>One of your bugs flies away while you’re trying to eat it.<br><strong>What does it say to you?</strong></li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. Your Web and Insects</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> The web and insects you draw represent the kind of love partner you want in your life.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The size, shape, and details of your web mirror how you envision love and relationships, while the insects symbolize the qualities you seek in a partner.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. The Bug’s Words as It Escapes</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> The words the bug says reflect what you might say when your love confession is rejected.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This spontaneous response reveals how you naturally cope with disappointment in romance—whether with humor, acceptance, or hurt feelings.</li>
</ul>
