<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You bought a popular magazine. How do you read it?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Do you read from the first page to the last?</li>

<li>Do you go straight to the article you’re interested in only?</li>

<li>Do you flip randomly and read only articles worthwhile?</li>

<li>Do you read the features in the same order as you always do?</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You make your money worth
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Reading the magazine thoroughly shows you value your purchases and like to get the full experience for your money.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You burn a hole in your pocket
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Skipping straight to the articles you want reflects impulsive tendencies and a preference for instant gratification in spending.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You spend economically
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Flipping randomly and focusing only on worthwhile content shows you are selective and practical with your money.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Your spending habit is very strict
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Following the same reading order consistently indicates discipline and a controlled, structured approach to finances.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
