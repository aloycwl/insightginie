<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You come across a berry field in the countryside.</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>How high is the fence surrounding the field?</li>

<li>How many berries do you take?</li>

<li>You get caught — what does the farmer say to you?</li>

<li>After everything, how did the berries taste, and how do you feel about being a thief?</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. Fence height</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Indicates your cheating self-control level — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> A low fence shows weak resistance to temptation, while a high fence shows strong self-discipline.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. Number of berries taken</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Reflects how many lovers you may have at a single time — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Taking few berries suggests monogamy; taking many indicates tendency to pursue multiple romantic interests simultaneously.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. Farmer’s reaction</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Shows what excuse you will use if caught cheating — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your imagined response reveals your mindset when confronted with guilt or wrongdoing.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. Berry taste &amp; feeling about theft</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Indicates how you feel during an affair — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Enjoying the berries without guilt suggests indulgence; feeling guilty reflects self-awareness and conscience during romantic entanglements.</li>
</ul>
