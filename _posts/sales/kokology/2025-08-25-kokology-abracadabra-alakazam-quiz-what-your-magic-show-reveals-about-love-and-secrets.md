<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>



<p>You are a magician performing a magic show. The curtain is rising.</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>How do you feel before the show begins?</li>



<li>Whom do you call to assist you, a person you know?</li>



<li>You mismanage your trick, what do you say to your assistant?</li>



<li>Back in your dressing room, how do you feel?</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How you feel in an affair
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your emotions before the show reflect your excitement, anxiety, or guilt in a secretive or risky romantic situation.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Someone you think is naive
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The assistant you choose represents a person you perceive as innocent, unsuspecting, or easily influenced in real life.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> What you will say to your partner if caught cheating
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your reaction to a mismanaged trick shows how you would defend, excuse, or respond when confronted about mistakes in sensitive situations.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How you feel after the whole cheating ordeal
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your post-show feelings mirror your inner emotions once a risky affair or secret is concluded, whether relief, regret, or satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
