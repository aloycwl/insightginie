<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>



<p>You visit your friend who has a new job, but he is having some kind of trouble. What did he do wrong?</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Didn&#8217;t come to the table after repeated calling</li>



<li>Made a mistake in order taking</li>



<li>Spilled something on the customer&#8217;s clothes</li>



<li>Cleared the table before the customer finished</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. Didn&#8217;t come to the table after repeated calling</strong> – You lack ability to concentrate</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Overlooking or forgetting tasks in this scenario reflects difficulty focusing and maintaining attention in your own life.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. Made a mistake in order taking</strong> – Always being late or bringing uninvited friends</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Errors due to disorganization mirror a tendency to be inattentive to schedules or social expectations.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. Spilled something on the customer&#8217;s clothes</strong> – You are a generally serious person</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Accidents in this context reflect how seriously you approach responsibilities and how disruptions can affect your sense of control.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. Cleared the table before the customer finished</strong> – You are over eager</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Acting too quickly or anticipating outcomes shows your impatience and tendency to rush situations without waiting for proper timing.</li>
</ul>
