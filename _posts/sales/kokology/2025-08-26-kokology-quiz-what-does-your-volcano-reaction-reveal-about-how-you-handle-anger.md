<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You are watching an erupting volcano from a safe distance. What is your first thought?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>&#8220;Maybe this is nature&#8217;s way of warning us to stop destroying the earth&#8221;</strong></li>

<li><strong>&#8220;I guess the pressure just got too high inside there&#8221;</strong></li>

<li><strong>&#8220;Wow – what a sight! I&#8217;m impressed&#8221;</strong></li>

<li><strong>&#8220;It&#8217;s in the nature of these things to blow up. Big deal&#8221;</strong></li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. &#8220;Maybe this is nature&#8217;s way of warning us to stop destroying the earth&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You thought you are responsible for people&#8217;s anger.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You tend to personalize outbursts, believing that others’ frustrations may be connected to your own actions or words.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. &#8220;I guess the pressure just got too high inside there&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You often blame others when things go wrong.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Rather than reflecting inward, you assume external pressure or someone else’s fault is the cause of the problem.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. &#8220;Wow – what a sight! I&#8217;m impressed&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You accept criticism and outbursts in a positive way.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Instead of feeling attacked, you recognize emotional explosions as natural expressions and handle them with composure.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. &#8220;It&#8217;s in the nature of these things to blow up. Big deal&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You can say anything out and do not pay attention to it.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You’re unfazed by drama and quick tempers, often brushing them off as trivial rather than taking them to heart.</li>
</ul>
