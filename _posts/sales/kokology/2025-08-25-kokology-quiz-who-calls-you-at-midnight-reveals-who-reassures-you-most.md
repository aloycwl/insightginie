<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>You’re deep asleep when suddenly your phone rings in the middle of the night.<br>You answer.</p>

<p><strong>Who is the person on the line, and what do they say to you?</strong></p>

<p>Pause and think carefully before reading the interpretation below.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Answer:</strong> The person who calls you at midnight</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This person represents <strong>the one you rely on most for reassurance and comfort</strong> in times of uncertainty or need. Your subconscious is showing you who you instinctively turn to when you need emotional support.</li>
</ul>
