### **Quiz Question**

Take a piece of paper and draw the following:

1. A **circle** – make it any size you like.
2. As many **triangles** as you wish.
3. As many **squares** as you wish.
4. Place them wherever you want—some may even overlap.

What do your drawings reveal about your personality and life balance?
