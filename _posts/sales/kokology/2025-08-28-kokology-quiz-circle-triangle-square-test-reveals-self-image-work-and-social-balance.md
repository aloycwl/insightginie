<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>Take a piece of paper and draw the following:</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>A <strong>circle</strong> – make it any size you like.</li>

<li>As many <strong>triangles</strong> as you wish.</li>

<li>As many <strong>squares</strong> as you wish.</li>

<li>Place them wherever you want—some may even overlap.</li>
</ol>

<p>What do your drawings reveal about your personality and life balance?</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Circle</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Large circle = great sense of self. Small circle = seeing yourself as insignificant.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The circle represents your self-image and confidence. Its size reflects how you perceive your personal worth.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Triangles</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Large triangle = work feels important. Many triangles = too many commitments.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Triangles symbolize career and obligations. Their size and number reveal how much priority and stress you place on work.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Squares</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Large square = social pressure. Many squares = feeling lost in the crowd.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Squares represent society and community. The bigger or more numerous they are, the more you feel burdened or overshadowed by others.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Overlap of Shapes</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Overlapping shapes = harmony in your life.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> When self, work, and social connections blend smoothly, it shows you’ve found a balance between personal identity, career, and relationships.</li>
</ul>
