<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>You stroll through the zoo and stop by a monkey’s cage. As you look into its eyes, you feel a strange connection—almost like you’re communicating.</p>

<p>👉 What do you think the monkey is trying to tell you?</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Your answer reflects how you feel when you’re part of a group or company, but just can’t express openly.</strong></p>

<p><strong>1. &#8220;Let me out of here!&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You often feel trapped in social settings.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This response shows a sense of confinement, as if being around others limits your freedom to express yourself.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. &#8220;Don’t ignore me!&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You want to be noticed and valued.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This reveals a need for recognition and a dislike of being overlooked in group dynamics.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. &#8220;Stay away from me.&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You prefer keeping your distance.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This choice reflects discomfort in crowds and a tendency to create space when surrounded by people.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. &#8220;Come play with me.&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You crave connection and interaction.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This indicates a playful and friendly side, showing that you enjoy socializing but may not always know how to express it.</li>
</ul>
