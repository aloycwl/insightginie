<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You’re watching live wrestling. Suddenly, you’re given the chance to step into the ring. What type of wrestler would you be?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>A <strong>giant bruiser</strong> who dominates opponents with sheer strength.</li>

<li>A <strong>technical wizard</strong> who uses elaborate clinches and slams on every new opponent.</li>

<li>A <strong>villain</strong> who relies on illegal holds and dirty tricks.</li>

<li>An <strong>expressionless masked wrestler</strong> who never shows emotions.</li>
</ol>

<p>Pick your fighter. Now, let’s see what it says about your love life.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. The Giant Bruiser</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You like to control your partner.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your wrestling style of overpowering opponents translates into a dominant personality in intimacy, where you feel most comfortable leading.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. The Technical Wizard</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You enjoy new tricks and techniques with each new partner.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Just as you master new wrestling moves, you thrive on variety and novelty in relationships, constantly seeking fresh experiences.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. The Villain</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You can’t be satisfied in traditional love making.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your willingness to break rules in the ring mirrors your need to break conventions in intimacy, craving excitement beyond the ordinary.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. The Masked Wrestler</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You don’t reveal your real feelings when making love.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your hidden face reflects emotional walls—you protect your vulnerability by keeping true emotions veiled even in close connections.</li>
</ul>
