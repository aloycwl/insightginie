<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>You are entering an old abandoned building.</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>How many steps do you go down a staircase to the basement?</li>

<li>You hear a sound—what is it? Weeping? Speaking?</li>

<li>What is your reaction to that sound?</li>

<li>You hear someone calling your name from above the stairs—who is it?</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How deep your psychological scar
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The number of steps you descend reflects the depth of emotional or psychological wounds you carry.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How you got through a bad experience
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The type of sound you perceive indicates your coping mechanisms for past difficulties—whether you are sensitive, cautious, or resilient.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How you approach your darkness
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your reaction to the sound shows how you confront fear, stress, or inner challenges.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Who you can rely on during trouble
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The person you hear calling your name represents your support system—the people you trust in difficult times.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
