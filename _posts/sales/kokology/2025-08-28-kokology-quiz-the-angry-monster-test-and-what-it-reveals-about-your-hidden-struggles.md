<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>A monster is heading your way angrily. Why do you think it’s coming toward you?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>It’s hungry and searching for food</li>

<li>It’s searching for lost love</li>

<li>It’s despondent because it’s so ugly</li>

<li>It’s angry at the entire world</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. It’s hungry and searching for food</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You may have diet problems.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Seeing hunger as the cause reflects a subconscious concern with eating habits, diet, or physical well-being.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. It’s searching for lost love</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You have difficulty in love life.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> A monster chasing love symbolizes emotional struggles and difficulties in romantic relationships.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. It’s despondent because it’s so ugly</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are dissatisfied with your appearance.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Linking ugliness to the monster highlights insecurities about looks or self-image.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. It’s angry at the entire world</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You have a pessimistic outlook.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Blaming the world for the monster’s rage reflects an inner belief that external forces are unfair or stacked against you.</li>
</ul>
