<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>



<p>You’re a new artist about to release your first CD. What type of jacket design would you pick?</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>A <strong>soothing scene</strong> using calming imagery.</li>



<li>A <strong>fun, cartoonish design</strong> or playful image.</li>



<li>An <strong>abstract pattern</strong> that makes people think.</li>



<li>A <strong>picture of yourself</strong> on the cover.</li>
</ol>



<p>Pick your cover. Now, let’s uncover what it says about your personality.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. Soothing Scene</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are a caring and gentle person but have a wall around you.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The serene imagery reflects your compassionate nature, while your choice of scenery instead of yourself shows emotional protection.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. Fun Cartoonish Design</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are sociable and talkative but unreliable.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your playful cover mirrors your outgoing personality, yet the whimsical style hints at a lack of consistency or follow-through.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. Abstract Pattern</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You like to express your talent.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Abstract designs signal creativity and a desire to showcase your unique abilities and thoughts to the world.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. Picture of Yourself</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are straightforward and honest.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Placing yourself on the cover reflects transparency and confidence—you show the world exactly who you are.</li>
</ul>
