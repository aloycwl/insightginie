<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>You went fishing, and as the fish reels out of the water so fast, it suddenly hits you.<br><strong>Where on your body did the fish hit?</strong></p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Your chosen body part</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> That part of your body is what you most want to protect</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The instinctive spot you imagine being hit reveals your subconscious sense of vulnerability. It highlights areas—physical or emotional—that you safeguard the most.</li>
</ul>
