### **Quiz Question**

A bird flies into your house and changes colors day after day: blue, yellow, red, and black. What color do you think it will be next?

1. Stay black
2. Back to blue
3. White
4. Gold

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

1. **Answer:** Pessimistic outlook
   * **Reasoning:** Choosing black indicates a tendency to focus on negative outcomes or challenges. It reflects a cautious, sometimes skeptical, perspective when facing uncertainty.
2. **Answer:** Practical optimist
   * **Reasoning:** Choosing blue again shows resilience and hope. You approach life with optimism but remain realistic, balancing expectations with practical thinking.
3. **Answer:** Cool and decisive under pressure
   * **Reasoning:** Selecting white demonstrates calmness and clarity. You can make rational decisions even in stressful situations, keeping composure under pressure.
4. **Answer:** Super opportunist
   * **Reasoning:** Picking gold indicates a strategic, opportunistic mindset. You are drawn to possibilities and leverage situations for maximum gain.
