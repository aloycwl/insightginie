<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>



<p>A bird flies into your house and changes colors day after day: blue, yellow, red, and black. What color do you think it will be next?</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Stay black</li>



<li>Back to blue</li>



<li>White</li>



<li>Gold</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Pessimistic outlook
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing black indicates a tendency to focus on negative outcomes or challenges. It reflects a cautious, sometimes skeptical, perspective when facing uncertainty.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Practical optimist
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing blue again shows resilience and hope. You approach life with optimism but remain realistic, balancing expectations with practical thinking.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Cool and decisive under pressure
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Selecting white demonstrates calmness and clarity. You can make rational decisions even in stressful situations, keeping composure under pressure.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Super opportunist
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Picking gold indicates a strategic, opportunistic mindset. You are drawn to possibilities and leverage situations for maximum gain.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
