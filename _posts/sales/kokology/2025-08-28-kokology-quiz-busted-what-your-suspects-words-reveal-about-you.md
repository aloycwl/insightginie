<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>You are chasing down a suspect. You finally run him down, gun pointed at his face. What does the suspect say to you?</p>

<p>Pick one response from your imagination.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>Answer:</strong> The things you say when you get caught doing something bad</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The suspect’s reaction reflects how you handle accountability or guilt in real life. If the suspect is defensive, remorseful, or evasive, it mirrors your own tendencies when confronted with mistakes or wrongdoing.</li>
</ul>
