<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>If you were an actor playing in a courtroom drama, which character would you see yourself as?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>The Lawyer</strong></li>

<li><strong>The Detective</strong></li>

<li><strong>The Accused</strong></li>

<li><strong>The Witness</strong></li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. The Lawyer</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are hotheaded and unable to hold your cool.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Just like a lawyer passionately arguing a case, you have a fiery spirit and a tendency to let emotions drive your reactions.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. The Detective</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> People will turn to you for help.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The detective role reflects your natural problem-solving skills and reliability, making others trust you in times of need.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. The Accused</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You tend to second-guess and judge yourself.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Taking the place of the accused suggests self-doubt and a habit of internal criticism, even when you’re not at fault.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. The Witness</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You try to get along with everyone.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> As a witness, you stay on the sidelines, preferring harmony and avoiding unnecessary conflict with others.</li>
</ul>
