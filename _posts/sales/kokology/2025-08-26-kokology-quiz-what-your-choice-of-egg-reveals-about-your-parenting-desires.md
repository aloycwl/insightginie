<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>



<p>Imagine cracking open an egg on a table in front of you. The egg can be of any shape, size, color, or species.</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>What kind of egg do you choose?
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Snake</li>



<li>Turtle</li>



<li>Dinosaur</li>



<li>Chicken</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. Snake</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You want your children to have wisdom and wealth</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing a snake egg reflects a desire for your children to be clever, resourceful, and capable of navigating life with intelligence and success.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. Turtle</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You want your children to have health and longevity</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The turtle symbolizes longevity and steadiness, showing your focus on ensuring your children grow up healthy and live long lives.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. Dinosaur</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You want your children to be unique</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> A dinosaur egg indicates a wish for your children to stand out, be strong, and leave a distinctive mark in the world.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. Chicken</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You want your children to be normal</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing a chicken egg reflects a desire for a simple, happy, and stable life for your children without extraordinary expectations.</li>
</ul>
