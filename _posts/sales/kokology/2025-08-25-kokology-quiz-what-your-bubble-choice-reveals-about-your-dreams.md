<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>



<p>In a playground, you are playing with bubbles. What scene do you imagine?</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Bubble floating high into the sky</li>



<li>Hundreds of tiny bubbles blowing out</li>



<li>You are blowing an enormous bubble</li>



<li>The bubbles are carried behind you</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. Bubble floating high into the sky</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Your dreams are elusive and unattainable — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing a single bubble soaring reflects lofty aspirations that feel out of reach or difficult to grasp.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. Hundreds of tiny bubbles blowing out</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Your dreams are attainable — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Many small bubbles represent realistic and achievable goals that you can steadily work toward.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. You are blowing an enormous bubble</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You have a focused and important dream — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Concentrating on one large bubble shows that you dedicate your energy to one central, meaningful ambition.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. The bubbles are carried behind you</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You have been disappointed and hence you do not dream much — <strong>Reasoning:</strong> Bubbles trailing behind suggest past setbacks have made you cautious or hesitant to pursue new dreams.</li>
</ul>
