<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You are lost in a deep cavern when a bat flies up and whispers something in your ear. What does it say?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>&#8220;I know where the exit is&#8221;</strong></li>

<li><strong>&#8220;Let me show you the way out&#8221;</strong></li>

<li><strong>&#8220;Keep looking&#8221;</strong></li>

<li><strong>&#8220;You&#8217;re never, ever going to get out of here&#8221;</strong></li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. &#8220;I know where the exit is&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are a busybody and a know-it-all.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Always quick to assert what you know, you may unintentionally come across as someone who meddles in other people’s affairs.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. &#8220;Let me show you the way out&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You have a big heart and are selfless.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This response reflects compassion and a natural tendency to help others, even if it means putting them before yourself.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. &#8220;Keep looking&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You encourage people to learn themselves.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Instead of providing direct answers, you prefer motivating others to grow through their own experiences.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. &#8220;You&#8217;re never, ever going to get out of here&#8221;</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You jeer and kick at people who are already down.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This choice points to a more cynical or harsh side, where you may criticize or discourage those who are struggling.</li>
</ul>
