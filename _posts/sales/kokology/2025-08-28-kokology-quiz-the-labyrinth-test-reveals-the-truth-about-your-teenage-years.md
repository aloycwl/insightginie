<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>You went through a giant maze and finally exited. What is your feeling?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>That was too easy</li>

<li>It took a while but no big deal</li>

<li>I was lost, I thought I would never get out</li>

<li>Someone inside helped me to get out</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. That was too easy</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You have a carefree teenage life.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing ease reflects a smooth adolescence with little emotional turbulence.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. It took a while but no big deal</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You did not have an easy teenage life but it forged who you are today.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Acknowledging the struggle shows challenges shaped your resilience and character.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. I was lost, I thought I would never get out</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You had a rough teenage life that resulted in self-doubt.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Feeling trapped in the maze reflects emotional struggles and lingering uncertainty from adolescence.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. Someone inside helped me to get out</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You always had someone to turn to.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The helping hand symbolizes supportive relationships that guided you through difficult times.</li>
</ul>
