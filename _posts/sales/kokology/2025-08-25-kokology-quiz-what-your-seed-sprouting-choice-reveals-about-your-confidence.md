<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>



<p>You are a scientist and have discovered a new plant species.<br>You plant 100 seeds in a harsh, inhospitable desert.</p>



<p><strong>Question:</strong> Out of 100 seeds, how many do you think will sprout?</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. 99–100 seeds sprout</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You may have self-confidence issues. You expect perfection and fear failure, which can sometimes overwhelm your judgment.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. 81–98 seeds sprout</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You are aware of your abilities and confident. You trust your skills while realistically anticipating minor obstacles.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. 61–80 seeds sprout</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You are a cautious optimist. You hope for the best but plan for setbacks and stay prepared for challenges.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. 41–60 seeds sprout</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You are still searching. You are exploring your potential and learning how to balance hope and realism.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>5. 21–40 seeds sprout</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You tend to be pessimistic. You may underestimate yourself and expect difficulties more than opportunities.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>6. 1–20 seeds sprout</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You need to work on recognizing your value. Self-doubt may be limiting your sense of achievement.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>7. 0 seeds sprout</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You cannot tolerate mistakes, even minor ones. This perfectionist tendency can hinder your ability to take risks.</li>
</ul>
