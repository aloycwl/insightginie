<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>What kind of dream car do you envision yourself driving?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>High-performance vehicle</li>

<li>Sleek and beautiful chassis</li>

<li>Status-symbol ride with price tag to match</li>

<li>Anything as long as it runs</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. High-performance vehicle</strong> – You appreciate good points of your partner</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing speed and performance reflects valuing qualities and abilities beyond surface-level appearances in your relationships.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. Sleek and beautiful chassis</strong> – You look at the physical appearance of your partner</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Prioritizing aesthetics in your car mirrors how you are drawn to outward beauty and presentation in your personal connections.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. Status-symbol ride with price tag to match</strong> – Social position and material success are crucial to you</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Selecting an expensive, prestigious car shows your focus on social status, wealth, and achievements, both in yourself and others.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. Anything as long as it runs</strong> – You are happy with anything that meets your minimum standard</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Practicality and contentment guide your choices, emphasizing function and sufficiency over luxury or appearances.</li>
</ul>
