<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>Imagine yourself as a seal in a zoo, performing tricks for the visitors.<br>As the show goes on, what runs through your mind while waiting for the show to end and the next bucket of fish from your trainer?</p>

<p>Choose your answer carefully—your response reveals your approach to work.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. Excited for the next bucket of fish</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You are highly motivated by tangible rewards at work. Recognition and bonuses drive your performance, and you thrive when incentives are clear.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. Thinking about the next trick you’ll perform</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You are proactive and ambitious. You focus on opportunities to shine and consistently look for ways to improve your skills.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. Feeling bored and waiting for the show to end</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You may approach work with minimal enthusiasm. Routine tasks bore you, and you tend to do only what is required.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. Observing the audience and trainer closely</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> You are strategic and attentive. You evaluate your environment carefully, learning from others to maximize your success.</li>
</ul>
