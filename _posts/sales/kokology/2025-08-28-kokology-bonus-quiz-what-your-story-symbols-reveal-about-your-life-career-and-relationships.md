### **Quiz Questions**

Imagine you are creating a story with the following elements: a human, a bridge, water, a rabbit, a key, and a rope.

1. Who is the human in your story, and what are they doing?
2. Describe the bridge—its condition, size, and surroundings.
3. What does the water look like, and how does it flow?
4. What role does the rabbit play in your story?
5. How is the key used, and what does it unlock?
6. How do you use the rope in your story?
