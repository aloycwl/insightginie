<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>



<p>Imagine you are creating a story with the following elements: a human, a bridge, water, a rabbit, a key, and a rope.</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Who is the human in your story, and what are they doing?</li>



<li>Describe the bridge—its condition, size, and surroundings.</li>



<li>What does the water look like, and how does it flow?</li>



<li>What role does the rabbit play in your story?</li>



<li>How is the key used, and what does it unlock?</li>



<li>How do you use the rope in your story?</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. The human</strong> – Reflects your own energy and personality</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The human in your story represents your inner vitality, confidence, and approach to life’s challenges.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. The bridge</strong> – Represents your current career stage</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The type and condition of the bridge indicate your progress, stability, and feelings about your professional journey.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. The water</strong> – Represents your wealth</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Calm, flowing water suggests financial ease, while turbulent water reflects instability or financial concerns.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. The rabbit</strong> – How you value your partner</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The rabbit symbolizes affection, attention, and the care you place in your relationships.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>5. The key</strong> – Your success factor</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> How the key is used or what it unlocks reflects the skills, mindset, or opportunities that will lead to your success.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>6. The rope</strong> – How you communicate</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The rope represents your communication style: whether it is supportive, flexible, or tense in relationships and interactions.</li>
</ul>
