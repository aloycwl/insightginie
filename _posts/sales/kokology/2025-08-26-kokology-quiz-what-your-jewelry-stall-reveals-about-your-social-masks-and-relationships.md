<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>



<p>Imagine you’re at a lively fair filled with exquisite boutiques and merchandise. You decide to open your own stall for handmade jewelry.</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>How many different types of accessories and bangles do you put on display?</li>



<li>Why do you think someone will walk over and leave without buying anything?</li>



<li>What type of salesmanship will you use to close a potential customer?</li>



<li>How would you describe your sales when the shop closes?</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. How many different types of accessories and bangles do you put on display?</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How many social masks you will put on.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The number of accessories reflects how many roles or personas you use when dealing with different people in life.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. Why do you think someone will walk over and leave without buying anything?</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How you feel after a heartbreak or rejection.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Your interpretation of a customer’s departure mirrors how you rationalize or cope with emotional rejection in real life.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. What type of salesmanship will you use to close a potential customer?</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How you express your strong points to others.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The way you persuade and present your work reflects the manner in which you showcase your strengths in social and personal settings.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. How would you describe your sales when the shop closes?</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Your social achievement.</li>



<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The way you summarize your performance mirrors how you measure success and accomplishments in your social life.</li>
</ul>
