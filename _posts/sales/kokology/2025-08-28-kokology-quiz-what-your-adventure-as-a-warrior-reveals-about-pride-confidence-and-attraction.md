<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Questions</strong></h3>

<p>You are a warrior chosen to accompany a great hero on a quest to recover a stolen treasure.</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>What does the hero say to you as you prepare to embark on your quest?</li>

<li>The king gives you a sword—describe it in detail.</li>

<li>You travel far, facing many obstacles and crises. How many enemies do you encounter?</li>

<li>You finally arrive at your destination. What expression do you see on your hero’s face?</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. What the hero says to you before the journey</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Your pride weak spot.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The words you imagine reflect where your pride is most vulnerable and sensitive.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. The sword given by the king</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> How you value your self-pride.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The sword’s design, strength, and quality mirror the way you perceive and protect your dignity.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. The number of enemies you encountered</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> The greater the number, the more confidence you have in your abilities.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Enemies represent challenges, and counting them shows how much faith you place in your own strength.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. The hero’s expression at the end</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> What you find most attractive in the opposite sex.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The hero’s face reflects the qualities you unconsciously admire and seek in others.</li>
</ul>
