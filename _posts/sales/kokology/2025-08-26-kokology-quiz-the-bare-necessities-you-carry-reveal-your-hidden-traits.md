### **Quiz Question**

You are leaving the house and have taken all the bare necessities. Which one extra item will you bring along with you?

1. Personal organizer
2. Hairspray or mousse
3. Lucky charm
4. Candy or gum

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. Personal organizer**

* **Answer:** You cannot trust your memory — **Reasoning:** Choosing an organizer shows you rely on external structure and written reminders to stay on track.

**2. Hairspray or mousse**

* **Answer:** Appearance is all-important to you — **Reasoning:** Prioritizing styling products reflects a deep concern with image, presentation, and how others perceive you.

**3. Lucky charm**

* **Answer:** You believe in bad luck — **Reasoning:** Carrying a charm reveals a tendency to depend on superstition or external forces rather than pure logic.

**4. Candy or gum**

* **Answer:** You have diet problems — **Reasoning:** Reaching for sweets or snacks indicates impulsive eating habits or struggles with self-control around food.
