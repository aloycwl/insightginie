### **Quiz Questions**

You are a member of a theater group.

1. What type of play do you want to play in, what is your big scene? Explain in detail.
2. What words does your main rival have for you on being chosen?
3. A director looks dissatisfied with your performance, why?
4. The crowds leave after the play, what do you say to the empty stage?

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

**1. What type of play do you want to play in, what is your big scene?** – This reflects your future

* **Reasoning:** Your choice of role and scene reveals your aspirations, ambitions, and the path you envision for your life.

**2. What words does your main rival have for you on being chosen?** – How your future self will speak to you

* **Reasoning:** Your rival's reaction mirrors your internal dialogue and guidance you may receive from your future self about challenges or achievements.

**3. A director looks dissatisfied with your performance, why?** – The areas you will make mistakes in the future

* **Reasoning:** Your interpretation of the director's dissatisfaction shows the mistakes or growth opportunities you are likely to face on your life journey.

**4. The crowds leave after the play, what do you say to the empty stage?** – What you will say at the end of life

* **Reasoning:** Your final words reflect your legacy, personal reflection, and how you perceive your accomplishments once all external validation is gone.
