### **Quiz Question**

Which Cinderella scene stands out the most to you?

1. Cinderella suffered at the hands of her wicked stepmother
2. Cinderella transformed into a princess by her fairy godmother
3. Cinderella loses her slipper when the clock strikes twelve
4. The prince finds Cinderella and fits the glass slipper on her foot

---

### **Answer Guide & Reasoning**

1. **Answer:** Tendency to look down at people
   * **Reasoning:** Focusing on Cinderella's suffering may indicate a habit of judging others or being critical, noticing flaws more than potential.
2. **Answer:** Impracticality
   * **Reasoning:** Being drawn to the magical transformation reflects a tendency to dream big, sometimes overlooking reality or practical limits.
3. **Answer:** Highly dependent on others
   * **Reasoning:** Noticing the lost slipper shows sensitivity to reliance on others or attachment to external help for success.
4. **Answer:** Too average
   * **Reasoning:** Highlighting the prince fitting the slipper suggests an emphasis on conventional or expected outcomes rather than uniqueness.
