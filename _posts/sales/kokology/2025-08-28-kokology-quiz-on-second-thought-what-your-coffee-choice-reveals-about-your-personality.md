<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>



<p>You ordered a sandwich and a cup of coffee without thinking, but after she leaves you suddenly have the mood for hot chocolate. What would you do?</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Keep looking through the menu thinking how good the taste of hot cocoa</li>



<li>Look around to see if the waitress will come back</li>



<li>Get up and find the waitress so you can change the order</li>



<li>Give up and just wait for the coffee</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<p><strong>1. Keep looking through the menu thinking how good the taste of hot cocoa</strong> – You don&#8217;t know how to call it quits</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Lingering indecision shows a tendency to overthink and difficulty moving on from what’s already chosen.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>2. Look around to see if the waitress will come back</strong> – You are not thrilled about the breakup and worry about what others might think</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Waiting for a second chance demonstrates hesitation and concern about external opinions, reflecting cautious or anxious tendencies.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>3. Get up and find the waitress so you can change the order</strong> – You will be shocked in breakup and will try to distract yourself</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Immediate action shows you respond actively to surprises or disruptions, using activity as a coping mechanism.</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>4. Give up and just wait for the coffee</strong> – You get over breakup easily</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Accepting the situation without fuss reflects adaptability and an ability to move on without dwelling.</li>
</ul>
