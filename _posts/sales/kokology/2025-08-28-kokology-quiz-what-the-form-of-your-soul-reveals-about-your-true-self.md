<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>

<p>Imagine your soul survives after death.<br>👉 What form do you think it would take?</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Same size and shape of your body</li>

<li>Bigger in size</li>

<li>Become tiny like a fairy</li>

<li>Become a flame or cloud, without definite form</li>
</ol>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>

<p><strong>1. Same size and shape of your body</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You accept yourself for who you are.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> Choosing this form reflects a sense of inner peace and contentment with your identity.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>2. Bigger in size</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You still have many goals left to achieve.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This shows ambition, a desire for growth, and the belief that your potential extends far beyond your current self.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>3. Become tiny like a fairy</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You are great, but haven’t yet discovered your best qualities.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This choice suggests hidden strengths and a self-image that underestimates your true potential.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>4. Become a flame or cloud, without definite form</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> You don’t concern yourself with weaknesses or strengths.</li>

<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> This reflects a free spirit, unconstrained by limitations or labels, flowing with life as it comes.</li>
</ul>
