<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Quiz Question</strong></h3>



<p>Who sends you a birthday card that you never expected?<br>Who gives you the biggest birthday present?</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Answer Guide &amp; Reasoning</strong></h3>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Someone you worship will send you a card
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The unexpected card represents people you admire or hold in high regard. It shows who genuinely acknowledges and values you, even if you didn’t anticipate it.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Answer:</strong> Someone with the biggest present is the one you often take for granted
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Reasoning:</strong> The person giving the largest gift symbolizes overlooked support in your life. It reflects those whose presence, efforts, or care you may not fully appreciate.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
