The Future of Sales Forecasting: Why AI Isn't Enough on Its Own
===============================================================

In the modern business landscape, data is often heralded as the new oil. Companies are racing to implement sophisticated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems and AI-driven forecasting tools to gain a competitive edge. However, the assumption that AI can replace human intuition in sales strategy is a dangerous misconception. To achieve accurate forecasting and effective sales management, businesses must embrace a synergistic approach: the power of machine learning augmented by human nuance.
