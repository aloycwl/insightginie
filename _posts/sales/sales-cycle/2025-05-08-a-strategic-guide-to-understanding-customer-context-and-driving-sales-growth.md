In today's highly competitive sales environment, traditional selling is no longer enough. Success now hinges on **strategic lead and opportunity coaching**—a structured approach to guiding sales professionals in identifying, nurturing, and converting high-potential prospects. By focusing on a prospect's **business challenges, buying context, and decision-making influences**, sales leaders can dramatically improve win rates and pipeline quality. This article explores the key components of effective lead and opportunity coaching, including customer context, opportunity objectives, influence mapping, situation analysis, and action planning.
