In today's highly competitive B2B environment, effective **account management** has evolved beyond transactional selling to become a strategic driver of long-term growth and profitability. It's no longer just about closing deals—**it's about maximizing value from existing accounts through meticulous planning, relationship building, and proactive sales strategy**. Whether you're managing a few high-value clients or a broad portfolio, success hinges on structured account planning, focused sales activities, and ongoing retention initiatives. This article delves into the critical components of account management—**from account planning sessions and sheets to expansion and retention strategies**—equipping your team with actionable insights to **increase customer lifetime value (CLV)** and drive sustained success.
