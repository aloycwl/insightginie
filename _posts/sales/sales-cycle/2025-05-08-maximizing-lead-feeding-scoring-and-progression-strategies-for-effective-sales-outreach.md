In today's competitive sales environment, lead generation and effective sales outreach are crucial to a business's success. However, generating leads is only part of the equation. The next critical steps involve **lead feeding**, **scoring**, and **progression**. By understanding how to properly nurture leads and advance them through the sales pipeline, companies can increase their chances of converting potential customers into loyal clients.

In this article, we'll dive deep into how **lead feeding** works, the importance of **lead scoring** and **progression**, why it takes an average of **8 touches to get an initial meeting**, and how finding **common ground** is key to breaking the ice and moving prospects forward. These strategies ensure that your sales team is equipped to handle leads effectively and maximize conversion opportunities.
