In **Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP)**, language is more than communication—it's a map of our reality. One of the key ways our mind constructs this map is through a process called **generalisation**. While generalisation helps us learn and make sense of the world, it can also lead to **limiting beliefs and mental blocks** if left unchecked.

Understanding and identifying generalisations is essential for personal growth, effective coaching, and transformational communication. In this article, we'll explore what generalisation means in NLP, how it affects thought and behavior, and how you can challenge and transform it using proven NLP tools.
