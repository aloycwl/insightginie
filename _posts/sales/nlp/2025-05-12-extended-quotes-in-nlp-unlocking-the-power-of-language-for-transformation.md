In **Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP)**, **Extended Quotes** are a powerful language technique used to influence perception, reframe experiences, and create lasting change. This NLP strategy involves quoting someone or something in a way that extends the meaning of the original statement, allowing the practitioner to influence the listener's subconscious mind subtly and effectively. By understanding how to use **Extended Quotes**, NLP practitioners can craft powerful interventions that shift limiting beliefs, promote positive actions, and foster personal transformation.

In this article, we will dive deep into what **Extended Quotes** are in the context of NLP, how they work, their applications, and how you can incorporate them into your own practice to promote profound change.
