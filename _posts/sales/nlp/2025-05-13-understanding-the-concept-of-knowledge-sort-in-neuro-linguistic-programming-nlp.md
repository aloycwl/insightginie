In the realm of **Neuro-Linguistic Programming** (NLP), numerous techniques are employed to improve communication, cognition, and behavior. One such technique, **Knowledge Sort**, stands as a pivotal tool to analyze and reorganize how individuals process and categorize information. This NLP model helps in understanding how people perceive, prioritize, and make sense of their experiences and the world around them.

In this article, we'll explore the **Knowledge Sort** concept in NLP in detail, examining its definition, benefits, applications, and the broader impact it can have on personal and professional growth.
