Self-confidence is a cornerstone of personal and professional success, affecting how we interact with others, approach challenges, and achieve our goals. In **Neuro-Linguistic Programming** (NLP), self-confidence is seen as a powerful tool that shapes our perception, communication, and behavior. By understanding the differences between **high self-confidence** and **low self-confidence**, individuals can use NLP techniques to enhance their confidence, overcome limiting beliefs, and improve their personal growth.

In this article, we will explore the concepts of **high** and **low self-confidence** from an NLP perspective, how they influence behavior, and how NLP techniques can be used to increase self-confidence in both personal and professional settings.
