In **Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP)**, understanding how language shapes our thoughts and behaviors is a powerful tool for personal growth, communication, and coaching. One of the core concepts in NLP that helps us examine how we express beliefs is the term **Lost Performative**. This term refers to language patterns that imply judgments or assessments without specifying who is making them.

The **Lost Performative** is a distortion of reality, and understanding how it works can significantly improve how we communicate and challenge limiting beliefs. In this article, we will explore what a Lost Performative is, how it affects our perceptions, and how to identify and address it in both our personal lives and coaching practices.
