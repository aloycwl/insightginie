The Overview Effect: How Seeing Earth from Space Changes Everything
===================================================================

Imagine floating in the profound silence of space, gazing back at our home planet. What would you see? Not countries or borders, but a vibrant, swirling marble of blues, greens, and whites, suspended in the infinite blackness. This isn't just a beautiful sight; for many who have experienced it, it's a profoundly transformative cognitive shift known as the **Overview Effect**.

Coined by author Frank White in 1987, the Overview Effect describes the cognitive shift in awareness reported by some astronauts during spaceflight, often while viewing the Earth from orbit. It's a feeling of awe, a profound understanding of the interconnectedness of all life on Earth, and a powerful sense of the planet's fragility and preciousness in the vast cosmos. It transcends the individual, fostering a deep sense of global unity and environmental urgency that reshapes one's perspective on life itself.
