The Art of Letting Go: Why Justification Is the Enemy of Self-Respect
=====================================================================

As we navigate the complexities of life, particularly as we enter our middle years and beyond, we often reach a profound realization: we are exhausted. For decades, many of us have carried an invisible burden—the need to explain, defend, and justify our existence, our choices, and our boundaries to people who have no intention of listening. Psychology tells us that one of the most significant acts of self-respect in later life is the deliberate abandonment of the need to be understood by those who have already made up their minds.
