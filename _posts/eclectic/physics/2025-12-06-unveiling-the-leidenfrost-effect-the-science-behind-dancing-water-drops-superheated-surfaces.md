Unveiling the Leidenfrost Effect: The Science Behind 'Dancing' Water Drops & Superheated Surfaces

Have you ever seen a droplet of water skittering across a scorching hot pan, seemingly defying gravity and refusing to boil away instantly? Far more than a kitchen curiosity, this principle of physics plays a critical role in everything from industrial cooling to the design of advanced engines. Join us as we dive deep into the fascinating world of superheated surfaces and the insulating power of vapor.
